# Timeline of the Addermarch Campaign

- [Duncan of Haldrenn](<../../people/addermarians/duncan-of-haldrenn.md>): Oct 1714 DR to Nov 1714 DR: Duncan meets Geoffrey/the Stranger in the Highmoor above Haldrenn while Duncan is still moving with the grazing flocks. Geoffrey is the one following Avatus/serpentine leads; Duncan knows local stories about the old hill-fort. **DM source:** Serpentine Weapon Backstory.
- [Duncan of Haldrenn](<../../people/addermarians/duncan-of-haldrenn.md>): Nov 1714 DR to Feb 1715 DR: Duncan winters in Haldrenn and talks more seriously than usual about hidden treasure. **Session source:** [Addermarch - Session 15](<addermarch-session-15.md>).
- [Duncan of Haldrenn](<../../people/addermarians/duncan-of-haldrenn.md>): Mar 1715 DR: Duncan leaves Haldrenn to seek the treasure. Villagers later assume he died in the late April storm. **Session source:** [Addermarch - Session 15](<addermarch-session-15.md>).
- [Piri](<../../people/addermarians/piri.md>): Mar 09, 1715 DR: Killed by the unnamed solider of [Torvaine Watch](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/torvaine-watch.md>) who found the [Serpentine Dagger](<../../things/magic-items/serpentine-dagger.md>) while scouting.
- [Duncan of Haldrenn](<../../people/addermarians/duncan-of-haldrenn.md>): Mar 30, 1715 DR to Apr 10, 1715 DR: Duncan and Geoffrey reach the Serpentine redoubt beneath Serethwyn Tor and begin exploring/excavating. **DM source:** Serpentine Weapon Backstory.
- [Bartoz](<../../people/addermarians/bartoz.md>): Apr 01, 1715 DR: Mortally wounded [Sir Einrik](<../../people/addermarians/einrik-arpaad.md>) while defending [Torvaine Watch](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/torvaine-watch.md>), and fell in the smithy, only to rise again as an undead.
- [Duncan of Haldrenn](<../../people/addermarians/duncan-of-haldrenn.md>): Apr 1715 DR: Duncan and Geoffrey find Avatus-era treasure and the Serpentine Battleaxe. Duncan takes the axe and begins falling under its influence. **DM source:** Serpentine Weapon Backstory.
- [Einrik Arpaad](<../../people/addermarians/einrik-arpaad.md>): Apr 01, 1715 DR: Succumbed to the Serpentine Dagger, murdered the garrison, and was slain by [Bartoz](<../../people/addermarians/bartoz.md>).
- [Hildo Brask](<../../people/addermarians/hildo-brask.md>): Apr 01, 1715 DR: Killed by the corrupted Sir Einrik while defending Torvaine Watch.
- [Duncan of Haldrenn](<../../people/addermarians/duncan-of-haldrenn.md>): Apr 15, 1715 DR to May 02, 1715 DR: Duncan remains near Serethwyn Tor with Geoffrey while the axe tightens its hold. **DM source:** Serpentine Weapon Backstory.
- [Addermarch - Session 01](<addermarch-session-01.md>): **Apr 25, 1715 DR, dawn:** In **[Valcroix](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/valcroix.md>)**, [Drou](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/drou.md>), [Fazoth de Brune](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/fazoth-de-brune.md>), and [Yvan Greenrabbit](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/yvan-greenrabbit.md>) agree to escort merchant [Oona Stonebridge](<../../people/halflings/oona-stonebridge.md>) to [Roscombe](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/roscombe.md>); the small caravan sets out under clear skies along the road.
- [Addermarch - Session 01](<addermarch-session-01.md>): **Apr 25, 1715 DR:** Travel is slow but peaceful through orchards, fields, and hamlets as the company heads north-west toward the forested hills.
- [Addermarch - Session 01](<addermarch-session-01.md>): **Apr 26, 1715 DR:** An uneventful march sees the road leave the river valley and begin climbing into the shadowed reaches of the [Torvaine Forest](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/torvaine-forest.md>).
- [Addermarch - Session 01](<addermarch-session-01.md>): **Apr 27, 1715 DR, morning:** The party clears a wind-fallen tree from the road; a sunning adder spooks the horses, throwing [Oona](<../../people/halflings/oona-stonebridge.md>), but she is quickly healed and the team presses on.
- [Addermarch - Session 01](<addermarch-session-01.md>): **Apr 27, 1715 DR, late afternoon:** Only a few miles from [Torvaine Watch](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/torvaine-watch.md>) the caravan is ambushed by two burrowing mohlers; the creatures are slain, yet their presence so near the garrison alarms the travelers, who camp for the night.
- [Addermarch - Session 01](<addermarch-session-01.md>): **Apr 28, 1715 DR, morning:** On the final approach the group discovers a peddler’s corpse drained of blood and apparently fleeing the fort.
- [Addermarch - Session 01](<addermarch-session-01.md>): **Apr 28, 1715 DR, early afternoon:** Arriving at [Torvaine Watch](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/torvaine-watch.md>) they find the portcullis locked and silent, encounter a ghostly knight who warns of death, and fight a mess-hall full of zombies. A key taken from the undead opens the gathering hall; more zombies in the barracks are destroyed, loot is gathered, and in Marshal [Hildo Brask](<../../people/addermarians/hildo-brask.md>)’s quarters the party uncovers a bloody trail and the marshal’s troubling letter.
- [Addermarch - Session 02](<addermarch-session-02.md>): **Apr 28, 1715 DR, early afternoon:** Crossing the hoarding to the eastern tower, the party fends off stirges lurking in the rafters, then follows a blood trail downstairs and ransacks an armory whose locked chest resists them. In the smithy the heroes slay the undead smith **[Bartoz](<../../people/addermarians/bartoz.md>)**, discover how the twin winches work, and coordinate across the bridge to raise [Torvaine Watch](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/torvaine-watch.md>)’s main gates.
- [Addermarch - Session 02](<addermarch-session-02.md>): **Apr 28, 1715 DR, late afternoon:** Venturing to the commander’s quarters they find Marshal **[Hildo Brask](<../../people/addermarians/hildo-brask.md>)**’s corpse, confront the wrathful ghost of Sir **[Einrik Arpaad](<../../people/addermarians/einrik-arpaad.md>)**, seize a cursed [Serpentine Dagger](<../../things/magic-items/serpentine-dagger.md>), and retreat as debris hurls after them.
- [Addermarch - Session 02](<addermarch-session-02.md>): **Apr 28, 1715 DR, evening:** After recovering Brask’s body and a treasure chest, the group quits [Torvaine Watch](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/torvaine-watch.md>) and camps beside the road with **[Oona Stonebridge](<../../people/halflings/oona-stonebridge.md>)** and her wagon.
- [Bartoz](<../../people/addermarians/bartoz.md>): Apr 28, 1715 DR: Undead remains defeated by [Fazoth de Brune](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/fazoth-de-brune.md>), [Drou](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/drou.md>), and [Yvan Greenrabbit](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/yvan-greenrabbit.md>) during the watch’s liberation.
- [Einrik Arpaad](<../../people/addermarians/einrik-arpaad.md>): Apr 28, 1715 DR: Confronted the [Addermarch Mercenaries](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/addermarch-mercenaries.md>) as a vengeful ghost.
- [Hildo Brask](<../../people/addermarians/hildo-brask.md>): Apr 28, 1715 DR: [Body recovered](<addermarch-session-02.md>) and honored by the [Addermarch Mercenaries](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/addermarch-mercenaries.md>)before leaving the fort.
- [Addermarch - Session 02](<addermarch-session-02.md>): **Apr 29, 1715 DR:** The caravan travels without incident along the uneven forest road through the [Torvaine Forest](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/torvaine-forest.md>).
- [Colette du Bois](<../../people/addermarians/colette-du-bois.md>): Apr 29, 1715 DR: Captured by goblin bandits while scouting in the [Highmoor](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/highmoor.md>)
- [Addermarch - Session 02](<addermarch-session-02.md>): **Apr 30, 1715 DR, morning:** South of [Roscombe](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/roscombe.md>) the party meets Sir **[Gareth Howell](<../../people/addermarians/gareth-howell.md>)** and a troop sent by Lady **[Elowen Duval](<../../people/addermarians/elowen-duval.md>)** to investigate the silence at the watch; [Fazoth](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/fazoth-de-brune.md>)’s account of the curse persuades Gareth to let the dagger remain in his care.
- [Addermarch - Session 02](<addermarch-session-02.md>): **Apr 30, 1715 DR, evening:** The companions reach **[Roscombe](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/roscombe.md>)** and secure lodging.
- [Bartoz](<../../people/addermarians/bartoz.md>): Apr 30, 1715 DR: Lady [Elowen Duval](<../../people/addermarians/elowen-duval.md>) recounted Bartoz’s sacrifice, cementing his legacy in [Roscombe](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/roscombe.md>).
- [Elowen Duval](<../../people/addermarians/elowen-duval.md>): Apr 30, 1715 DR: [Sent Sir Gareth Howell and a troop of soldiers](<addermarch-session-02.md>) to investigate the silence at [Torvaine Watch](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/torvaine-watch.md>).
- [Gareth Howell](<../../people/addermarians/gareth-howell.md>): Apr 30, 1715 DR: Met the [Addermarch Mercenaries](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/addermarch-mercenaries.md>) south of [Roscombe](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/roscombe.md>) while marching to relieve [Torvaine Watch](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/torvaine-watch.md>).
- [Addermarch - Session 02](<addermarch-session-02.md>): **May 01, 1715 DR:** Fare-welling Oona, they report to Lady Duval, claim a bounty for recovered arms, and are asked to consult priest **[Owen Tavish](<../../people/addermarians/owen-tavish.md>)** of **[The Sibyl](<../../gods-and-religions/gods/incorporeal-gods/mos-numena-pantheon/the-sibyl.md>)** regarding the dagger; Duval also relates a rider’s full tale of the tomb-found blade and the massacre at [Torvaine Watch](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/torvaine-watch.md>).
- [Elowen Duval](<../../people/addermarians/elowen-duval.md>): May 01, 1715 DR: [Received the Addermarch Mercenaries' report](<addermarch-session-02.md>) on [Torvaine Watch](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/torvaine-watch.md>), paid them for recovered arms and treasure, and urged them to consult [Owen Tavish](<../../people/addermarians/owen-tavish.md>) about the [Serpentine Dagger](<../../things/magic-items/serpentine-dagger.md>).
- [Addermarch - Session 03](<addermarch-session-03.md>): **May 02, 1715 DR:** The party receives letters from priest **[Owen Tavish](<../../people/addermarians/owen-tavish.md>)** and Lady **[Elowen Duval](<../../people/addermarians/elowen-duval.md>)**. [Fazoth](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/fazoth-de-brune.md>) visits the Sunday market and sells ancient coins recovered from [Torvaine Watch](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/torvaine-watch.md>). Alchemist **[Caradoc](<../../people/addermarians/caradoc.md>)** arrives and hires them to slay a cockatrice and return with its gizzard as proof.
- [Caradoc](<../../people/addermarians/caradoc.md>): May 02, 1715 DR: [Hired the Addermarch Mercenaries](<addermarch-session-03.md>) to slay a cockatrice and procure its gizzard as proof of capability.
- [Elowen Duval](<../../people/addermarians/elowen-duval.md>): May 02, 1715 DR: [Wrote to the Addermarch Mercenaries](<handouts/letter-from-elowen-duval.md>) thanking them for helping make the road safe, funding their stay at [The Traveler's Rest](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/the-traveler-s-rest.md>), and inviting them to dinner at the manor.
- [Owen Tavish](<../../people/addermarians/owen-tavish.md>): May 02, 1715 DR: [Wrote to the Addermarch Mercenaries](<handouts/letter-from-owen-tavish.md>) while departing to help Sir [Gareth Howell](<../../people/addermarians/gareth-howell.md>) cleanse the haunting at [Torvaine Watch](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/torvaine-watch.md>), warning them to keep the [Serpentine Dagger](<../../things/magic-items/serpentine-dagger.md>) secret and safe.
- [Addermarch - Session 03](<addermarch-session-03.md>): **May 03, 1715 DR, morning:** The companions leave [Roscombe](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/roscombe.md>), cross open farmland and the rocky hills of the [Stonewolds](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/stonewolds.md>); near the **[Kilrath Standing Stones](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/kilrath-standing-stones.md>)** they locate the cockatrice cave.
- [Addermarch - Session 03](<addermarch-session-03.md>): **May 03, 1715 DR, midday:** Inside they discover a petrified dwarf, a strange map, and fend off a shadow-animated skeleton.In the main cavern they defeat an adult cockatrice and its hatchlings amid a dormant fey gate and a lifelike statue of a shepherd boy. A furious naiad binds [Fazoth](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/fazoth-de-brune.md>) and [Yvan](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/yvan-greenrabbit.md>) until they purge red algae tainting her pool; treasure—including Duval family jewels and an odd golden symbol of **[The Sibyl](<../../gods-and-religions/gods/incorporeal-gods/mos-numena-pantheon/the-sibyl.md>)**—is recovered and the fey gate is destroyed.
- [Addermarch - Session 03](<addermarch-session-03.md>): **May 03, 1715 DR, evening:** Laden with gizzard, eggs, and loot, the party camps on the road back toward [Roscombe](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/roscombe.md>).
- [Duncan of Haldrenn](<../../people/addermarians/duncan-of-haldrenn.md>): May 03, 1715 DR: Duncan and Geoffrey secretly return to Duncan’s cottage in Haldrenn; Duncan kills Geoffrey in the root cellar and burns the evidence. **DM source, consistent with session evidence:** Serpentine Weapon Backstory; [Addermarch - Session 15](<addermarch-session-15.md>).
- [Addermarch - Session 03](<addermarch-session-03.md>): **May 04, 1715 DR, morning:** A detour to **[Brantor](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/brantor.md>)** lets them deliver the gizzard to [Caradoc](<../../people/addermarians/caradoc.md>).
- [Addermarch - Session 03](<addermarch-session-03.md>): **May 04, 1715 DR, evening:** Returning to [Roscombe](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/roscombe.md>) they present the recovered jewels to Lady [Elowen Duval](<../../people/addermarians/elowen-duval.md>), claim the long-standing bounty on the thief who stole them, and settle again at [The Traveler's Rest](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/the-traveler-s-rest.md>).
- [Caradoc](<../../people/addermarians/caradoc.md>): May 04, 1715 DR: Received the cockatrice gizzard from the [Addermarch Mercenaries](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/addermarch-mercenaries.md>) at [Brantor](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/brantor.md>).
- [Duncan of Haldrenn](<../../people/addermarians/duncan-of-haldrenn.md>): May 04, 1715 DR: Duncan flees Haldrenn again with the axe, now seeking the “heart” from the old stories. **DM source:** Serpentine Weapon Backstory; Avatus Cursed Items Timeline.
- [Elowen Duval](<../../people/addermarians/elowen-duval.md>): May 04, 1715 DR: [Rewarded the Addermarch Mercenaries](<addermarch-session-03.md>) for returning Duval family jewels stolen by a bandit two years earlier.
- [Addermarch - Session 04](<addermarch-session-04.md>): **May 05, 1715 DR:** In [Roscombe](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/roscombe.md>), the party sells the vial of magical weapon-oil looted from [Torvaine Watch](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/torvaine-watch.md>) and purchases extra healing potions at the bustling market square.
- [Addermarch - Session 04](<addermarch-session-04.md>): **May 06, 1715 DR, morning:** At the Temple of the Sibyl in [Roscombe](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/roscombe.md>), the party confers with [Owen Tavish](<../../people/addermarians/owen-tavish.md>) about the [Serpentine Dagger](<../../things/magic-items/serpentine-dagger.md>), and sell him an ancient Sibylline holy symbol.
- [Addermarch - Session 04](<addermarch-session-04.md>): **May 06, 1715 DR, afternoon:** At [Caradoc](<../../people/addermarians/caradoc.md>)’s cottage near [Brantor](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/brantor.md>), the party commissions a dose of [Trueflame](<../../things/materials/trueflame.md>) to aid in destroying the [Serpentine Dagger](<../../things/magic-items/serpentine-dagger.md>), and accepts a contract to recover a stolen “glowing poison” recipe from local Goblins.
- [Caradoc](<../../people/addermarians/caradoc.md>): May 06, 1715 DR: [Agreed to craft Trueflame](<addermarch-session-04.md>) for the [Addermarch Mercenaries](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/addermarch-mercenaries.md>) and contracted them to recover a stolen glowing-poison recipe from goblin bandits.
- [Duncan of Haldrenn](<../../people/addermarians/duncan-of-haldrenn.md>): May 06, 1715 DR: Duncan reaches the Feywild/Singing Tree and cuts out the Singing Tree’s heart. **DM source, cleaned date:** Avatus Cursed Items Timeline.
- [Einrik Arpaad](<../../people/addermarians/einrik-arpaad.md>): May 06, 1715 DR: Spirit appeased and dispersed by [Owen Tavish](<../../people/addermarians/owen-tavish.md>).
- [Isolde of Roscombe](<../../people/addermarians/isolde-of-roscombe.md>): May 06, 1715 DR: Welcomed the [Addermarch Mercenaries](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/addermarch-mercenaries.md>) to the temple and ushered them to Owen to discuss the Serpentine Dagger.
- [Owen Tavish](<../../people/addermarians/owen-tavish.md>): May 06, 1715 DR: [Met with the Addermarch Mercenaries](<addermarch-session-04.md>) at the Temple of the Sibyl after returning from laying [Einrik Arpaad](<../../people/addermarians/einrik-arpaad.md>)'s ghost to rest at [Torvaine Watch](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/torvaine-watch.md>).
- [Owen Tavish](<../../people/addermarians/owen-tavish.md>): May 06, 1715 DR: Suggested [Trueflame](<../../things/materials/trueflame.md>) as a possible means of destroying the [Serpentine Dagger](<../../things/magic-items/serpentine-dagger.md>), bought an old Sibylline holy symbol recovered from the [Kilrath Caves](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/kilrath-caves.md>), and played chess with [Fazoth de Brune](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/fazoth-de-brune.md>).
- [Addermarch - Session 04](<addermarch-session-04.md>): **May 07, 1715 DR, evening:** Over dinner in Lady **[Elowen Duval](<../../people/addermarians/elowen-duval.md>)’s** hall—complete with [Drou](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/drou.md>)’s performance, [Yvan](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/yvan-greenrabbit.md>)’s live painting, and [Fazoth](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/fazoth-de-brune.md>)’s fireworks—they learn from Captain **[Gareth Howell](<../../people/addermarians/gareth-howell.md>)** of a goblin hideout and his missing squire; the party vows to investigate.
- [Duncan of Haldrenn](<../../people/addermarians/duncan-of-haldrenn.md>): May 07, 1715 DR: The corrupted song begins affecting animals around Haldrenn; Olay is killed by his maddened bull. **Session source/inference:** [Addermarch - Session 11](<addermarch-session-11.md>); [Olay](<../../people/addermarians/olay.md>).
- [Elowen Duval](<../../people/addermarians/elowen-duval.md>): May 07, 1715 DR: [Hosted a manor dinner](<addermarch-session-04.md>) for the [Addermarch Mercenaries](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/addermarch-mercenaries.md>), where [Drou](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/drou.md>) performed and Sir [Gareth Howell](<../../people/addermarians/gareth-howell.md>) discussed goblin activity and his missing squire.
- [Gareth Howell](<../../people/addermarians/gareth-howell.md>): May 07, 1715 DR: Shared intelligence with the [Addermarch Mercenaries](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/addermarch-mercenaries.md>) at Lady Duval’s dinner regarding a missing squire and goblin activity.
- [Olay](<../../people/addermarians/olay.md>): May 07, 1715 DR: Killed by his enraged bull during a period of strange songs reported near Haldrenn.
- [Addermarch - Session 04](<addermarch-session-04.md>): **May 08, 1715 DR, morning:** Trekking north into the hills they reach a cave entrance, trigger a collapsing-floor trap, and rout the first wave of goblin sentries with chromatic orbs, invisibility, and misty step.
- [Addermarch - Session 04](<addermarch-session-04.md>): **May 08, 1715 DR, afternoon:** Following tracks to a hidden trapdoor they descend, dodge another pit alarm, burst into the goblin living-quarters, and—after a brutal melee of cloud-of-daggers, fist-flurries, and arcane blasts—slay the bandits and seize the room.
- [Addermarch - Session 05](<addermarch-session-05.md>): **May 08, 1715 DR, late afternoon:** Masquerading as a goblin, [Yvan](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/yvan-greenrabbit.md>) infiltrates the guardroom, locates captive squire **[Colette du Bois](<../../people/addermarians/colette-du-bois.md>)**, and with [Drou](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/drou.md>) and [Fazoth](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/fazoth-de-brune.md>) swiftly cuts down the drunken jailers before freeing her. After bribing the lair’s giant skittering spiders with pantry beef, the party slips through a secret door, battles the goblin queen and her minions in the throne room, and secures both treasure and the stolen **glowing-poison recipe**.
- [Addermarch - Session 05](<addermarch-session-05.md>): **May 08, 1715 DR, evening:** The party returns to [Roscombe](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/roscombe.md>), stopping in **[Brantor](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/brantor.md>)** to exchange the recipe (plus gold) for [Caradoc](<../../people/addermarians/caradoc.md>)’s completed vial of **[Trueflame](<../../things/materials/trueflame.md>)**.
- [Caradoc](<../../people/addermarians/caradoc.md>): May 08, 1715 DR: [Gave the Addermarch Mercenaries Trueflame](<addermarch-session-05.md>) in exchange for the recovered glowing-poison recipe and payment.
- [Colette du Bois](<../../people/addermarians/colette-du-bois.md>): May 08, 1715 DR: Rescued from the goblin queen’s cells by the [Addermarch Mercenaries](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/addermarch-mercenaries.md>) during their assault on the [Highmoor](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/highmoor.md>) lair.

- [Addermarch - Session 05](<addermarch-session-05.md>): **May 09, 1715 DR, morning:** In the Temple of the Sibyl, [Owen Tavish](<../../people/addermarians/owen-tavish.md>) oversees as the heroes use [Trueflame](<../../things/materials/trueflame.md>) to finally destroy the cursed **[Serpentine Dagger](<../../things/magic-items/serpentine-dagger.md>)**.
- [Addermarch - Session 06](<addermarch-session-06.md>): **May 09, 1715 DR, afternoon:** A quiet market day in [Roscombe](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/roscombe.md>) ends with alchemist **[Caradoc](<../../people/addermarians/caradoc.md>)** proposing a new quest: descend a nearby deep-cave network and retrieve the rare living mineral [Uligium](<../../things/materials/uligium.md>)
