# Addermarch Campaign - Session 16

>[!info] Trail to Serethwyn Tor: in which old records point toward a cursed hill-fort
> *Featuring: [Yvan Greenrabbit](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/yvan-greenrabbit.md>), [Drou](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/drou.md>), [Fazoth de Brune](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/fazoth-de-brune.md>)*
> *In Taelgar: May 22, 1715 DR - May 24, 1715 DR*
> *On Earth: Sunday November 30, 2025*
> *[Roscombe](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/roscombe.md>) and environs*

Research in Roscombe and a visit to the ranger [Arvanko](<../../people/other-nonhumans/arvanko.md>) uncovers information about ancient sites from the wars with [Avatus](<../../people/historical-figures/avatus.md>) and points the party toward the ruined hill-fort of [Serethwyn Tor](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/serethwyn-tor.md>). 

## Timeline

- May 22, 1715 DR, morning: At [The Traveler's Rest](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/the-traveler-s-rest.md>) in [Roscombe](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/roscombe.md>), the party interviews [Colette du Bois](<../../people/addermarians/colette-du-bois.md>), innkeeper [Bertrand LeBlanc](<../../people/addermarians/bertrand-leblanc.md>), and chambermaid [Emma Cole](<../../people/addermarians/emma-cole.md>) about Geoffrey, the dead stranger tied to Duncan’s axe, then meets [Elowen Duval](<../../people/addermarians/elowen-duval.md>) for lunch and is directed to scholar [Ernault](<../../people/addermarians/ernault.md>) in the keep’s archives.
- May 22, 1715 DR, afternoon: Traveling to [Caradoc](<../../people/addermarians/caradoc.md>)’s cottage near [Brantor](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/brantor.md>), the party collects magic items, commissions more [Trueflame](<../../things/materials/trueflame.md>) to destroy the cursed battle axe, and departs for  [Arvanko](<../../people/other-nonhumans/arvanko.md>)'s homestead. 
- May 23, 1715 DR: Reaching [Arvanko](<../../people/other-nonhumans/arvanko.md>)’s hill-country homestead, the heroes earn his trust and learn the location of the ruined hill-fort of [Serethwyn Tor](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/serethwyn-tor.md>), then depart to return to Caradoc's, camping on the return journey. 
- May 24, 1715 DR: The party arrives back at [Caradoc](<../../people/addermarians/caradoc.md>)’s cottage by midday, retrieves their prepared Trueflame, and sets out along the road toward Serethwyn Tor.

## Narrative

### Saturday, May 22nd, 2 Robert I

The party wakes at [The Traveler's Rest](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/the-traveler-s-rest.md>) in [Roscombe](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/roscombe.md>) and begins the day by chasing the trail of Geoffrey, the stranger dead in Duncan's cottage and connected to the [Serpentine Battleaxe](<../../things/magic-items/serpentine-battleaxe.md>). After making a lunchtime appointment to meet [Elowen Duval](<../../people/addermarians/elowen-duval.md>), they stop at to speak with [Colette du Bois](<../../people/addermarians/colette-du-bois.md>), who remembers Geoffrey staying at the inn, having a brief audience with [Elowen Duval](<../../people/addermarians/elowen-duval.md>), and giving off a dubious, secretive air. 

Back at the inn, they speak with [Bertrand LeBlanc](<../../people/addermarians/bertrand-leblanc.md>), who recalls Geoffrey arriving from [Valcroix](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/valcroix.md>) in the fall. The man mostly kept to his room, eating alone and disappearing for days at a time into the [Highmoor](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/highmoor.md>). In March he departed in earnest, outfitted for a serious venture: ropes, ladders, and the gear of someone planning to climb or delve. [Emma Cole](<../../people/addermarians/emma-cole.md>), Bertrand’s niece and the inn’s chambermaid, shares that once, while tidying Geoffrey’s room, she glimpsed a map spread on his table, crammed with annotations in a strange language. One word she recognized—[Avatus](<../../people/historical-figures/avatus.md>)—and she recalls several spots circled, especially in the mountains north of the [Umber](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/rivers/wistel-enst-watershed/umber.md>).

At lunch with Lady [Elowen Duval](<../../people/addermarians/elowen-duval.md>), she tells the party she remembers Geoffrey as suspicious and insistent about accessing the founding records of [Carlinshire](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/carlinshire.md>). Her notes confirm that she allowed him to speak with [Ernault](<../../people/addermarians/ernault.md>) but refused to let him copy anything. After lunch, she sends the party on to Ernault with her blessing to read and copy as they wish.

Up in a cluttered study stacked with papers and tomes, [Ernault](<../../people/addermarians/ernault.md>) remembers Geoffrey well—rude, dismissive, and only interested in copying passages about [Avatus](<../../people/historical-figures/avatus.md>)-era history, not in Ernault's scholarly interpretations. Ernault admits he is no numismatist and cannot date the party’s coin, but he happily shares his knowledge of the [Dominion of Avatus](<../../history/historical-realms/dominion-of-avatus.md>) and the old frontier. Among the early records he notes an odd entry: [Serethwyn Tor](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/serethwyn-tor.md>), an old hill-fort, was destroyed and ordered “not to be disturbed” by the rangers of the time. If ancient things still lie buried anywhere, Ernault suggests, that would be a prime candidate. He also mentions [Arvanko](<../../people/other-nonhumans/arvanko.md>) the Stoneborn, a ranger active in the area who may know more.

Leaving the keep, the heroes head out of town toward [Caradoc](<../../people/addermarians/caradoc.md>)’s cottage. There they collect magic items from Caradoc's old adventuring days that he gifted them, and commission another batch of [Trueflame](<../../things/materials/trueflame.md>) to unmake the battle axe. Caradoc warns that Arvanko is prickly but trustworthy, and after stocking up on healing potions the party camps along the road leading into the hills.

### Sunday, May 23rd, 2 Robert I

Pushing their pace through the uplands, the party reaches [Arvanko](<../../people/other-nonhumans/arvanko.md>)’s homestead by mid‑afternoon. The tall Stoneborn ranger, with gray‑black mottled skin, is working on an immense stone monument carved with the names of rangers who have fallen in western Addermarch. Curt and wary, he greets the strangers with suspicion: why, he demands, do they want to know where old cursed weapons lie?

Yvan’s earnest explanation sways him, and Arvanko accepts that the party is trying to contain danger rather than unleash it. He points out [Serethwyn Tor](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/serethwyn-tor.md>) on the carved monument and on their map, noting that a ranger named [Cadan](<../../people/addermarians/cadan.md>) died there in battle long ago and that the rangers send someone past every year or two to ensure the site remains undisturbed. It has been a year since the last check, he admits, and another is overdue.

Arvanko also speaks of a registry of serpentine weapons, a set of records listing known cursed weapons and their fates. Locally, he believes it was lost, though a copy might still survive in [Adderfell](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/adderfell.md>) or [Sembara](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/sembara/sembara.md>). Before they depart, he gifts the party a low-range [Drankorian Message Stone](<../../things/magic-items/drankorian-message-stone.md>), and asks them to report in once they reach Serethwyn Tor. The party then heads back toward [Caradoc](<../../people/addermarians/caradoc.md>)’s cottage, camping on the cart track as night falls.

### Monday, May 24th, 2 Robert I

The next day, the party reaches [Caradoc](<../../people/addermarians/caradoc.md>)'s by midday, and pick up their commissioned dose of [Trueflame](<../../things/materials/trueflame.md>). They then turn northeast, heading into the Highmoor towards  [Serethwyn Tor](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/serethwyn-tor.md>), walking for the rest of the afternoon before camping for the night.
