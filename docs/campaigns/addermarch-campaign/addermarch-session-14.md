# Addermarch Campaign - Session 14

>[!info] The Singing Tree: in which the fey threat is temporarily halted
> *Featuring: [Yvan Greenrabbit](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/yvan-greenrabbit.md>), [Drou](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/drou.md>), [Fazoth de Brune](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/fazoth-de-brune.md>)*
> *In Taelgar: May 18, 1715 DR - May 19, 1715 DR*
> *On Earth: Sunday November 2, 2025*
> *[Highmoor](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/highmoor.md>) and the [Feywild](<../../cosmology/feywild.md>)*

The party defeats the Singing Tree, speaks to a rescued fey, and temporarily disables the [Nightsong Roots](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/nightsong-roots.md>) Feywild portal before returning to [Haldrenn](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/haldrenn.md>). 

## Timeline

- **May 18, 1715 DR, night**: Defeat the Singing Tree, rescue [Vaelithar](<../../people/fey/vaelithar.md>), depart the [Feywild](<../../cosmology/feywild.md>), disable the [Nightsong Roots](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/nightsong-roots.md>) portal, and camp halfway to [Haldrenn](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/haldrenn.md>)
- **May 19, 1715 DR, morning**: Travel the remaining distance back to [Haldrenn](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/haldrenn.md>)

## Cast of Characters

- **[Yvan](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/yvan-greenrabbit.md>)** — Halfling sorcerer whose fire and mobility ultimately bring down the Singing Tree.
- **[Drou](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/drou.md>)** — Warrior-musician whose daggers, resilience, and instrumental talents help disable the portal.
- **[Fazoth](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/fazoth-de-brune.md>)** — A warlock who battles the tree’s curse, delivering critical magical strikes.
- **[Vaelithar](<../../people/fey/vaelithar.md>)** — A fey from **[Twilight's Edge](<../../gazetteer/extraplanar/feywild/twilight-s-edge.md>)**, rescued from the roots; bearer of the tale of the stolen heart.
- **[Liraene](<../../people/fey/liraene.md>)** — Vaelithar’s fallen companion, recovered from the roots after the battle.
- **The Singing Tree** — A corrupted fey tree whose stolen heart twists its song into violence.
- **Aurenxis** — A transmuter and alchemist who once traveled widely through the Feywild; proposed contact for further answers.

## Places

- **[Twilight's Edge](<../../gazetteer/extraplanar/feywild/twilight-s-edge.md>)** — The fey realm where Vaelithar and Liraene hail from.
- **[Nightsong Roots](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/nightsong-roots.md>)** — A wind-chime portal between the Feywild and the mortal world, partially disabled by the party.
- **[Haldrenn](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/haldrenn.md>)** — The nearby village to which the party returns after closing the portal.

## Narrative

### Tuesday, May 18th, 2 Robert I

In the [Feywild](<../../cosmology/feywild.md>), [Yvan](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/yvan-greenrabbit.md>), [Drou](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/drou.md>), and [Fazoth](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/fazoth-de-brune.md>) creep towards a clearing in the forest, where a large tree sits, emitting a cursed song: the Singing Tree, already showing signs of serious damage from numerous scorch marks. Launching an attack, [Fazoth](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/fazoth-de-brune.md>) strikes with _Witch Bolt_ as the tree drags [Fazoth](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/fazoth-de-brune.md>) and [Yvan](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/yvan-greenrabbit.md>) close with its roots, grappling them. [Yvan](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/yvan-greenrabbit.md>) uses _Misty Step_ to escape; [Drou](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/drou.md>) charges in with her daggers but they have little effect on the hard bark of the tree. As the tree bludgeons the party with its branches, [Fazoth](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/fazoth-de-brune.md>) also escapes with _Misty Step_ and launches an _Eldritch Blast_. The song, however, grows louder, and buries into the minds of [Fazoth](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/fazoth-de-brune.md>) and [Drou](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/drou.md>), cursing and enraging them to attack indiscriminately. Despite [Yvan](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/yvan-greenrabbit.md>)'s _Scorching Ray_, the fire biting into the wood, the battle turns and nearly gets away from the party. [Drou](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/drou.md>), enraged by the curse, attacks [Fazoth](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/fazoth-de-brune.md>), and [Fazoth](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/fazoth-de-brune.md>) attacks [Drou](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/drou.md>), knocking her unconscious, as [Yvan](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/yvan-greenrabbit.md>) finally brings down the Singing Tree with a _Sorcerous Burst_. 

With the Singing Tree subdued, the party searches the area. [Yvan](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/yvan-greenrabbit.md>) notices that two mounds of roots and earth seem to be covering people; cutting through one (ruining [Drou](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/drou.md>)'s dagger in the process) reveals [Vaelithar](<../../people/fey/vaelithar.md>), a fae from [Twilight's Edge](<../../gazetteer/extraplanar/feywild/twilight-s-edge.md>), still alive. He tells a story: him and his companion, [Liraene](<../../people/fey/liraene.md>), came across the tree recently, and fought it, nearly winning but were trapped in the end. While they were trapped, they felt the tree's memories. A man from the [mundane world](<../../cosmology/material-plane.md>), with an impossibly sharp battle axe, a glowing green gem attached, attacked and attacked, cutting the tree open and stealing its heart. Without a heart, its song turned to anger and violence. 

[Vaelithar](<../../people/fey/vaelithar.md>) says the heart must be returned to stop the song -- the tree cannot be killed permanently, it will always regrow. Indeed, the party sees a small shoot has already sprouted from the soil around the fallen tree, which quickly decayed to dirt in the minutes after the battle. He does not know more, but says the party should speak to Aurenxis a transmuter and alchemist who used to travel in the [Feywild](<../../cosmology/feywild.md>) a lot.

After speaking with [Vaelithar](<../../people/fey/vaelithar.md>), the party digs up the other mound. The woman inside has passed away; [Vaelithar](<../../people/fey/vaelithar.md>) takes a pendant from her neck to bring back to [Twilight's Edge](<../../gazetteer/extraplanar/feywild/twilight-s-edge.md>), but offers an enchanted fey pact wand and a mundane but pretty ring to the party. 

The party leaves the [Feywild](<../../cosmology/feywild.md>), and decides to try to close the [Nightsong Roots](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/nightsong-roots.md>) portal. After some experimenting, they manage to combine [Yvan](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/yvan-greenrabbit.md>)'s magical talents and [Drou](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/drou.md>)'s musical talents: [Yvan](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/yvan-greenrabbit.md>) freezes the wind chime roots of the portal, so they play a discordant tune, and [Drou](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/drou.md>) plays the original correct tune to open the portal. The discordance causes each chime in turn to fracture and crack. After cracking four of the seven chimes, the party decides that is enough, and heads back towards [Haldrenn](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/haldrenn.md>), camping about half way. 

### Wednesday, May 19th, 2 Robert I

Our session ends after an uneventful morning of travel, as the party arrives back in [Haldrenn](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/haldrenn.md>) just before lunch.
