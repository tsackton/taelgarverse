# Addermarch Campaign - Session 20

>[!info] The Heart Beneath Serethwyn Tor: in which the axe is unmade
> *Featuring: [Yvan Greenrabbit](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/yvan-greenrabbit.md>), [Drou](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/drou.md>), [Fazoth de Brune](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/fazoth-de-brune.md>)*
> *In Taelgar: May 27, 1715 DR - May 28, 1715 DR*
> *On Earth: Sunday March 8, 2026*
> *[Serethwyn Tor](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/serethwyn-tor.md>) and the mine below*

The party defeats the serpentine warlock beneath [Serethwyn Tor](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/serethwyn-tor.md>), recovers [Duncan of Haldrenn](<../../people/addermarians/duncan-of-haldrenn.md>)'s letters and the stolen fey heart, uses [Trueflame](<../../things/materials/trueflame.md>) to destroy the [Serpentine Battleaxe](<../../things/magic-items/serpentine-battleaxe.md>), learns more of the [Avatus wars](<../../events/1300s/serpentine-wars.md>) from a dead ranger, and sets out for [Caradoc](<../../people/addermarians/caradoc.md>)'s to seek a way back into the [Feywild](<../../cosmology/feywild.md>).

## Timeline

- May 27, 1715 DR, **late afternoon**: The party defeats the serpentine warlock in the deepest chamber beneath Serethwyn Tor.
- May 27, 1715 DR, **evening**: They search the chamber, recover Duncan's letters and the stolen fey heart, and use [Trueflame](<../../things/materials/trueflame.md>) to destroy the [Serpentine Battleaxe](<../../things/magic-items/serpentine-battleaxe.md>) and a serpentine pendant.
- May 27, 1715 DR, **night**: Climbing back out through the mine, they question a dead ranger with _Speak with Dead_, learning more about the [war with Avatus](<../../events/1300s/serpentine-wars.md>) and the lost [Serpentine Register](<../../things/books/serpentine-register.md>), then camp in the upper mine chamber.
- May 28, 1715 DR, **morning**: The party sets out for [Caradoc](<../../people/addermarians/caradoc.md>)'s, hoping to find a way back into the [Feywild](<../../cosmology/feywild.md>) to return the stolen heart.

- **[Feywild](<../../cosmology/feywild.md>)** - The destination to which the party hopes to return the stolen heart.

## Narrative

### Thursday, May 27th, 2 Robert I

Pressing onward from the lower tunnels, the party enters the deepest chamber beneath [Serethwyn Tor](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/serethwyn-tor.md>) and confronts the serpentine warlock. The enemy is dangerous, armed with the cursed [Serpentine Battleaxe](<../../things/magic-items/serpentine-battleaxe.md>), but the fight turns quickly. [Fazoth](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/fazoth-de-brune.md>) unleashes an astonishing series of attacks - _Witch Bolt_, eldritch blasts, and strikes from his fey ally - while [Drou](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/drou.md>) smashes the foe with thunder and hand-to-hand blows, and [Yvan](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/yvan-greenrabbit.md>) adds his own arcane force. The warlock falls before he can fully bring his strength to bear.

With the chamber secured, the party takes stock of what lies below. [Duncan of Haldrenn](<../../people/addermarians/duncan-of-haldrenn.md>)'s decapitated body lies there among other remains. Nearby are the [Serpentine Battleaxe](<../../things/magic-items/serpentine-battleaxe.md>), a [serpentine](<../../things/materials/serpentine.md>) pendant worn by the warlock, scattered coins, a wand the warlock carried, and walls veined with serpentine that had been scraped into a pile. On a small altar rests the stolen fey heart, ringed with serpentine dust and corruption.

The party uses [Trueflame](<../../things/materials/trueflame.md>) to destroy the axe and the pendant, though they do not have enough to burn away the dust as well. They recover the heart and then read the letters Duncan carried. Addressed to [Kelvyn](<../../people/addermarians/kelvyn.md>) in [Adderfell](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/adderfell.md>), they trace Duncan's increasingly rambling journey and reveal one crucial fact: some unknown patron had been paying [Geoffrey](<../../people/other-humans/geoffrey.md>) to search for [serpentine](<../../things/materials/serpentine.md>).

The way out proves awkward without the rope lost earlier, but with careful climbing, a bit of leaping across the rubble, and Fazoth's _Misty Step_, the party makes its way back up the shaft and into the upper mines. There they question one of the dead rangers with _Speak with Dead_. The ranger confirms more of the old history: the [wars in these hills](<../../events/1300s/serpentine-wars.md>) were fought over [serpentine](<../../things/materials/serpentine.md>), [Avatus](<../../people/historical-figures/avatus.md>) had a base in the region, and the Rangers once kept a [Serpentine Register](<../../things/books/serpentine-register.md>) recording the cursed weapons and their fates.

That night the party camps again in the upper mine chamber where they had previously rested, reporting to [Arvanko](<../../people/other-nonhumans/arvanko.md>) by [Drankorian Message Stone](<../../things/magic-items/drankorian-message-stone.md>) about the events here.

### Friday, May 28th, 2 Robert I

In the morning, carrying the recovered heart, the party sets out for [Caradoc](<../../people/addermarians/caradoc.md>)'s cottage to seek a path back into the [Feywild](<../../cosmology/feywild.md>) and return what Duncan stole.
