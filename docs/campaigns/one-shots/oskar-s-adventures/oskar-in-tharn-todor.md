# Oskar in Tharn Todor

After [Riswynn](<../../../people/pcs/dunmar-fellowship/riswynn.md>) returned to [Tharn Todor](<../../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/realms/nardith/tharn-todor.md>) with the [Shield of the Brawnanvil Clan](<../../dunmari-frontier-campaign/treasure/shield-of-the-brawnanvil-clan.md>), recovered from [Raven's Hold](<../../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/hara-basin/raven-s-hold.md>), she met a dwarven wanderer, [Oskar](<../../../people/pcs/other-pcs/oskar-s-companions/oskar.md>), who had recently returned from a long journey to the north. These are the stories of their adventures in the spring of DR 1748.

*Meta: this was three family one-shots played in spring and early summer in 2021.*

## Episode 1: The Sleeping Aboleth

Riswynn meets Oskar and Agnor at the [The Red Shield](<../../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/realms/nardith/the-red-shield.md>), a tavern owned by [Oskar](<../../../people/pcs/other-pcs/oskar-s-companions/oskar.md>)'s family in [Tharn Todor](<../../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/realms/nardith/tharn-todor.md>), to discuss news from [Bailon](<../../../people/dwarves/bailon.md>), the forewoman of one of the deep mines beneath the city. She reports that several of her dwarves are trapped, and she is hoping that [Oskar](<../../../people/pcs/other-pcs/oskar-s-companions/oskar.md>) and his companions will investigate. Descending deep below the city, [Oskar](<../../../people/pcs/other-pcs/oskar-s-companions/oskar.md>) and his companions find a strange chamber, where they defeat a haunted slime creature, discover the ancient remains of the aboleth [Xeron](<../../../people/other-nonhumans/xeron.md>), now content to slumber undisturbed, and rescue most of the lost dwarves.

- May 10, 1748 DR: [Riswynn](<../../../people/pcs/dunmar-fellowship/riswynn.md>), [Oskar](<../../../people/pcs/other-pcs/oskar-s-companions/oskar.md>), and [Agnor](<../../../people/pcs/other-pcs/oskar-s-companions/agnor.md>) defeat a haunted slime monster in mines beneath Tharn Todor and discover the ancient remains of the aboleth [Xeron](<../../../people/other-nonhumans/xeron.md>).
- May 11, 1748 DR: [Riswynn](<../../../people/pcs/dunmar-fellowship/riswynn.md>), [Oskar](<../../../people/pcs/other-pcs/oskar-s-companions/oskar.md>), and [Agnor](<../../../people/pcs/other-pcs/oskar-s-companions/agnor.md>) return to [Tharn Todor](<../../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/realms/nardith/tharn-todor.md>)

## Episode 2: The Celestial Meteor

[Ken-to](<../../../people/pcs/other-pcs/oskar-s-companions/ken-to.md>), a stoneborn mystic living in the [Yuvanti Mountains](<../../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/yuvanti-mountains.md>), brings news of a strange meteor that crashed in the high peaks to [The Red Shield](<../../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/realms/nardith/the-red-shield.md>). He tells [Riswynn](<../../../people/pcs/dunmar-fellowship/riswynn.md>) and [Oskar](<../../../people/pcs/other-pcs/oskar-s-companions/oskar.md>) about a cloud of black smoke and ash that now hangs over the mountain, and dreams of a fire spreading from the mountain and consuming the land below, as a red hand reaching up from cracks in the ground. Worse, a few days ago a wounded creature staggered down the mountain, warning of a ritual to summon a servant of an ancient evil from beyond this world.

The next morning, [Riswynn](<../../../people/pcs/dunmar-fellowship/riswynn.md>) and [Oskar](<../../../people/pcs/other-pcs/oskar-s-companions/oskar.md>) set out for the mountains, heading to [Ken-to](<../../../people/pcs/other-pcs/oskar-s-companions/ken-to.md>)'s hut. They speak with the wounded creature, and then head into the mountains where they fight and defeat a devil, attempting to complete a summoning ritual. 

- Apr 24, 1748 DR: A strange meteor crashes in [Yuvanti Mountains](<../../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/yuvanti-mountains.md>)
- May 14, 1748 DR: A strange, almost angelic humanoid staggers down the mountain and speaks to [Ken-to](<../../../people/pcs/other-pcs/oskar-s-companions/ken-to.md>)
- May 18, 1748 DR: [Ken-to](<../../../people/pcs/other-pcs/oskar-s-companions/ken-to.md>) arrives in [Tharn Todor](<../../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/realms/nardith/tharn-todor.md>), gives [Riswynn](<../../../people/pcs/dunmar-fellowship/riswynn.md>) and [Oskar](<../../../people/pcs/other-pcs/oskar-s-companions/oskar.md>) news of the strange meteor and wounded [angelic creature](<../../../creatures/extraplanar/deva.md>), and asks for help.
- May 19, 1748 DR: [Riswynn](<../../../people/pcs/dunmar-fellowship/riswynn.md>), [Ken-to](<../../../people/pcs/other-pcs/oskar-s-companions/ken-to.md>), and [Oskar](<../../../people/pcs/other-pcs/oskar-s-companions/oskar.md>) travel to [Ken-to](<../../../people/pcs/other-pcs/oskar-s-companions/ken-to.md>)'s hut to investigate situation, and defeat a devil who is trying to summon more fiends.

## Episode 3: The Cultist's Cave

Several merchant families in [Tharn Todor](<../../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/realms/nardith/tharn-todor.md>) ask [Riswynn](<../../../people/pcs/dunmar-fellowship/riswynn.md>) and [Oskar](<../../../people/pcs/other-pcs/oskar-s-companions/oskar.md>) to investigate bandit attacks on the road to [Nayahar](<../../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/realms/dunmar/western-dunmar/nayahar.md>). They depart the city, traveling along the road, and encounter a group of bandits engaged in combat with [Ghemdorn](<../../../people/pcs/other-pcs/oskar-s-companions/ghemdorn.md>), a satyr paladin, and [Eva](<../../../people/pcs/other-pcs/oskar-s-companions/eva.md>), an elven druid. After the bandits are defeated, the group speaks with [Jenny Honeypot](<../../../people/halflings/jenny-honeypot.md>), a halfling merchant who lost several of her crew, and her husband, [Mica Honeypot](<../../../people/halflings/mica-honeypot.md>), to the bandit attack. [Riswynn](<../../../people/pcs/dunmar-fellowship/riswynn.md>), [Oskar](<../../../people/pcs/other-pcs/oskar-s-companions/oskar.md>), [Ghemdorn](<../../../people/pcs/other-pcs/oskar-s-companions/ghemdorn.md>), and [Eva](<../../../people/pcs/other-pcs/oskar-s-companions/eva.md>) agree to investigate, and find a group of bandits in a cave who are under the dark influence of a strange aberration which they call That Which Lurks Beneath the Tide. The group defeats the bandits and rescues [Mica Honeypot](<../../../people/halflings/mica-honeypot.md>) and the halfling crew. Oskar and Riswynn return to [Tharn Todor](<../../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/realms/nardith/tharn-todor.md>) after parting ways with [Ghemdorn](<../../../people/pcs/other-pcs/oskar-s-companions/ghemdorn.md>) and [Eva](<../../../people/pcs/other-pcs/oskar-s-companions/eva.md>).
