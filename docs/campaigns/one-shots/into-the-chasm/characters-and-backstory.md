The party consists of

Eolo, a brash young kenku from the [Far North](<../../../gazetteer/faraway-places/far-north.md>). His village was destroyed by a white dragon when he was young, and he spent much of his childhood raised by Stoneborn in the [Sentinels](<../../../gazetteer/sentinel-range.md>). Recently he has decided to learn (or teach?) the kenku at the Kemeko Monastery how to fight. 

Ekko, a kenku. He grew up in the Kemeko Monastery, but as a young adult discovered a mystic connection to the Chasm. He saved one of the Vėjo Vaikai from a fall, and came to live with them in their small community around the headwaters of the [Teft](<../../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/rivers/teft-watershed/teft.md>).

[Finnan Oakstride](<../../../people/pcs/into-the-chasm/finnan-oakstride.md>), a halfling lore-gatherer, interested in ancient tales, wintering in the small village of Dravnieki

Justas Rhostrin, an itinerant priest, tender of the [Kestavo](<../../../gods-and-religions/religions/northern-folk-religions/kestavo.md>), also wintering in the small village of Dravnieki.

Jrain Fanlish, a traveler, messanger, and sometimes member of the Mezhai.
