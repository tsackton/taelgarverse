![Shatterstorm](/taelgarverse/assets/shatterstorm.webp){align="right"; width="400"}Shatterstorm is a magical dwarven warhammer, enchanted by the Brawnanvils before the [Great War](<../../../events/1500s/great-war.md>), but lost when the dwarves fled [Raven's Hold](<../../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/hara-basin/raven-s-hold.md>) in the chaos of the [Great War](<../../../events/1500s/great-war.md>). 


Recovered by [Riswynn](<../../../people/pcs/dunmar-fellowship/riswynn.md>) and the [Dunmar Fellowship](<../../../people/pcs/dunmar-fellowship/dunmar-fellowship.md>) from the [dwarven outpost](<../../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/hara-basin/dwarven-outpost-raven-s-hold.md>) near Raven's Hold in DR 1748, and now wielded by [Riswynn](<../../../people/pcs/dunmar-fellowship/riswynn.md>).
