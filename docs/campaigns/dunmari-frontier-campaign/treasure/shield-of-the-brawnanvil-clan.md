# The Shield of the Brawnanvil Clan

![Brawnanvil Shield](/taelgarverse/assets/brawnanvil-shield.webp){align="right"; width="350"}The Shield of the Brawnanvil Clan is an elaborate decorative shield of polished steel, worked with scenes from dwarven life. It is a Brawnanvil family heirloom, not a practical battle shield, whose magic draws a dwarf holding it into a recreation of a story from the Brawnanvil past; a Brawnanvil can guide the vision toward a particular memory or place.

The shield was lost in the [dwarven outpost near Raven's hold](<../../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/hara-basin/dwarven-outpost-raven-s-hold.md>) when the Brawnanvils and other dwarves fled during the [Great War](<../../../events/1500s/great-war.md>). [Riswynn](<../../../people/pcs/dunmar-fellowship/riswynn.md>) recovered it in DR 1748 and returned it to the Brawnanvils in [Tharn Todor](<../../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/realms/nardith/tharn-todor.md>).

## Chronology of the Shield

- (1544 DR): The Brawnanvils flee the [Dwarven Outpost (Raven's Hold)](<../../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/hara-basin/dwarven-outpost-raven-s-hold.md>) during the upheavals around the [Great War](<../../../events/1500s/great-war.md>), leaving the shield sealed in the Brawnanvil vault. 
- Apr 05, 1748 DR: [Riswynn](<../../../people/pcs/dunmar-fellowship/riswynn.md>) meets the [Dunmar Fellowship](<../../../people/pcs/dunmar-fellowship/dunmar-fellowship.md>) in [Karawa](<../../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/realms/dunmar/eastern-dunmar/karawa.md>) while seeking companions to help recover the shield from [Raven's Hold](<../../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/hara-basin/raven-s-hold.md>).
- Apr 12, 1748 DR: [Riswynn](<../../../people/pcs/dunmar-fellowship/riswynn.md>) recovers the [Shield of the Brawnanvil Clan](<shield-of-the-brawnanvil-clan.md>) from the sealed Brawnanvil vault in the [Dwarven Outpost (Raven's Hold)](<../../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/hara-basin/dwarven-outpost-raven-s-hold.md>).
- Apr 13, 1748 DR: [Riswynn](<../../../people/pcs/dunmar-fellowship/riswynn.md>) shows the shield to a damaged dwarven stone construct guarding the outpost forge and mines; the construct recognizes or accepts its authority and lets the party pass.
- Apr 16, 1748 DR: [Riswynn](<../../../people/pcs/dunmar-fellowship/riswynn.md>) parts ways with the [Dunmar Fellowship](<../../../people/pcs/dunmar-fellowship/dunmar-fellowship.md>) after [Stormcaller Tower](<../../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/hara-basin/stormcaller-tower.md>) and heads home to [Tharn Todor](<../../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/realms/nardith/tharn-todor.md>) with the Brawnanvil clan artifacts.
- May 05, 1748 DR: [Riswynn](<../../../people/pcs/dunmar-fellowship/riswynn.md>) arrives in [Tharn Todor](<../../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/realms/nardith/tharn-todor.md>) with the shield, and returns it to the clan elders.
