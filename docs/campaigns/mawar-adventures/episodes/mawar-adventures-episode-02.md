# Mawar Adventures Episode 02

!!! info "The Tragedy of Azar: in which a grieving killer is unmasked"
	*Featuring: [Ryu](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/ryu.md>), [Wazir](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/wazir.md>), [Trok](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/trok.md>), [Kaleho](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/kaleho.md>)*
	*In Taelgar: Aug 03, 1747 DR to Aug 09, 1747 DR*
	*On Earth: Thursday, August 19, 2021*
	*[Hamri](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/hamri.md>) and the [North Cliffs](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/north-cliffs.md>)*

Clues from [the Ciphered Scroll](<../notes/the-ciphered-scroll.md>) and the people of [Hamri](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/hamri.md>) lead [Ryu](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/ryu.md>), [Trok](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/trok.md>), [Wazir](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/wazir.md>), and [Kaleho](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/kaleho.md>) to [Azar the Lost](<../../../people/mawarans/azar-the-lost.md>), whose [Blood Knife](<../../../things/magic-items/blood-knife.md>) murders end after a dawn fight at Shark's Dock.

## Timeline

- Aug 03, 1747 DR, evening: [Wazir](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/wazir.md>) presents the decoded [The Ciphered Scroll](<../notes/the-ciphered-scroll.md>) to [Ryu](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/ryu.md>), [Trok](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/trok.md>), and [Kaleho](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/kaleho.md>) at the [Leviathan Inn](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/leviathan-inn.md>) in [Hamri](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/hamri.md>).
- Aug 04, 1747 DR: The party searches Airion's tower in the [North Cliffs](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/north-cliffs.md>), discovering older stonework beneath the ruined watchtower, then traces its recent occupant through Hamri's informants, uncovering evidence that [Azar the Lost](<../../../people/mawarans/azar-the-lost.md>) was living in hiding, likely tied to deaths among [gatza](<../../../things/materials/gatza.md>) addicts in the Drowned Flats, and seeking to purchase a lead-lined box from the smith [Ovina](<../../../people/dwarves/ovina.md>) on Shark's Dock.
- Aug 05, 1747 DR: The party confronts Azar on Shark's Dock, defeating him in a tense fight after he refuses to parley. After the fight, the party stabilizes Azar and contains the dockside chaos, hears [Azar's full tale](<../notes/azar-s-tale.md>), and clears his lair in the [Splinters](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/splinters.md>).
- Aug 06, 1747 DR: [Wazir](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/wazir.md>) identifies the recovered knife and brass bowl.
- Aug 09, 1747 DR: [Azar the Lost](<../../../people/mawarans/azar-the-lost.md>) leaves [Hamri](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/hamri.md>) with [Benat](<../../../people/lizardfolk/benat.md>) for the [lizardfolk](<../../../creatures/species/lizardfolk.md>) village.

### The Dawn Confrontation

<div class="taelgar-session-zoom__beat" data-session-zoom-beat data-session-zoom-key="mawar-adventures-session-2" data-zoom="short">
  <div class="taelgar-session-zoom__level" data-zoom-level="short"><p>At dawn, Azar collects the box, admits <a href="/taelgarverse/people/extraplanar-powers/gazankoa/">Gazankoa</a> is helping him save his family, and tries to lure <a href="/taelgarverse/people/pcs/mawar/ryu/">Ryu</a> onto the phantom boat.</p></div>
  <div class="taelgar-session-zoom__level" data-zoom-level="intermediate"><p>At dawn, <a href="/taelgarverse/people/pcs/mawar/ryu/">Ryu</a> spots the phantom boat approaching and the party takes positions around Shark&#x27;s Dock. Azar collects the finished box from <a href="/taelgarverse/people/dwarves/ovina/">Ovina</a>, <a href="/taelgarverse/people/pcs/mawar/ryu/">Ryu</a> blocks him with questions, <a href="/taelgarverse/people/pcs/mawar/wazir/">Wazir</a> uses <a href="/taelgarverse/people/mawarans/nadine/">Nadine</a>&#x27;s name to shake him, and Azar admits his name and his faith that <a href="/taelgarverse/people/extraplanar-powers/gazankoa/">Gazankoa</a> can bring back the lost dead.</p></div>

Azar had lost <a href="/taelgarverse/people/mawarans/nadine/">Nadine</a>, become hooked on <a href="/taelgarverse/things/materials/gatza/">gatza</a>, and heard <a href="/taelgarverse/people/extraplanar-powers/gazankoa/">Gazankoa</a> speak through the <a href="/taelgarverse/things/magic-items/blood-knife/">Blood Knife</a>. He believed that killing people in the right way, with their blood replaced by water, would restore his family. That evening, the party searches the <a href="/taelgarverse/gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/splinters/">Splinters</a>, quickly destroys two more drowned zombies, and finds <a href="/taelgarverse/people/mawarans/sefa/">Sefa</a> dead in the lair. On Aug 6, <a href="/taelgarverse/people/pcs/mawar/wazir/">Wazir</a> identifies the <a href="/taelgarverse/things/magic-items/blood-knife/">Blood Knife</a>, now locked in the lead-lined box, as a mystery beyond his magic, and the <a href="/taelgarverse/campaigns/mawar-adventures/items-and-treasure/airion-s-bowl-of-commanding-water-elementals/">brass bowl</a> as a bowl of commanding water elementals that must be filled with fresh ocean water and used immediately. A few days later, on Aug 9, Azar leaves south with <a href="/taelgarverse/people/lizardfolk/benat/">Benat</a> to heal before returning to make amends to <a href="/taelgarverse/gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/hamri/">Hamri</a>.</p></div>
