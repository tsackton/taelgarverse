# Mawar Adventures Episode 2

>[!info] The Tragedy of Azar: in which a grieving killer is unmasked
> *Featuring: [Ryu](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/ryu.md>), [Wazir](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/wazir.md>), [Trok](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/trok.md>), [Kaleho](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/kaleho.md>)*

> *On Earth: Thursday, August 19, 2021*
> *[Hamri](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/hamri.md>) and the [North Cliffs](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/north-cliffs.md>)*

Clues from [the Ciphered Scroll](<../notes/the-ciphered-scroll.md>) and the people of [Hamri](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/hamri.md>) lead [Ryu](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/ryu.md>), [Trok](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/trok.md>), [Wazir](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/wazir.md>), and [Kaleho](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/kaleho.md>) to [Azar the Lost](<../../../people/mawarans/azar-the-lost.md>), whose [Blood Knife](<../../../things/magic-items/blood-knife.md>) murders end after a dawn fight at Shark's Dock.

# Timeline

- Aug 03, 1747 DR, evening: [Wazir](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/wazir.md>) presents the decoded [The Ciphered Scroll](<../notes/the-ciphered-scroll.md>) to [Ryu](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/ryu.md>), [Trok](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/trok.md>), and [Kaleho](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/kaleho.md>) at the [Leviathan Inn](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/leviathan-inn.md>) in [Hamri](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/hamri.md>).
- Aug 04, 1747 DR: The party searches Airion's tower in the [North Cliffs](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/north-cliffs.md>), discovering older stonework beneath the ruined watchtower, then traces its recent occupant through Hamri's informants, uncovering evidence that [Azar the Lost](<../../../people/mawarans/azar-the-lost.md>) was living in hiding, likely tied to deaths among [gatza](<../../../things/materials/gatza.md>) addicts in the Drowned Flats, and seeking to purchase a lead-lined box from the smith [Ovina](<../../../people/dwarves/ovina.md>) on Shark's Dock.

After the fight, the party stabilizes Azar and contains the dockside chaos, hears [Azar's full tale](<../notes/azar-s-tale.md>), and clears his lair in the [Splinters](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/splinters.md>).
- Aug 06, 1747 DR: [Wazir](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/wazir.md>) identifies the recovered knife and brass bowl.
- Aug 09, 1747 DR: [Azar the Lost](<../../../people/mawarans/azar-the-lost.md>) leaves [Hamri](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/hamri.md>) with [Benat](<../../../people/lizardfolk/benat.md>) for the [lizardfolk](<../../../creatures/species/lizardfolk.md>) village.

## Narrative

At the Leviathan, [Wazir](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/wazir.md>) finally lays out what he has learned from [The Ciphered Scroll](<../notes/the-ciphered-scroll.md>). Its author, later identified as Azar, sought to restore his dead wife [Nadine](<../../../people/mawarans/nadine.md>). To do so, he experimented on vulnerable people whose bodies filled with water, and eventually sought clues from the  legends of [Airion](<../../../people/other-humans/airion.md>) the Mistspeaker, leading to summoning a [marid](<../../../creatures/extraplanar/marid.md>), [Nimessa](<../../../people/other-nonhumans/nimessa.md>), and making sacrifices to [Gazankoa](<../../../people/extraplanar-powers/gazankoa.md>). [Ryu](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/ryu.md>), [Trok](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/trok.md>), [Kaleho](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/kaleho.md>), and [Wazir](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/wazir.md>) debate resurrection lore, [gatza](<../../../things/materials/gatza.md>) visions, and Airion's ruined tower in the [North Cliffs](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/north-cliffs.md>) above the old sea cave before agreeing to rest and investigate the tower in the morning.

The next morning, the group climbs to Airion's ruined tower in the [North Cliffs](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/north-cliffs.md>), above the sea cave, and finds evidence that someone has surreptitiously been living there. The tower proves older than the surrounding watchtower ruin, and its interior throws them into a convincing water realm where [Wazir](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/wazir.md>), [Trok](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/trok.md>), and [Kaleho](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/kaleho.md>) contend with the sensation of drowning, swimming, and being trapped in a bubble of force. While the illusion fades back to a shallow flooded chamber, it leaves the party shaken and wondering about the connection to [Azar the Lost](<../../../people/mawarans/azar-the-lost.md>).

Seeking more information, the party fans out across [Hamri](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/hamri.md>) for local witnesses. [Ryu](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/ryu.md>) and [Trok](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/trok.md>) speak with [Jaeson](<../../../people/mawarans/jaeson.md>), while the others inquire with [Samar](<../../../people/mawarans/samar.md>) and [Reliah](<../../../people/mawarans/reliah.md>) in the Drowned Flats and [Orden](<../../../people/mawarans/orden.md>) by the docks. Their accounts build a profile of a worn older man who appeared in recent weeks, had been living in or near the tower, left fish remains and the smell of [gatza](<../../../things/materials/gatza.md>), may connect to the Drowned Flats deaths, and had been asking around about lead. After a morning of chasing rumors, the party reconvenes at the market before lunch.

Around midday, the party regroups at the fish market and learns from [Benat](<../../../people/lizardfolk/benat.md>) that Airion's tower belongs to a much older magical history around [Hamri](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/hamri.md>) and its water-linked extraplanar weak spots. The party splits again: [Kaleho](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/kaleho.md>) and [Trok](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/trok.md>) visit [Ovina](<../../../people/dwarves/ovina.md>), who admits that the old man paid with a valuable gold-and-sapphire ring for a sealed, lead-lined box, and [Wazir](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/wazir.md>) and [Ryu](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/ryu.md>) climb to Glitter Cliff to ask [Sulfi](<../../../people/mawarans/sulfi-mahadra.md>) about the phantom boat. [Sulfi](<../../../people/mawarans/sulfi-mahadra.md>) declines to meet directly, but her servant reports that she knows of no strange ship and wants any such vessel reported for proper fees and duties.

In the late afternoon, the party regroups at Shark's Dock. [Wazir](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/wazir.md>) tests the [dismantled ring](<../items-and-treasure/airion-s-ring-of-elemental-binding.md>) and the lead-lined box at Ovina's shop; the group theorizes that the lead-lined box may be a way to contain the strange  [magical knife](<../../../things/magic-items/blood-knife.md>) associated with Azar. [Ryu](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/ryu.md>) and [Trok](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/trok.md>) speak with [Yaz](<../../../people/mawarans/yaz.md>) after dark about the living ocean and the [Splinters](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/splinters.md>), and the group returns to the Leviathan to plan. They decide that one person will approach Azar when he comes to collect the box in the morning, while the others remain hidden near the dock.

At dawn, the party waits around Shark's Dock while [Ryu](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/ryu.md>) watches the harbor and spots the phantom boat coming in fast from the north. Azar arrives in oilskin cloaks, collects the lead-lined box from [Ovina](<../../../people/dwarves/ovina.md>), and tries to walk back to the dock without answering Ryu's questions about the tower, the deaths, and the [Sentient Ocean](<../../../people/extraplanar-powers/sentient-ocean.md>). Invisible [Wazir](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/wazir.md>) whispers Nadine's name through a coin, Azar reveals himself and insists [Gazankoa](<../../../people/extraplanar-powers/gazankoa.md>) is helping him save his family, and [Ryu](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/ryu.md>) realizes the invitation onto the boat is a trap as weapons come out.

The failed parley turns violent as Azar throws black tentacles across Shark's Dock and runs for the phantom boat while his drowned servant rises from the vessel. [Ryu](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/ryu.md>) and [Wazir](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/wazir.md>) are crushed and restrained by the tentacles, [Trok](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/trok.md>) drags Azar into the water, [Kaleho](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/kaleho.md>) lands punishing crossbow shots, and the lead-lined box is dropped in the boat as the party tries to push the vessel out of Azar's reach. Azar's Armor of Agathys knocks [Trok](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/trok.md>) unconscious, Ryu's healing spirit brings him back, Wazir's shatter strips away the cold armor, and Azar's lightning kills a bystander before Trok's crocodile-shaped Eldritch Maul strikes finally break the tentacles and drop Azar unconscious; [Kaleho](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/kaleho.md>) then kills the drowned servant.

Afterward, [Wazir](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/wazir.md>) heals Azar and the group binds him while they recover from the fight, calm Shark's Dock, tend the woman killed by Azar's lightning, and hear [Azar's full tale](<../notes/azar-s-tale.md>). Azar had lost [Nadine](<../../../people/mawarans/nadine.md>), become hooked on [gatza](<../../../things/materials/gatza.md>), and heard [Gazankoa](<../../../people/extraplanar-powers/gazankoa.md>) speak through the [Blood Knife](<../../../things/magic-items/blood-knife.md>). He believed that killing people in the right way, with their blood replaced by water, would restore his family. That evening, the party searches the [Splinters](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/splinters.md>), quickly destroys two more drowned zombies, and finds [Sefa](<../../../people/mawarans/sefa.md>) dead in the lair. From the lair, they recover [three annotated books from Airion's library](<../items-and-treasure/airion-s-elemental-research.md>), a [brass bowl](<../items-and-treasure/airion-s-bowl-of-commanding-water-elementals.md>), and scattered coins that are paid as restitution to [Sefa](<../../../people/mawarans/sefa.md>) and the families of the dead who can be found. On Aug 6, [Wazir](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/wazir.md>) identifies the [Blood Knife](<../../../things/magic-items/blood-knife.md>), now locked in the lead-lined box, as a mystery beyond his magic, and the brass bowl as a bowl of commanding water elementals that must be filled with fresh ocean water and used immediately. A few days later, on Aug 9, Azar leaves south with [Benat](<../../../people/lizardfolk/benat.md>) to heal before returning to make amends to [Hamri](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/hamri.md>).

Azar rejects the parley at Shark's Dock, fights with black tentacles, cold armor, lightning, and a drowned servant, and is knocked unconscious by [Trok](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/trok.md>) while [Kaleho](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/kaleho.md>) kills the servant.
