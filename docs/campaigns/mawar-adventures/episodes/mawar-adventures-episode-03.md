# Mawar Adventures Episode 03

!!! info "The Tomb of Yerkir-khor: in which a missing treasure hunter leads to a hidden tomb"
	*Featuring: [Ryu](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/ryu.md>), [Trok](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/trok.md>), [Kaleho](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/kaleho.md>), [Nerissa](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/nerissa.md>)*
	*In Taelgar: Oct 23, 1747 DR to Oct 28, 1747 DR*
	*On Earth: Saturday, November 27, 2021*
	*[Hamri](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/hamri.md>) and [Yerkir-khor's monument and tomb](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/tomb-of-yerkir-khor.md>)*

[Orden](<../../../people/mawarans/orden.md>) asks the party to find [Hiyasa](<../../../people/mawarans/hiyasa.md>), whose treasure map leads them from [Hamri](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/hamri.md>) into [Yerkir-khor's buried earth tomb](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/tomb-of-yerkir-khor.md>), where they survive traps, stone guardians, and a near-fatal fight before returning with her and the [tomb](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/tomb-of-yerkir-khor.md>)'s strange spoils.

## Timeline

- Oct 23, 1747 DR, evening: [Orden](<../../../people/mawarans/orden.md>) asks [Trok](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/trok.md>) and [Kaleho](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/kaleho.md>) to find his daughter [Hiyasa](<../../../people/mawarans/hiyasa.md>), who recently returned to [Hamri](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/hamri.md>) after ten years away and has gone missing while seeking treasure.
- Oct 24, 1747 DR, morning: [Trok](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/trok.md>) and [Kaleho](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/kaleho.md>) recruit [Ryu](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/ryu.md>) and [Nerissa](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/nerissa.md>) to help, gather rumors from [Abelard](<../../../people/mawarans/abelard.md>) at the [Leviathan Inn](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/leviathan-inn.md>), and head up the [Mirmir](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/rivers/mirmir.md>) following [Hiyasa](<../../../people/mawarans/hiyasa.md>)'s tracks.
- Oct 24, 1747 DR - Oct 26, 1747 DR: The party travels up the [Mirmir](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/rivers/mirmir.md>); [Ryu](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/ryu.md>) tracks [Hiyasa](<../../../people/mawarans/hiyasa.md>) inland to [Yerkir-khor's monument](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/tomb-of-yerkir-khor.md>).
- Oct 26, 1747 DR, midday: The party descends into [Yerkir-khor's tomb](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/tomb-of-yerkir-khor.md>), dodges traps, and destroys its first guardians.
- Oct 26, 1747 DR, afternoon: Continuing deeper into [the tomb](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/tomb-of-yerkir-khor.md>), the party finds [Hiyasa](<../../../people/mawarans/hiyasa.md>) in the inner sanctum and frees her after destroying Yerkir-khor's ancient earth protector.
- Oct 26, 1747 DR - Oct 28, 1747 DR: The party travels back to [Hamri](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/hamri.md>) with [Hiyasa](<../../../people/mawarans/hiyasa.md>), the [treasure map](<../items-and-treasure/hiyasa-s-treasure-map.md>) she brought from [Chardon](<../../../gazetteer/greater-chardon/chardonian-empire/chardon/chardon.md>), [tomb](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/tomb-of-yerkir-khor.md>) treasure, and [ancient papers](<../items-and-treasure/ancient-texts-of-yerkir-khor.md>) for [Wazir](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/wazir.md>) to decipher.

- [Yerkir-khor](<../../../people/other-nonhumans/yerkir-khor.md>) (elemental): buried earth lord.

## Places

- [Hamri](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/hamri.md>) (town in the [Mawar Confederacy](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/mawar-confederacy.md>), the [Mawakel Peninsula](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/mawakel-peninsula.md>)): coastal town on the [Mawakel Peninsula](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/mawakel-peninsula.md>) where [Orden](<../../../people/mawarans/orden.md>) asks for help, the party gathers rumors, and the recovered treasure is later divided. Session context includes: Trok's beach shack, Ryu's tower, the [Leviathan Inn](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/leviathan-inn.md>), and return inn scene with Wazir.
- [Yerkir-khor's monument and tomb](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/tomb-of-yerkir-khor.md>): partially buried stone flower monument in the inland forest, containing [Yerkir-khor's tomb](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/tomb-of-yerkir-khor.md>), an ancient earth-themed tomb filled with crystal mosaics, traps, stone guardians, and treasure. Session context includes: open trap door, entrance shaft, crystal-mosaic hall, upper guardian chamber, lower corridors, lightning-crystal stair, and inner sanctum.

  <div class="taelgar-session-zoom__level" data-zoom-level="short"><p>The guardian rejects the ocean offerings presented as tribute, sparking a battle that ends when <a href="/taelgarverse/people/pcs/mawar/ryu/">Ryu</a> destroys the guardian ending the stone magic trapping <a href="/taelgarverse/people/mawarans/hiyasa/">Hiyasa</a>.</p></div>
  <div class="taelgar-session-zoom__level" data-zoom-level="intermediate"><p>The offered tribute of shells, a pearl, and <a href="/taelgarverse/people/pcs/mawar/kaleho/">Kaleho</a>&#x27;s merfolk book only enrage the guardian, which treats the ocean-linked offerings as insults to <a href="/taelgarverse/people/other-nonhumans/yerkir-khor/">Yerkir-khor</a>. The fight turns desperate when it changes the floor to mud, traps <a href="/taelgarverse/people/mawarans/hiyasa/">Hiyasa</a> and others, knocks <a href="/taelgarverse/people/pcs/mawar/trok/">Trok</a> unconscious, and nearly hardens him into stone before <a href="/taelgarverse/people/pcs/mawar/ryu/">Ryu</a> lands the killing blow on the guardian and revives <a href="/taelgarverse/people/pcs/mawar/trok/">Trok</a>.</p></div>

The stone magic broken, <a href="/taelgarverse/people/mawarans/hiyasa/">Hiyasa</a> frees herself. She tries to pocket one of the jars, but relaxes after <a href="/taelgarverse/people/pcs/mawar/trok/">Trok</a> makes clear they came as a favor to <a href="/taelgarverse/people/mawarans/orden/">Orden</a> rather than as rival tomb robbers.</p></div>

## Combat

- Gargoyle Wardens: stone gargoyles, serpent statue construct. Four stone gargoyles defend the main hall and try to activate the serpent-headed statue; the party destroys them before the statue fully wakes.
- Guardian of [Yerkir-khor](<../../../people/other-nonhumans/yerkir-khor.md>): stone guardian of [Yerkir-khor](<../../../people/other-nonhumans/yerkir-khor.md>). The guardian attacks after rejecting the party's tribute and is destroyed by [Ryu](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/ryu.md>) after nearly entombing [Trok](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/trok.md>) in hardening mud.
