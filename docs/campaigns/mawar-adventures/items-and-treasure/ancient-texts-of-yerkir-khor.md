# The Ancient Texts of Yerkir-Khor
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
- :octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } __(ancient papers)__  
   Owned by [Wazir](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/wazir.md>)  
</div>


A collection of notes recovered by the [Adventurers of Mawar](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/adventurers-of-mawar.md>) from the [Tomb of Yerkir-Khor](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/tomb-of-yerkir-khor.md>) in DR 1747. They were found in the inner sanctum, protected and preserved by some kind of magical yellow dust, inside one of six gem-stoppered jars behind the altar. 

The adventurers brough the papers  back to [Hamri](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/hamri.md>) and given to [Wazir](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/wazir.md>) to decipher.
