# The Amulet of Khathayi
:octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } **(amulet)**  

A golden amulet carried by a fire cultist dedicated to a mysterious entity known as [Khathayi the Flametongue](<../../../people/other-humans/khathayi.md>). It was found by [Ryu](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/ryu.md>), [Nerissa](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/nerissa.md>), and [Ander](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/ander.md>), who noticed that the motif on the amulet matched a symbol on [Hiyasa's Treasure Map](<hiyasa-s-treasure-map.md>) drawn near an island north of the peninsula.
