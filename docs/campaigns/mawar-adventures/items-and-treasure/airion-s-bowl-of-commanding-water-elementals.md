# Airion's Bowl of Commanding Water Elementals
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
- :octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } __(elemental command bowl)__  
   Owned by [Wazir](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/wazir.md>)  
</div>


A magical bowl recovered from Azar's lair in the [Splinters](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/splinters.md>). It was one of the treasures of [Airion the Mistspeaker](<../../../people/other-humans/airion.md>) that [Azar the Lost](<../../../people/mawarans/azar-the-lost.md>) carried with him after returning from the mountains to the western coast of the [Mawakel Peninsula](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/mawakel-peninsula.md>).

After the fight at [Shark's Dock](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/hamri.md#the-sharks-dock>), [Wazir](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/wazir.md>) identified it more specifically as a bowl of commanding water elementals. It must be filled with fresh ocean water and used immediately in order to work.
