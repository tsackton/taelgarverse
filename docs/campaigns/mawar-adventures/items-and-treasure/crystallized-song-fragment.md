# The Crystallized Song of the Sentient Ocean
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
- :octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } __(crystal)__  
   Owned by [Rayna](<../../../people/mawarans/rayna.md>)  
</div>


A strange, magical crystal fragment connected to the [Sentient Ocean](<../../../people/extraplanar-powers/sentient-ocean.md>). It was associated with the [Shrine to Guzo the Mariner](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/shrine-to-guzo-the-mariner.md>) in [Hamri](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/hamri.md>) for many years, though the story of how it came to be there is not known. It was later [recovered](<../episodes/mawar-adventures-episode-05.md>) by the [Adventurers of Mawar](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/adventurers-of-mawar.md>) and given to [Rayna](<../../../people/mawarans/rayna.md>), to return to [Omi](<../../../gazetteer/faraway-places/omi.md>).
