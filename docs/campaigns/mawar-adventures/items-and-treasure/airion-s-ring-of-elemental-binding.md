# The Ring of Elemental Binding
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
- :octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } __(ring)__  
   Owned by [Ovina](<../../../people/dwarves/ovina.md>)  
</div>


One of the treasures of [Airion the Mistspeaker](<../../../people/other-humans/airion.md>), recovered by [Azar the Lost](<../../../people/mawarans/azar-the-lost.md>) during his search for lore about elemental magic, genies, and living ocean water. According to [Azar's Tale](<../notes/azar-s-tale.md>), the ring could bind a genie and command one service from it.

Azar used the ring to compel [Nimessa](<../../../people/other-nonhumans/nimessa.md>), a [marid](<../../../creatures/extraplanar/marid.md>) of the [Elemental Plane of Water](<../../../cosmology/energy-realms/elemental-plane-of-water.md>), to steal living water from the [Sentient Ocean](<../../../people/extraplanar-powers/sentient-ocean.md>) and bring it to [Hamri](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/hamri.md>). [The Ciphered Scroll](<../notes/the-ciphered-scroll.md>) records that Nimessa had little choice but to obey and that she could not harm Azar until a thousand and one days had passed after the command.

The spent ring, no longer magical, remains a valuable gold-and-sapphire ring, given by Azar to [Ovina](<../../../people/dwarves/ovina.md>) as payment for the construction of a lead-lined box meant to quiet the [Blood Knife](<../../../things/magic-items/blood-knife.md>).
