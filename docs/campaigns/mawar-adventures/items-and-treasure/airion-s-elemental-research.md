# Airion's Research on the Elemental Plane of Water
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
- :octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } __(annotated books of elemental lore)__  
   Owned by [Wazir](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/wazir.md>)  
</div>


Three annotated books concerning the [Elemental Plane of Water](<../../../cosmology/energy-realms/elemental-plane-of-water.md>) and the [Sentient Ocean](<../../../people/extraplanar-powers/sentient-ocean.md>), once owned by [Airion the Mistspeaker](<../../../people/other-humans/airion.md>). His marginal glosses connect their content to his own research into elemental magic, planar weak points, genies, and the [Sentient Ocean](<../../../people/extraplanar-powers/sentient-ocean.md>).

The three books are:

- [On the Mysteries of the Infinite Depths](<../../../things/books/on-the-mysteries-of-the-infinite-depths.md>), a Drankorian natural history treatise on leviathans and other deep creatures of the [Elemental Plane of Water](<../../../cosmology/energy-realms/elemental-plane-of-water.md>), including [Gazankoa](<../../../people/extraplanar-powers/gazankoa.md>).
- [On Elemental Forms](<../../../things/books/on-elemental-forms.md>), a Drankorian-era treatise on elemental beings, especially the sentient and powerful genies.
- [Studies of the Westward Deeps](<../../../things/books/studies-of-the-westward-deeps.md>), an Elvish natural history and maritime survey of the western ocean, preserved here in Airion's annotated copy, which includes a discussion of the [Sentient Ocean](<../../../people/extraplanar-powers/sentient-ocean.md>). 

Azar studied the annotated books after emerging in [Mendin](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/mendin.md>) with Airion's treasure. From Airion's glosses, he learned enough about Yi'weti to believe that living water could make his false resurrection ritual work. That conclusion led him to use [Airion's Ring of Elemental Binding](<airion-s-ring-of-elemental-binding.md>) to compel [Nimessa](<../../../people/other-nonhumans/nimessa.md>), a [marid](<../../../creatures/extraplanar/marid.md>), to steal living water from the [Sentient Ocean](<../../../people/extraplanar-powers/sentient-ocean.md>).

After Azar's defeat at [Shark's Dock](<../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/hamri.md#the-sharks-dock>), the [Adventurers of Mawar](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/adventurers-of-mawar.md>) searched his lair in the Splinters and recovered the books along with the brass bowl and coins.
