# Pandar

Pandar was the secretive northern kingdom ruled by [Cha'mutte](<../../people/extraplanar-powers/cha-mutte.md>) and used as a staging ground for his armies. Sustained almost entirely by magic, Pandar collapsed upon Cha'mutte's death.
