# Amani
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } A destroyed realm in the [Northwest Coast](<../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/northwest-coast.md>)  
</div>


Amani was a prominent realm, occupying much of the land around what is now [Greater Voltara](<../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/northern-provinces/greater-voltara.md>), during and immediately after the [Great War](<../../events/1500s/great-war.md>). The realm was completely decimated during the [Blood Years](<../../events/1500s/blood-years.md>) and survives only in rumors, legends, and folklore.
