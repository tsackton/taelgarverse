# Serpentine
:octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } **(material)**  

Serpentine is a pale green gemstone that is associated with necromancy and enchantment magic. 

Serpentine weapons, produced by [Avatus](<../../people/historical-figures/avatus.md>), played a major role in the [Serpentine Wars](<../../events/1300s/serpentine-wars.md>) between the [Dominion of Avatus](<../../history/historical-realms/dominion-of-avatus.md>) and [Addermarch](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/addermarch.md>). Tracking and safely disposing these cursed weapons after these wars was an early formative task for the [Rangers](<../../groups/rangers.md>), who recorded the [Serpentine Register](<../books/serpentine-register.md>) to document their efforts.
