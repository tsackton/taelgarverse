# Prime Arcana
:octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } **(hypothesized alchemical substance)**  

The Prime Arcana is a theoretical alchemical substance, discussed in [The Way of the Stone](<../books/the-way-of-the-stone.md>). Rumors and legends suggest that many alchemists have sought this material, but there are no conclusive proofs it exists. It is said to be a key ingredient in many concoctions of great power, especially those granting invulnerability and youth. 

The mad alchemist [Grimstone](<../../people/other-humans/grimstone.md>), working in [Western Cymea](<../../gazetteer/western-green-sea/realms/western-cymea.md>), believed the Prime Arcana was related to petrification. He thought that transmuted flesh retained the essence of flesh, that magically created stone retained magic, and that a living soul turned into stone might produce the substance he needed. This led him to capture prisoners and use a captive basilisk to petrify them for study. He was [killed](<../../campaigns/one-shots/labyrinths-of-the-lost/labyrinths-of-the-lost.md>) when several of his [kidnap victims](<../../people/pcs/other-pcs/labyrinth-prisoners/labyrinth-prisoners.md>) turned on him.
