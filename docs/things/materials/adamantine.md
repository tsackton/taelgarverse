# Adamantine
:octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } **(material)**  

Adamantine is a valuable and extremely hard metal, prized for the making of arms, armor, and durable magical devices. It can also be used to help stabilize and channel other magical effects and is often incorporated into magical items.
