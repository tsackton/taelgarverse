# The Lay of Estar
:octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } **An [Elvish](<../../creatures/species/elves.md>) poem, written in ancient times**  

*The Lay of Estar* is an ancient elven poem about Estar, a friend of wanderers and one of the first elves to discover, explore, and name the elemental planes. Its description of the [Elemental Plane of Air](<../../cosmology/energy-realms/elemental-plane-of-air.md>) presents the plane as a realm of endless skies, drifting clouds, howling winds, and floating earth motes.
