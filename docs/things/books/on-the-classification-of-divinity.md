# On the Classification of Divinity
:octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } **A [Drankorian](<../../history/historical-realms/drankorian-empire.md>) theological treatise, written during the [Drankorian Era](<../../history/history-of-the-drankorian-empire.md>)**  

*On the Classification of Divinity* is an influential early Drankorian work of theological cosmology. Later scholars often cite it as source for the distinction between [Embodied Gods](<../../gods-and-religions/gods/embodied-gods/embodied-gods.md>), powerful celestial beings with embodied manifestations, and [Incorporeal Gods](<../../gods-and-religions/gods/incorporeal-gods/incorporeal-gods.md>), spiritual essences drawn from the divine power of creation, though the treatise itself does not claim to have discovered this theological distinction.
