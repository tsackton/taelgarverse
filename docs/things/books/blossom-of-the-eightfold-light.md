# Blossom of the Eightfold Light
:octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } **A [Mos Numena](<../../gods-and-religions/religions/mos-numena/mos-numena.md>) religious poem, written during the [Drankorian Era](<../../history/history-of-the-drankorian-empire.md>)**  

*Blossom of the Eightfold Light* is an ancient [Mos Numenan](<../../gods-and-religions/religions/mos-numena/mos-numena.md>) lyrical poem about the [Divine Presence](<../../gods-and-religions/gods/high-gods/divine-presence.md>), the [Plane of Creation](<../../cosmology/plane-of-creation.md>), and the [Eight Divines](<../../gods-and-religions/gods/incorporeal-gods/mos-numena-pantheon/mos-numena-pantheon.md>).
