# On the Power of Creation
:octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } **A [Stoneborn](<../../creatures/species/stoneborn.md>) cosmological treatise, by Yendalo**  

*On the Power of Creation* is a treatise by Yendalo, the Stoneborn metaphysicist. Yendalo argues that the [Land of the Dead](<../../cosmology/land-of-the-dead.md>) is best understood as the Divine Veil, a cosmological barrier shielding the Inner Realms from the full force of the [Plane of Creation](<../../cosmology/plane-of-creation.md>), rather than simply a gathering place for souls.
