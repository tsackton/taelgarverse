# The Siren's Embrace
:octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } **A play**  

*The Siren's Embrace* is a famous tragedy that shaped poetic images of the [Elemental Plane of Water](<../../cosmology/energy-realms/elemental-plane-of-water.md>). It includes a vision of a vast coral reef bursting with life beneath a shimmering sea.
