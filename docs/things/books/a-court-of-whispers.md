# A Court of Whispers
:octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } **An [Apporian](<../../gazetteer/greater-chardon/chardonian-empire/apporia/apporia.md>) play, written in the DR 1400s**  

*A Court of Whispers* is a tragedy about Avim of Cedrano, who enters a strange fey court full of games of chance and rare delicacies. Avim believes he spends only an afternoon there, but returns to [Cedrano](<../../gazetteer/greater-chardon/chardonian-empire/apporia/cedrano.md>) to find that ten years have passed.
