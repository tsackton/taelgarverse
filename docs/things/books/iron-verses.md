# Iron Verses
:octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } **A [Dwarven](<../../creatures/species/dwarves.md>) religious epic**  

*Iron Verses* is among the most famous dwarven religious epics describing the [Heart of the Mountain](<../../cosmology/spiritual-realms/heart-of-the-mountain.md>), the divine realm of the [Bahrazel](<../../gods-and-religions/gods/embodied-gods/bahrazel/bahrazel.md>). One passage frequently translated into human tongues describes this realm as a vast cavern of endless tunnels, crystal lanterns, glittering gemstones, a dark central pool, and the distant sound of hammer on iron.
