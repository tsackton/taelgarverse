# On the Mysteries of the Infinite Depths
:octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } **A [Drankorian](<../../history/historical-realms/drankorian-empire.md>) natural history treatise, written during the [Drankorian Era](<../../history/history-of-the-drankorian-empire.md>), by Leandros of Drankor**  

*On the Mysteries of the Infinite Depths* is Leandros of Drankor's catalog of leviathans and other deep creatures of the [Elemental Plane of Water](<../../cosmology/energy-realms/elemental-plane-of-water.md>). It describes colossal squids the size of cities, endless serpents, shadowy unidentified forms, and the vast being called [Gazankoa](<../../people/extraplanar-powers/gazankoa.md>).
