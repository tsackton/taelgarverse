The Serpentine Register was a [Ranger](<../../groups/rangers.md>) record of known [Serpentine](<../materials/serpentine.md>) weapons and their fates, compiled in the aftermath of the Addermarch fighting in the [Serpentine Wars](<../../events/1300s/serpentine-wars.md>).

Originally produced by the [Rangers](<../../groups/rangers.md>) of [Carlinshire](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/carlinshire.md>), the local copy was lost, but surviving copies may exist in [Adderfell](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/adderfell.md>), [Sembara](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/sembara/sembara.md>), or elsewhere.
