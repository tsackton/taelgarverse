# The Riven Veil
:octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } **A [Drankorian](<../../history/historical-realms/drankorian-empire.md>) theological treatise, written during the [Drankorian Era](<../../history/history-of-the-drankorian-empire.md>), by Marcion of Iridel**  

*The Riven Veil* is an ancient Drankorian text by the theological cosmologist Marcion of Iridel. Marcion claims that the [Abyss](<../../cosmology/spiritual-realms/abyss.md>) arose from the worst impulses of humanity, drawing on Chironides the Wanderer's argument that human preaching of hellfire and damnation helped shape the Abyss out of Soulstuff.
