# On the Nature of Reflections and Transitive Magic
:octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } **A cosmological treatise**  

*On the Nature of Reflections and Transitive Magic* collects the most extensive speculation on the hypothesized [Mirror Realm](<../../cosmology/demiplanes-and-echo-realms/mirror-realm.md>). The work claims the Mirror Realm is real but dangerous to access, because a traveler who enters it allows their reflection to leave and potentially replace them in the [Material Plane](<../../cosmology/material-plane.md>).
