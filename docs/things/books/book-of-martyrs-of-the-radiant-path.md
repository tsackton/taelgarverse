# Book of Martyrs of the Radiant Path
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
- :octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } __A [Mos Numena](<../../gods-and-religions/religions/mos-numena/mos-numena.md>) martyrology, compiled by priests of the [Radiant Path](<../../groups/drankorian-societies/radiant-path.md>)__  
   Last updated in DR 1053 (697 years ago)  
</div>


The Book of Martyrs of the Radiant Path is a hidden record and memorial that was kept by the priests of the [Radiant Path](<../../groups/drankorian-societies/radiant-path.md>) in [Drankor](<../../gazetteer/drankorian-hinterland/drankor/drankor.md>). It records those who vanished or were taken during [Apollyon](<../../people/historical-figures/drankorian-emperors/apollyon.md>)’s reign, preserving their names and brief life stories for the faithful. One copy was recovered from the [Temple of the Eight Divines](<../../gazetteer/drankorian-hinterland/drankor/temple-of-the-eight-divines.md>) in [Drankor](<../../gazetteer/drankorian-hinterland/drankor/drankor.md>) by the [Dunmar Fellowship](<../../people/pcs/dunmar-fellowship/dunmar-fellowship.md>) in DR 1749; whether other copies exist is not known.


In DR 1749, the [Dunmar Fellowship](<../../people/pcs/dunmar-fellowship/dunmar-fellowship.md>) recovered a copy of book from the administrative complex of the [Temple of the Eight Divines](<../../gazetteer/drankorian-hinterland/drankor/temple-of-the-eight-divines.md>) while seeking the souls bound in [Apollyon's Phylactery](<../artifacts-of-power/apollyon-s-phylactery.md>). The surviving pages included accounts of [Antonius Patrius](<../../people/historical-figures/antonius-patrius.md>), Floria of Aphasium, Marius of Drankor, and Valanthe, four of the seven souls bound to [Apollyon's Phylactery](<../artifacts-of-power/apollyon-s-phylactery.md>).
