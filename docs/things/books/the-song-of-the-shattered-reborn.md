# The Song of the Shattered, Reborn
:octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } **An epic poem**  

*The Song of the Shattered, Reborn* is an epic poem whose depiction of the [Land of the Dead](<../../cosmology/land-of-the-dead.md>) is among the most famous in Taelgar. It describes the realm as a gray expanse under a monochrome sun, where the ground flows like an outgoing tide and draws souls toward a far shore.
