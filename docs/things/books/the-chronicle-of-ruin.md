# The Chronicle of Ruin
:octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } **A cosmological collection**  

*The Chronicle of Ruin* is the most comprehensive known collection of tales concerning the [Abyss](<../../cosmology/spiritual-realms/abyss.md>). It describes the Abyss as a place of endless decay, ruined fortresses, collapsing kingdoms, rivers of blood, shifting landscapes, and layers spiraling downward into the domains of demon lords.
