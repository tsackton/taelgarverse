# Aetherium
:octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } **A [Drankorian](<../../history/historical-realms/drankorian-empire.md>) cosmological treatise, written during the [Drankorian Era](<../../history/history-of-the-drankorian-empire.md>)**  

The *Aetherium* is a famous Drankorian treatise on the [Ethereal Plane](<../../cosmology/ethereal-plane.md>). It is the basis for the dominant school of thought that identifies the Ethereal Plane as an echo or extension of the [Plane of Magic](<../../cosmology/plane-of-magic.md>), becoming more transparent and dilute near the borders of other planes.
