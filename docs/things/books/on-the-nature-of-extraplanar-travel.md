# On the Nature of Extraplanar Travel
:octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } **A cosmological treatise**  

*On the Nature of Extraplanar Travel* is a seminal work recording careful experimentation with planar travel. Its best-attested conclusion is that [extraplanar weak points](<../../cosmology/planar-concepts/extraplanar-weak-point.md>) do not make it easier to physically cross from one plane to another.
