# Verdant Canticles
:octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } **An [Elvish](<../../creatures/species/elves.md>) poem collection**  

*Verdant Canticles* is an elven collection of tales and poems about [Arborea](<../../cosmology/spiritual-realms/arborea.md>). Its songs describe a paradise of untouched beauty, with storms galloping across plains like wild beasts and trees reaching so high their crowns fade into the sky, while also warning that such nature may be too vast and uncontrolled to be safe.
