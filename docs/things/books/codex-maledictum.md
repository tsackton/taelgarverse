# Codex Maledictum
:octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } **A cosmological treatise**  

*Codex Maledictum* is an infamous collection of accounts from supposed planar travelers who claimed to have glimpsed [Pandemonium](<../../cosmology/spiritual-realms/pandemonium.md>). The surviving descriptions gathered in the book are fragmentary and contradictory, depicting Pandemonium as a howling void of endless tunnels, wind-carved stone, and shadows that twist into laughter.
