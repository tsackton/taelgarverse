# On the Ordering of Giants
:octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } **(book)**  

An ancient scholarly treatise on giants, written in a strange language presumed to be the first tongue of humans. Only one copy is known to exist: it was preserved in the library of [Eudomes](<../../people/historical-figures/eudomes.md>) at [Stormcaller Tower](<../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/hara-basin/stormcaller-tower.md>), recovered by the [Dunmar Fellowship](<../../people/pcs/dunmar-fellowship/dunmar-fellowship.md>), and now is kept in the moving library on [Vindristjarna](<../ships/vindristjarna.md>). 

The book describes the magical and metaphysical underpinning thought, by human scholars at least, to result in the well known differences among different types of giants.
