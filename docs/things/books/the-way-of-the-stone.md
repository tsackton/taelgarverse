# The Way of the Stone
:octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } **An alchemical tome, written before DR 1600, by an unknown author**  

*The Way of the Stone* is a fragile alchemical tome written in Draconic. The book contains recipes for invulnerability, youth, immortality, and related elixirs. But all the recipes depend on something called the [Prime Arcana](<../materials/prime-arcana.md>), which is only loosely described as a magical stone associated with transmutation magic. 

One extant copy was found on [Grimstone](<../../people/other-humans/grimstone.md>)'s corpse after the [Labyrinth Prisoners](<../../people/pcs/other-pcs/labyrinth-prisoners/labyrinth-prisoners.md>) killed him in his laboratory beneath [Perdoli Manor](<../../gazetteer/western-green-sea/cymea/perdoli-manor.md>); based on Grimstone's notes, he believed that the Prime Arcana could be made from the right petrified living victim, but the veracity of this statement is unknown.
