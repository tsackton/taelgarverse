# Light of the Everglade
:octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } **An [Elvish](<../../creatures/species/elves.md>) epic poem**  

*Light of the Everglade* is an elven epic poem, that tells, among other things, of the bard Laurlinde and how her silver voice and noble demeanor won her a boon from the archfey known as the [Queen of Sunset](<../../people/extraplanar-powers/archfey/cloudspinner.md>).
