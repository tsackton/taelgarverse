# Upon the Fractured Earth
:octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } **A cosmological treatise, written in ancient times**  

*Upon the Fractured Earth* is an ancient treatise that survives only in fragments. It argues that even a brief portal to the [Abyss](<../../cosmology/spiritual-realms/abyss.md>) can blight the land, twisting plants, imbuing stones with whispers of madness, and leaving scars that may endure for centuries.
