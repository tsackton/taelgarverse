# The Codex of Compound Planes
:octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } **A cosmological treatise, written in the DR 1500s, by [Airion the Mistspeaker](<../../people/other-humans/airion.md>)**  

*The Codex of Compound Planes* is a work by [Airion the Mistspeaker](<../../people/other-humans/airion.md>) on compound elemental realms. Airion claims that careful experimentation with planar travel revealed secondary elemental energies, including Frostfell, Pyreforge, Miredepths, Smokeshroud, Cloudmere, Bloomswell, Ashspire, and Ironroot.
