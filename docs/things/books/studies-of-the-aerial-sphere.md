# Studies of the Aerial Sphere
:octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } **A [Drankorian](<../../history/historical-realms/drankorian-empire.md>) cosmological monograph series, written during the [Drankorian Era](<../../history/history-of-the-drankorian-empire.md>), by members of the [Occulta Ludum](<../../groups/drankorian-societies/occulta-ludum.md>)**  

*Studies of the Aerial Sphere* is a monograph series on travel in the [Elemental Plane of Air](<../../cosmology/energy-realms/elemental-plane-of-air.md>), published by the Drankorian scholarly society known as the [Occulta Ludum](<../../groups/drankorian-societies/occulta-ludum.md>). Only three of a reported ten volumes survive in full, but the surviving volumes preserve first-hand accounts of falling, flight, wind currents, and navigation in the plane.
