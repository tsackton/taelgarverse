# The Crown of Purity
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>

   Created by Unknown  
   Owned by [Riswynn](<../../people/pcs/dunmar-fellowship/riswynn.md>)  
</div>


The Crown of Purity is an ancient and immensely powerful relic, rumored to be created from a fragment of reality during the Riving, whose form shifts to match its bearer. In the last days of the [Drankorian Empire](<../../history/historical-realms/drankorian-empire.md>), it was part of Apollyon’s imperial regalia and a focus for his dominion over the people of [Drankor](<../../gazetteer/drankorian-hinterland/drankor/drankor.md>). After his fall it passed out of sight. 


In the years after the [Great War](<../../events/1500s/great-war.md>), the Crown resurfaced in [Chardon](<../../gazetteer/greater-chardon/chardonian-empire/chardon/chardon.md>), still tied to Apollyon's purpose. It came to be controlled by the cult known as [The Cleansed](<../../groups/the-cleansed.md>), who used the crown to enthrall and command those they deemed impure, including forcing numerous [Chardonians](<../../gazetteer/greater-chardon/chardonian-empire/chardonian-empire.md>) to serve [Fausto](<../../people/chardonians/fausto.md>). Through the crown's magic, [The Cleansed](<../../groups/the-cleansed.md>) were able to infiltrate and control the [Hetaeri Magica](<../../groups/chardonian-organizations/hetaeri-magica.md>) and the [Society of the Open Scroll](<../../groups/chardonian-organizations/society-of-the-open-scroll.md>), key organizations in Chardon's magical establishment. 

In DR 1749, upon [Fausto](<../../people/chardonians/fausto.md>)'s death, [it was claimed](<../../campaigns/dunmari-frontier-campaign/session-notes/session-116-dufr.md>) by the [Dunmar Fellowship](<../../people/pcs/dunmar-fellowship/dunmar-fellowship.md>). After [much debate](<../../campaigns/dunmari-frontier-campaign/session-notes/session-117-dufr.md>), it was eventually taken by [Riswynn](<../../people/pcs/dunmar-fellowship/riswynn.md>) to aid in her quest to free more orcs from [Thark](<../../gods-and-religions/gods/embodied-gods/thark.md>). Upon [learning how to control its magic](<../../campaigns/dunmari-frontier-campaign/session-notes/session-123-dufr.md>) from the [Cloudspinner](<../../people/extraplanar-powers/archfey/cloudspinner.md>), its appearance changed again, reflecting a new bearer and purpose.
