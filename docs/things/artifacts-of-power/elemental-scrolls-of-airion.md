# The Elemental Scrolls of Airion
:octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } **(scroll)**  

The Elemental Scrolls of Airion are four legendary scrolls of elemental power crafted by the wizard [Airion Mistspeaker](<../../people/other-humans/airion.md>) before the [Great War](<../../events/1500s/great-war.md>), but lost amid the upheavals of the [Blood Years](<../../events/1500s/blood-years.md>). The four scrolls are known as the Flamekeeper Scroll, the Stonemaster Scroll, the Skyborn Scroll, and the [Deepwater Scroll](<../magic-items/deepwater-scroll.md>). 


In DR 1747, they became the subject of a long search by the [Great Library](<../../gazetteer/greater-chardon/chardonian-empire/chardon/great-library.md>). A group of treasure seekers, who would later become known as the [Silver Tempests](<../../people/pcs/silver-tempests/silver-tempests.md>), were hired by [Archivist Lyra](<../../people/chardonians/lyra.md>) of the [Great Library](<../../gazetteer/greater-chardon/chardonian-empire/chardon/great-library.md>) to recover these scrolls, from various locations associated with [Airion](<../../people/other-humans/airion.md>) in [Greater Voltara](<../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/northern-provinces/greater-voltara.md>) and environs. They succeeded, and all four scrolls were returned to [Chardon](<../../gazetteer/greater-chardon/chardonian-empire/chardon/chardon.md>) in the summer of DR 1747.
