# The Serpentine Dagger

The Serpentine Dagger was a cursed jeweled dagger associated with [Avatus](<../../people/historical-figures/avatus.md>). Those who handled it fell under a paranoid compulsion to keep the blade close, eventually descending to violent madness, and the curse drove multiple murders at [Torvaine Watch](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/torvaine-watch.md>) in DR 1715.

Recovered by the [Addermarch Mercenaries](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/addermarch-mercenaries.md>) after the fall of the watch, the dagger was ultimately unmade in the Temple of the Sibyl in [Roscombe](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/roscombe.md>) using the alchemical fire [Trueflame](<../materials/trueflame.md>), under the supervision of [Owen Tavish](<../../people/addermarians/owen-tavish.md>).
