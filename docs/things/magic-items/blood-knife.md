# The Blood Knife
:octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } **(magic dagger)**  

The Blood Knife is a wicked-looking curved dagger made from some kind of black metal. It is connected to [Gazankoa](<../../people/extraplanar-powers/gazankoa.md>) and was used by [Azar](<../../people/mawarans/azar-the-lost.md>) in the murders around [Hamri](<../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/hamri.md>), where victims were stabbed, drained of blood, and used in his failed attempts to restore the drowned dead.

The knife is somehow associated with Gazankoa, and possession of it brings dangerous dreams and influence. After Azar's defeat, the knife was recovered from his lair and sealed inside a lead-lined box, which appeared to quiet or contain it.
