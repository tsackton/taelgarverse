# The Serpentine Battleaxe
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
- :octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } __(Avatan magic weapon)__  
   Unmade by the [Addermarch Mercenaries](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/addermarch-mercenaries.md>) with [Trueflame](<../materials/trueflame.md>) on May 27th, 1715  
</div>


The Serpentine Battleaxe was a heavy, magically sharp battleaxe set with sinuous green [serpentine](<../materials/serpentine.md>) along the haft and head, forged in the era of the [Dominion of Avatus](<../../history/historical-realms/dominion-of-avatus.md>). It bore significant similarity to the [Serpentine Dagger](<serpentine-dagger.md>); both weapons were tied to the [Avatus-era wars](<../../events/1300s/serpentine-wars.md>) and carried enchantments that twisted their wielders toward obsession, isolation, and escalating violence.

On May 27, 1715, the [Addermarch Mercenaries](<../../people/pcs/addermarch/addermarch-mercenaries.md>) defeated the serpentine warlock beneath [Serethwyn Tor](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/serethwyn-tor.md>) and used [Trueflame](<../materials/trueflame.md>) to unmake the Serpentine Battleaxe.
