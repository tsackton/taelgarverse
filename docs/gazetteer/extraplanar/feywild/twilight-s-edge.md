# The Twilight's Edge
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } An extraplanar domain in the [Feywild](<../../../cosmology/feywild.md>), [Multiverse](<../../../cosmology/multiverse.md>)  
</div>


Twilight's Edge is a small Feywild realm, ruled by [Lord Serenveil](<../../../people/extraplanar-powers/archfey/lord-serenveil.md>), the Prince of the Vesperwind and Guardian of Twilight's Edge. It is one of the lesser twilight realms, coincident with lower [Enst](<../../greater-sembara/rivers/wistel-enst-watershed/enst.md>) and parts of northern [Addermarch](<../../greater-sembara/addermarch/addermarch.md>), caught between the larger powers of [Twilight's Grace](<twilight-s-grace.md>), Duskmire, and the ancient domain of [Morlaith](<../../../people/extraplanar-powers/archfey/morlaith.md>). 

Located on the immediate sunward side of the Sunset Gate, it is a land of the setting sun, wild in places, with unsettled borders.
