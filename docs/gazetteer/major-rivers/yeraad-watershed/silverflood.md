# Silverflood
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } A river in [Alta Tonaro](<../../greater-chardon/chardonian-empire/alta-tonaro/alta-tonaro.md>), the [Chardonian Empire](<../../greater-chardon/chardonian-empire/chardonian-empire.md>)  
    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } Located in the [Yeraad Watershed](<./yeraad-watershed.md>)  
</div>


The Silverflood is the principal river of Alta Tonaro, draining the mineral‑rich foothills north of the [Chataan Mountains](<../../greater-chardon/chataan-mountains.md>). 

In the late DR 1600s, traces of [chalyte](<../../../things/materials/chalyte.md>) were [discovered](<../../../campaigns/dunmari-frontier-campaign/letters-and-notes/reports-on-chalyte-in-alta-tonaro.md>) by [Chardonian](<../../greater-chardon/chardonian-empire/chardonian-empire.md>) explorers along the upper Silverflood and its tributaries, igniting a rush that transformed the region, though within a generation the easy lodes and river pans were exhausted, and the area fell from prominence as deeper reserves in the [Chataan Mountains](<../../greater-chardon/chataan-mountains.md>) proved too perilous to extract.
