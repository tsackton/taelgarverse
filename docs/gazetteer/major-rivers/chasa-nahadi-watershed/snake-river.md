# The Snake River
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } A river in the [Erbalta Plains](<../../northwest-coast/erbalta-plains/erbalta-plains.md>), the [Northwest Coast](<../../northwest-coast/northwest-coast.md>)  
    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } Located in the [Chasa-Nahadi Watershed](<./chasa-nahadi-watershed.md>)  
</div>


The Snake River is a long, meandering river that flows east of [Voltara](<../../northwest-coast/northern-provinces/voltara/voltara.md>) through the grasslands of the [Erbalta Plains](<../../northwest-coast/erbalta-plains/erbalta-plains.md>), forming one of the two great drainage branches of the northern [Chasa-Nahadi Watershed](<./chasa-nahadi-watershed.md>). The Snake River marks the eastern extent of [Greater Voltara](<../../northwest-coast/northern-provinces/greater-voltara.md>), and a small [Chardonian](<../../greater-chardon/chardonian-empire/chardonian-empire.md>) fort, the [Snake River Fort](<../../northwest-coast/northern-provinces/snake-river-fort.md>), guards the one easy crossing. 

Just north of [Lake Valandros](<../../greater-chardon/lake-valandros.md>) it joins the [Zarnato](<./zarnato.md>). Major tributaries include the [Kelvaros](<./kelvaros.md>) (draining [Pykolon Lake](<./pykolon-lake.md>)) and the [Wild River](<./wild-river.md>).
