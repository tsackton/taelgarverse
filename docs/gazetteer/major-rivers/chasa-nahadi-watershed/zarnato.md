# Zarnato
:speaker:{ .middle } *(zahr-NAH-toh)*  
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } A river in the [Northwest Coast](<../../northwest-coast/northwest-coast.md>)  
    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } Located in the [Chasa-Nahadi Watershed](<chasa-nahadi-watershed.md>)  
</div>


The Zarnato is the largest river of the northern branch of the [Chasa-Nahadi Watershed](<chasa-nahadi-watershed.md>), draining the interior of the [Northern Provinces](<../../northwest-coast/northern-provinces/northern-provinces.md>) and the western [Sentinels](<../../sentinel-range.md>). Just north of [Lake Valandros](<../../greater-chardon/lake-valandros.md>) it is joined by the [Snake River](<snake-river.md>), and the combined waters ultimately reach the lake.

The major tributaries of the Zarnato include the [Barzhen](<barzhen.md>), the outlet of the [Blackwater Fens](<../../northern-sentinels/blackwater-fens.md>), and the [Arqa](<arqa.md>), a swift river that descends from the Sentinels through wooded ravines.
