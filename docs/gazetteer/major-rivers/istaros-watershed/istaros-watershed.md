# The Istaros Watershed
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } A watershed in Taelgar  
</div>


The Istaros watershed is the vast river basin that gathers into the [Istaros](<istaros.md>) and its many tributaries, stretching from the [Mostreve Hills](<../../greater-sembara/mostreve-hills.md>) to the [Sea of Storms](<../../drankorian-hinterland/sea-of-storms.md>). For much of recorded history it has been a fertile crossroads, central first to vast elven realm of [Alcarinquë](<../../../history/historical-realms/alcarinque.md>), then to the [Drankorian Empire](<../../../history/historical-realms/drankorian-empire.md>); even after the [Fall of Drankor](<../../../events/1000s/1059/fall-of-drankor.md>) the upper Istaros served as a crucial connection between [Cymea](<../../western-green-sea/realms/cymea.md>) and the [Green Sea](<../../green-sea.md>), the [Istabor Alliance](<../../../history/historical-realms/istabor-alliance.md>), and the [Dunmari](<../../greater-dunmar/realms/dunmar/dunmar.md>). 

Since the [Great War](<../../../events/1500s/great-war.md>), much of the watershed has been defined by the scars and magical curses of [Cha'mutte](<../../../people/extraplanar-powers/cha-mutte.md>). The upper Istaros flows through the [Plaguelands](<../../upper-istaros/plaguelands.md>), where the ruined city of [Isingue](<../../upper-istaros/isingue.md>) lies near one of the watershed’s most storied confluences; beyond, it passes into the [Desolation of Cha'mutte](<../../drankorian-hinterland/desolation-of-cha-mutte.md>) around the ruined city of [Drankor](<../../drankorian-hinterland/drankor/drankor.md>).
