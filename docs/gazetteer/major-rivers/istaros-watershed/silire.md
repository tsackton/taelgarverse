# Silírë
:speaker:{ .middle } *(see-LEER-eh)*  
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } A river in the [Istaros Watershed](<istaros-watershed.md>)  
</div>


The Silírë is the easternmost of the three rivers that feed [Lathirion Lake](<lathirion-lake.md>). Together with the [Naithalë](<naithale.md>) and the [Velassë](<velasse.md>), its waters form the [Thalúrien](<thalurien.md>) downstream of the lake.
