# The ~Yuvanti Gap~
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } A gap in the [Darba Highlands](<./darba-highlands.md>), [Greater Dunmar](<../greater-dunmar.md>)  
</div>




The Yuvanti Gap is a highland saddle at the northern fringe of the [Yuvanti Mountains](<../yuvanti-mountains.md>), opening onto the rolling hills  [Darba Highlands](<./darba-highlands.md>). It is the height of land between the [Yuvanti Mountains](<../yuvanti-mountains.md>) and the [Chataan Mountains](<../../greater-chardon/chataan-mountains.md>) to the northwest; the source of the headwaters of the [Rudhan](<../rivers/rudhan.md>); and an important orographic barrier that shapes the passage of the monsoons, creating a rain shadow that keeps the interior of central Dunmar and eastern Dunmar drier than the coast.
