# Lunacosta

Lunacosta is a coastal market town in [Western Cymea](<../realms/western-cymea.md>), located at the mouth of the [Serrania River](<serrania-river.md>) on the [Western Gulf](<../../greater-sembara/western-gulf.md>). Its harbor makes it a natural gathering point for river traffic and travelers moving between the coast and the roads inland.

The town is modest but active by local standards, with inns and markets that draw farmers, fishers, sailors, and visitors from the surrounding countryside.
