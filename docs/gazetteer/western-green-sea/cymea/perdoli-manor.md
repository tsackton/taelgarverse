Perdoli Manor is a ruined estate in the hills of [Western Cymea](<../realms/western-cymea.md>), about a day's journey from [Vistalba](<vistalba.md>). It was built in DR 1479 by the [Perdoli Family](<../../../groups/perdoli-family.md>), wealthy merchants and traders who drew their fortune from wine, trade, and control of a marble quarry.

The estate once included formal gardens, a private chapel to [The Mother](<../../../gods-and-religions/gods/incorporeal-gods/mos-numena-pantheon/the-mother.md>), a smithy, service buildings, private family chambers, a hidden archive, wine cellars, and a master bedroom bolt-hole. After the Perdoli family declined in the wake of the [Great War](<../../../events/1500s/great-war.md>), the manor passed through later owners and fell into ruin. It was later occupied by a medusa, then by pirates and bandits who expanded the [old bolt-hole](<grimstone-s-lair.md>) into tunnels and a cave hideout.

By DR 1748, Perdoli Manor had become the center of [Grimstone](<../../../people/other-humans/grimstone.md>)'s prisoner-taking and petrification operation, with goblins occupying the grounds and Grimstone occupying the old pirate bolt-hole. In October DR 1748, the [Labyrinth Prisoners](<../../../people/pcs/other-pcs/labyrinth-prisoners/labyrinth-prisoners.md>) [escaped from the smithy](<../../../campaigns/one-shots/labyrinths-of-the-lost/labyrinths-of-the-lost-session-1.md>), crossed the estate through [hidden tunnels in the ruined chapel](<../../../campaigns/one-shots/labyrinths-of-the-lost/labyrinths-of-the-lost-session-2.md>), found Grimstone's lair, and [killed him](<../../../campaigns/one-shots/labyrinths-of-the-lost/labyrinths-of-the-lost-session-3.md>). 

Since then, the fate, and occupants, if any, of the manor are unknown.
