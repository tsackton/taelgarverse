# The Serranía River
:speaker:{ .middle } *(Seh-rrah-nee-ah)*

</div>


The Serrania River is a rushing, rocky river in [Western Cymea](<../realms/western-cymea.md>), flowing from the [Plataca Hills](<plataca-hills.md>) to the [Western Gulf](<../../greater-sembara/western-gulf.md>). Only the first ten miles or so from the coast are easily navigable, though a cart track runs further inland, connecting small agricultural villages. 

[Lunacosta](<lunacosta.md>) stands at the river's mouth, where the Serrania reaches the coast and forms a useful harbor.
