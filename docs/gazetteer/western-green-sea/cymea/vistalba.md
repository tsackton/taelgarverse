# Vistalba
:speaker:{ .middle } *(VEESS-tahl-bah)*  
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } A trading port in [Western Cymea](<../realms/western-cymea.md>), [Cymea](<../realms/cymea.md>), the [Western Green Sea](<../western-green-sea.md>)  
</div>


Vistalba is notable market town in [Western Cymea](<../realms/western-cymea.md>), a trading port on the [Western Gulf](<../../greater-sembara/western-gulf.md>) with a decent harbor. The harbor dates to [Drankorian](<../../../history/historical-realms/drankorian-empire.md>) days, when Vistalba was richer and handled the movement of Cymean wine, silver, and other goods.

The town has declined from that earlier prosperity, leaving it with a run-down, lost-in-the-past character. Even so, its old harbor remains a fine and safe port, and it looks to investment from Tollen looks to revive its fortunes.
