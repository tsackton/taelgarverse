# Kedron
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } A river in [Cedrano](<cedrano.md>), the [Chardonian Empire](<../chardonian-empire.md>), [Greater Chardon](<../../greater-chardon.md>)  
</div>


The Kedron is the principal river of the province of [Cedrano](<cedrano.md>), flowing through the Kedron valley to the sea at [Castrella](<castrella.md>). The valley is among the few inhabited stretches of Cedrano’s rugged interior.
