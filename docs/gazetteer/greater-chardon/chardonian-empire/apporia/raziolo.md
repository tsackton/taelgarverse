# Raziolo
:speaker:{ .middle } *(ra-TSYO-lo)*  
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } A province in the [Chardonian Empire](<../chardonian-empire.md>)  
    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } Located in [Apporia](<./apporia.md>)  
</div>


Raziolo is one of the three Apporian provinces on the northern coast of [the Apporian Peninsula](<./apporia.md>), occupying the flat coastal plain on the northern tip of the peninsula. It is a region of hot, dry summers and mild winters, where fields of winter wheat give way to brown earth and olive groves. 

Raziolo was not overrun during the [War of the Dark Rift](<../../../../events/1500s/war-of-the-dark-rift.md>), was the last of the Apporian provinces to join the [Chardonian Empire](<../chardonian-empire.md>), and still retains an independent, autonomous streak.
