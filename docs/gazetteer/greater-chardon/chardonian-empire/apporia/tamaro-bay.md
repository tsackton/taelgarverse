# Tamaro Bay
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>

Tamaro Bay is a bay on the western coast of [the Apporian Peninsula](<apporia.md>). It is best known in folk song: the [Ballad of the Jade Garden](<../../../../primary-sources/songs/ballad-of-the-jade-garden.md>) places a legendary kelp-forest “jade garden” *a hundred leagues west o’ Tamaro Bay*. It is rumored to be a location where merfolk can ocassionally be seen, and their songs heard on the waves.
