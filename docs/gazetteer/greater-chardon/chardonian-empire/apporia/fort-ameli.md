# Fort Ameli
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>

    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } Located in [Apporia](<apporia.md>)  
</div>


Fort Ameli is a [Chardonian](<../chardonian-empire.md>) fort that guards the entrance to the [Old Chardon Canal](<old-chardon-canal.md>). It lies on the canal, about 10 miles inland from southern entrance, where the broad tidal estuary around [Pergia](<pergia.md>) narrows to the canal proper. The fort was originally constructed during the [Drankorian Era](<../../../../history/history-of-the-drankorian-empire.md>), though it has been expanded and rebuilt several times since. 

Ships making passage through the canal are required to dock here, pay tolls, and take on a windcaller pilot.
