# The Riftstone Gorge
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } A gorge in the [Chataan Mountains](<../../chataan-mountains.md>), [Greater Chardon](<../../greater-chardon.md>)  
</div>


Riftstone Gorge is a deep, broken canyon system cut into the dry northern flank of the [Chataan Mountains](<../../chataan-mountains.md>), beneath the towering bulk of [Thundercrown](<thundercrown.md>). The wide central canyon is flanked by a maze of cliffs, shelves, slot-canyons, and narrow riparian bottoms. Most of the year it is a land of dust, wind, pale stone, and sparse hardy woodland, but after rains it briefly greens with grasses, herbs, and sudden wildflowers. The gorge is harsh, mineral-rich, and strategically important: difficult to cross, dangerous to travel, but full of hidden seeps, mine cuts, and old roads clinging to the walls.

The Riftstone Gorge was the site of a major Chardonian defeat, the [Riftstone Gorge Disaster](<../../../../campaigns/dunmari-frontier-campaign/letters-and-notes/reports-on-chalyte-in-alta-tonaro.md#report-of-the-riftstone-gorge-disaster>). The gorge remains a byword for the dangers of the deep chalyte reserves in the Chataans.
