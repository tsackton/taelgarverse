# The Tower of the Colossus
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } A ruined tower in the [Garamjala Desert](<./garamjala-desert.md>), the [Desolation of Cha'mutte](<../desolation-of-cha-mutte.md>), the [Drankorian Hinterland](<../drankorian-hinterland.md>)  
</div>


The Tower of the Colossus is a half‑buried white marble tower rising from the sands of the [Garamjala Plateau](<./garamjala-plateau.md>). Its upper levels are open to wind and sun, while the lower levels have long been choked with drifting sand. 


In DR 1749 the [Dunmar Fellowship](<../../../people/pcs/dunmar-fellowship/dunmar-fellowship.md>) entered the tower, confronted a colossal ancient construct trapped within, and destroyed it. Beneath the sand they discovered sealed chambers and a hidden archive, carrying away the [Tower of the Colossus Treasure](<../../../campaigns/dunmari-frontier-campaign/hoards/tower-of-the-colossus-treasure.md>).
