# University of the Blessed Waters
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } A ruined university in [Drankor](<./drankor.md>)  
</div>


The University of the Blessed Waters, also called the Thalorian, was one of the three great universities of [Drankor](<./drankor.md>) in the last centuries of the Drankorian Empire. It stood southeast of the [Temple of the Eight Divines](<./temple-of-the-eight-divines.md>), amid gardens and lecture halls that once housed a Faculty of Natural History and other schools.

In the present ruins, the university is overgrown and unstable. Its approach leads through jungle‑choked streets to a walled garden marked by two guardian statues, still animated by lingering magic.
