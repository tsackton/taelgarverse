# Apollyon's Temple
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } A ruined temple in [Drankor](<./drankor.md>)  
</div>


Apollyon's Temple is a vast structure in [Drankor](<./drankor.md>), raised during Apollyon’s reign, as monument and focus for his quest to become a god. The main hall is carved with iconography of worshippers in reverence, with a central altar and side chapels that once displayed the regalia and relics of his cult. After Apollyon’s fall the temple became a prison to contain him, magically sealed in the jungle‑covered city. 


In DR 1749 the [Dunmar Fellowship](<../../../people/pcs/dunmar-fellowship/dunmar-fellowship.md>) forced entry into the undercrypt, pursued Apollyon into his inner sanctum, and defeated him there.
