# Apollyon's Tower
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } A tower on the [Circular Island](<./circular-island.md>)  
</div>


Apollyon's Tower is a black marble spire on the coast of the [Circular Island](<./circular-island.md>), once a stronghold and retreat of the Drankorian emperor [Apollyon](<../../people/historical-figures/drankorian-emperors/apollyon.md>), a place of dark study and craft. The tower itself is almost 250 feet tall, with a main section that rises 140 feet from the ground, and battlements and a tower extension that rise another 100 feet, capped with a platform open to the sky. After the [Fall of Drankor](<../../events/1000s/1059/fall-of-drankor.md>), the tower stood abandoned and warded, looming over the island’s storm‑lashed shore. 


In DR 1749 the [Dunmar Fellowship](<../../people/pcs/dunmar-fellowship/dunmar-fellowship.md>) assaulted the ruin, uncovering its forge, libraries, and throne room, and carrying away the treasures now catalogued as [Apollyon's Tower Treasure](<../../campaigns/dunmari-frontier-campaign/hoards/apollyon-s-tower-treasure.md>).
