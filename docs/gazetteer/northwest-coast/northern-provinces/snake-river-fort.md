# The Snake River Fort
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>

A small Chardonian fort at a [Snake River](<../../major-rivers/chasa-nahadi-watershed/snake-river.md>) crossing east of [Voltara](<voltara/voltara.md>), marking the edge of settled territory before the open plains. Travelers receive warnings here about raids and missing patrols.
