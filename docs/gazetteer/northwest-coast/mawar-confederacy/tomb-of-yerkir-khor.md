# The Tomb of Yerkir-Khor
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } An ancient earth tomb in the [Mawar Confederacy](<./mawar-confederacy.md>), the [Mawakel Peninsula](<./mawakel-peninsula.md>), the [Northwest Coast](<../northwest-coast.md>)  
</div>


The Tomb of Yerkir-Khor is an ancient earth-themed tomb in the inland forest of the [Mawar Confederacy](<./mawar-confederacy.md>), several days up the [Mirmir](<../rivers/mirmir.md>) from [Hamri](<./hamri.md>). 

Its entrance inscription, written in Terran, reads: "Here lies [Yerkir-khor](<../../../people/other-nonhumans/yerkir-khor.md>) the Undaunted, Prince of Earth, Lord of the Rock Below, and Guardian of the Crystal Skies." The tomb or monument appears to honor Yerkir-khor as an earth-lord tied to caverns, mountains, canals, mining, farming, and trade with dwarves.

In DR 1747, the [Adventurers of Mawar](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/adventurers-of-mawar.md>) [entered the tomb](<../../../campaigns/mawar-adventures/episodes/mawar-adventures-episode-03.md>) on the trail of [Hiyasa](<../../../people/mawarans/hiyasa.md>), destroyed (or, at least, temporarily disabled) several traps and guardians, and looted most of the remaining treasure.
