# Tzingola
:speaker:{ .middle } *(tseen-GOH-lah)*  
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } A salt marsh in the [Mawakel Peninsula](<./mawakel-peninsula.md>), the [Northwest Coast](<../northwest-coast.md>)  
</div>


Tzingola, or the Tzingola Marshes, is the local lizardfolk name for the extensive salt marshes along the western edge of the [Mawakel Peninsula](<./mawakel-peninsula.md>), north of [Hamri](<./hamri.md>). In Hamri, the same marshes are often just called the North Marshes. 

The lizardfolk village of [Eskorola](<./eskorola.md>) stands near the southern end of Tzingola, where tidal channels, marsh grass, barrier islands, oyster farms, and sheltered pools shape daily life. Most travel through the marsh is by swimming, canoe, or narrow elevated walkways between huts and communal islands.

In DR 1747, a fire cultist used a hidden lair in the marsh north of [Eskorola](<./eskorola.md>) to hold the dragon turtle [Chaessenth](<../../../people/other-nonhumans/chaessenth.md>) under magical control, using him to demand gatza from the local lizardfolk. [Ryu](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/ryu.md>), [Nerissa](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/nerissa.md>), and [Ander](<../../../people/pcs/mawar/ander.md>) entered the marsh, fought the cultist's servants, and freed Chaessenth.
