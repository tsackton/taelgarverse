# Thordûn
:speaker:{ .middle } *(THOR-doon)*  
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>

Thordûn (the Western Door), is the largest and most elaborate of the western gates to to the dwarven kingdom of [Am'khazar](<./am-khazar.md>), though not the most frequently used. It is located on the edge of the [Sentinels](<../../sentinel-range.md>), north of the [Crimson Forest](<../crimson-forest.md>), in the [Highveil Forest](<../highveil-forest.md>).
