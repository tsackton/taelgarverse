# Hralgar's Palace
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } A ruined palace in the [Sentinel Range](<../sentinel-range.md>)  
</div>


Hralgar's Palace is a ruined mountain palace in the [Sentinels](<../sentinel-range.md>), once belonging to the storm giant [Hralgar](<../../people/giants/hralgar.md>). In the current day, the palace is abandoned, its halls broken and weather-worn. Travelers speak of strange, dreamlike hazards in its depths.


In DR 1748, the [Dunmar Fellowship](<../../people/pcs/dunmar-fellowship/dunmar-fellowship.md>) [ventured to the palace](<../../campaigns/dunmari-frontier-campaign/session-notes/session-73-dufr.md>) and recovered a [phasing stone](<../../campaigns/dunmari-frontier-campaign/treasure/hralgar-s-phasing-stone.md>) from Hralgar’s library—an artifact later used to restore the skyship [Vindristjarna](<../../things/ships/vindristjarna.md>). They also recorded notes on ancient skyships and [recovered](<../../campaigns/dunmari-frontier-campaign/hoards/hralgar-s-palace-treasure.md>) several giant-sized books from the library.
