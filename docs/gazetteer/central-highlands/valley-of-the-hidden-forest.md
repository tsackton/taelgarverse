# The Valley of the Hidden Forest
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>

</div>


The Valley of the Hidden Forest is a rumored hidden valley in the [Sentinels](<../sentinel-range.md>), which may or may not exist. 


The Valley of the Hidden Forest, called *Naun Tarvanos* in Elvish, was a pre-Great-War refuge, part of the legend of [Rai](<../../people/pcs/great-war/rai.md>) and rumored to be a place where [Rai](<../../people/pcs/great-war/rai.md>) trained. The name [was heard](<../../campaigns/dunmari-frontier-campaign/session-notes/session-113-dufr.md>) by the [Dunmar Fellowship](<../../people/pcs/dunmar-fellowship/dunmar-fellowship.md>) in a vision of [Rai](<../../people/pcs/great-war/rai.md>), and his familiar [Dimitaur](<../../people/pcs/great-war/companions/dimitaur.md>) associated it with a distant place where [Rai](<../../people/pcs/great-war/rai.md>) trained and learned.
