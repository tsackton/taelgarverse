# The Kilrath Caves
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } A cave system in the [Stonewolds](<./stonewolds.md>), [Carlinshire](<./carlinshire.md>), [Addermarch](<./addermarch.md>), [Greater Sembara](<../greater-sembara.md>)  
</div>


Northwest of [Roscombe](<./roscombe.md>), the Kilrath Caves wind beneath an [ancient ring of stones](<./kilrath-standing-stones.md>). Recent explorations uncovered a fey-tainted pool, a shadow-haunted side passage, and a cockatrice brood laired around a defunct crystal gate.
