# Highmoor
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } A moor in [Carlinshire](<carlinshire.md>), [Addermarch](<addermarch.md>), [Greater Sembara](<../greater-sembara.md>)  
</div>


The Highmoor is a band of rolling downs, rocky ridges, scrub, and broken limestone outcrops north and east of [Roscombe](<roscombe.md>), between the settled approaches of [Carlinshire](<carlinshire.md>) and the higher ground of [Westcliff](<westcliff.md>). It is less wooded, poorer-soiled, and more thinly settled than the [Stonewolds](<stonewolds.md>) to the west. Its people are mostly shepherds, cattle-herders, charcoal burners, and crofters living in tiny upland villages.

Rough cart tracks cross the moor between [Roscombe](<roscombe.md>), [Brantor](<brantor.md>), and scattered highland settlements, but travel is slow and exposed. The moor supports pockets of woodland, sheltered dales, small springs, summer pastures, and isolated caves or hollows.

## Locations

[Haldrenn](<haldrenn.md>) is a remote Highmoor village known for cattle, charcoal, and wooden ridge-wards tied to local fey traditions.

[Nightsong Roots](<nightsong-roots.md>) is a fog-filled hollow east of [Haldrenn](<haldrenn.md>), where the roots of a fallen tree form a portal to the [Feywild](<../../../cosmology/feywild.md>).

A cave system in northern Highmoor was turned into a trap-filled lair by goblins in DR 1715, until the [lair was cleared](<../../../campaigns/addermarch-campaign/addermarch-session-05.md>) by the [Addermarch Mercenaries](<../../../people/pcs/addermarch/addermarch-mercenaries.md>) after the goblins captured [Colette du Bois](<../../../people/addermarians/colette-du-bois.md>) and stole an alchemical recipe sought by [Caradoc](<../../../people/addermarians/caradoc.md>).
