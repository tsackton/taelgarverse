# Serethwyn Tor

Serethwyn Tor is an old hill-fort in western [Carlinshire](<carlinshire.md>), built on a rocky rise of the [Westcliff](<westcliff.md>) overlooking the [Highmoor](<highmoor.md>). While its full history is obscure, it likely served as a major garrison and forward command post for the armies of [Avatus](<../../../people/historical-figures/avatus.md>) during the [Serpentine Wars](<../../../events/1300s/serpentine-wars.md>). The site was deliberately dismantled and sealed after Addermarch's victory by the [Rangers](<../../../groups/rangers.md>), and commanded to be left undisturbed. In the early DR 1700s, it is occasionally patrolled by Rangers to ensure it remains abandoned and sealed.

## Ruins and Mines

The ruined fort is reached by an old road climbing out of the Highmoor. A warning marker, written in Sembaran, Elvish, and Stoneborn, marks the site as dangerous and sealed by order of the [Rangers](<../../../groups/rangers.md>). The surface ruins preserve signs of ancient fire damage, Avatus-era military gear, and the remains of fighting in and around the barracks and keep.

Beneath the fort is an old [serpentine](<../../../things/materials/serpentine.md>) mine complex. The upper mine was blocked by a massive intentional cave-in, but ventilation shafts uncovered by severe storms in the spring of DR 1715 lead deeper into the hill.
