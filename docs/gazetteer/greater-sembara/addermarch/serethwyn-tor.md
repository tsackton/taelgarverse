# Serethwyn Tor
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } A ruin in [Carlinshire](<./carlinshire.md>), [Addermarch](<./addermarch.md>), [Greater Sembara](<../greater-sembara.md>)  
</div>


Serethwyn Tor is an old hill‑fort in western [Carlinshire](<./carlinshire.md>), built on a rocky rise of the [Westcliff](<./westcliff.md>) overlooking the [Highmoor](<./highmoor.md>). While its full history is obscure, it likely served as a major garrison and forward command post for the armies of the [Avatus](<../../../people/historical-figures/avatus.md>) during his wars with Addermarch. The site was deliberately dismantled and sealed after Addermarch's victory by [The Rangers](<../../../groups/the-rangers.md>), and commanded to be left undisturbed. In the early DR 1700s, it is occasionally patrolled by Rangers to ensure it remains abandoned and sealed.
