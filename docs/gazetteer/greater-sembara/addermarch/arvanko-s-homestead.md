# Arvanko's Homestead
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } A homestead in [Carlinshire](<carlinshire.md>), [Addermarch](<addermarch.md>), [Greater Sembara](<../greater-sembara.md>)  
</div>


The stoneborn [ranger](<../../../groups/rangers.md>) [Arvanko](<../../../people/other-nonhumans/arvanko.md>) lives in an isolated homestead southeast of [Roscombe](<roscombe.md>), on the eaves of the [Torvaine Forest](<torvaine-forest.md>). 

His home is notable for the still-growing, hand-carved stone monument, shaped from a hillside by [Arvanko](<../../../people/other-nonhumans/arvanko.md>), dedicated to the [Rangers](<../../../groups/rangers.md>) and others who died in western [Addermarch](<addermarch.md>). The monument is a low circular stone wall, on which many names are carved, dating to the early days of the Rangers in the [wars against Avatus](<../../../events/1300s/serpentine-wars.md>). There is space at the four cardinal points for large statues, currently occupied by uncarved standing stones with rough etchings of a fox, a tree, a watchtower, and a staff.
