# Westcliff
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } A line of hills in the [Mostreve Hills](<../mostreve-hills.md>), [Greater Sembara](<../greater-sembara.md>)  
</div>


Westcliff is the long escarpment that bounds the western edge of the [Mostreve Hills](<../mostreve-hills.md>), a line of broken crags and stepped ledges. The cliffs are a major barrier to travel, with no practical routes for carts to pass west to the [Maseau Gap](<../maseau-gap.md>) beyond. Only a few clefts and engineered switchbacks permit passage of sure footed travelers to the plains beyond.

The highest peaks of the [Mostreve Hills](<../mostreve-hills.md>) can be found here.
