# The Kilrath Standing Stones
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>

</div>


The Kilrath Standing Stones are a ring of weather‑worn megaliths set on a low hill in the [Stonewolds](<./stonewolds.md>) of western [Carlinshire](<./carlinshire.md>). Lichens and knife‑marks cover the stones; in mist the circle seems larger than it is, and birds avoid alighting within. Local tales insist the hill is a meeting place for old powers, or a warning of dangers from the [Feywild](<../../../cosmology/feywild.md>). The surviving uprights describe an uneven ring with a toppled lintel near the east. Traces of ancient carving, mostly eroded, suggest an ancient origin, predating the Drankorian Empire.
