# The Darkwood Keep
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } A keep in [Addermarch](<./addermarch.md>), [Greater Sembara](<../greater-sembara.md>)  
</div>


Darkwood Keep is a frontier fortress on the southern edge of [Addermarch](<./addermarch.md>), raised to watch the forest eaves of the [Darkwood](<./darkwood.md>) and the approaches from the [Plaguelands](<../../upper-istaros/plaguelands.md>). The road from [Adderfell](<./adderfell.md>) passes through [Tygate](<./tygate.md>) before turning southwest to supply the keep and the smaller works that guard the border.
