# The Maerwyn Mountains
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } A mountain range in the [Mostreve Hills](<mostreve-hills.md>), [Greater Sembara](<greater-sembara.md>)  
</div>


The Maerwyn Mountains are a line of high peaks in the [Mostreve Hills](<mostreve-hills.md>), running northwest along the border between [Addermarch](<addermarch/addermarch.md>) and [Tyrwingha](<tyrwingha/tyrwingha.md>). They rise from the rough country near the [Valenfray Hills](<addermarch/valenfray-hills.md>) and trend north toward the [Enst](<rivers/wistel-enst-watershed/enst.md>) river valley; and are highest in the south near the headwaters of the [Branth](<rivers/wistel-enst-watershed/branth.md>).
