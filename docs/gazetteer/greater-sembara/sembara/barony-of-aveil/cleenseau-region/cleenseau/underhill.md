# Underhill
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } A neighborhood in [Cleenseau](<./cleenseau.md>), the [Manor of Cleenseau](<../manor-of-cleenseau.md>), the [Barony of Aveil](<../../barony-of-aveil.md>), [Sembara](<../../../sembara.md>), [Greater Sembara](<../../../../greater-sembara.md>)  
</div>


A neighborhood in Cleenseau built at the base of the small hill on which Essford Manor sits. It is a relatively poor neighborhood, characterized by dense tenements and longer walks access to clean water than elsewhere in Cleenseau.
