# Embry

Embry is the capital city of [Sembara](<../sembara.md>), perched on a bluff above the [Semb](<../../rivers/semb-watershed/semb.md>). It is the seat of the royal court, and a busy river port where grain and wool from upriver are unloaded from barges and sold on to [Tollender](<../../tollen/tollen.md>) and [halfling](<../../../../creatures/species/halflings.md>) merchants bound for [Tollen](<../../tollen/tollen.md>) and the wider [Green Sea](<../../../green-sea.md>). 

For all its size and power, Embry remains a rural-facing and pastoral city. Temples to the [Eight Divines](<../../../../gods-and-religions/religions/mos-numena/mos-numena.md>) are prominent, but the wealth of the court and nobility rests on the harvests and sheep from their agricultural holdings in the hinterlands.
