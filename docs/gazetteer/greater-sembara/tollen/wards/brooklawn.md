# Brooklawn
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } A ward in the [Free City of Tollen](<../tollen.md>), the [Western Green Sea](<../../../western-green-sea/western-green-sea.md>)  
</div>


Brooklawn is a ward of [Tollen](<../tollen.md>) that spreads across both sides of the [Little River](<../../rivers/volta-watershed/little-river.md>). This area is swampy and prone to flooding, with a long history of industrial use for tanning, leather-working, and dye-works. 

For most of the city's history, Brooklawn stood outside the walls, and was a separate suburb, protected in Drankorian times by the fort on [Haurhill](<./haurhill.md>). When the walls were expanded west in the years after the [Great War](<../../../../events/1500s/great-war.md>), the old suburb of Brooklawn was split into [Brooklawn Inner](<./brooklawn-inner.md>) and [Brooklawn Outer](<./brooklawn-outer.md>). 

The [Little River](<../../rivers/volta-watershed/little-river.md>) now runs in a stone-lined channel through [Brooklawn Inner](<./brooklawn-inner.md>).
