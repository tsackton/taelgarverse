# Southbridge
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } A ward in the [Free City of Tollen](<../tollen.md>), the [Western Green Sea](<../../../western-green-sea/western-green-sea.md>)  
</div>


Southbridge is the oldest and most prosperous ward on the south bank of the [Volta](<../../rivers/volta-watershed/volta.md>), across the river from [Bridgeward](<./bridgeward.md>), spreading from the south end of the [Tollen Bridge](<../places/tollen-bridge.md>). Though once swampy ground, this area was drained and reclaimed in Drankorian times; the ancient Drankorian road south meets the [Tollen Bridge](<../places/tollen-bridge.md>) here. Downriver of the [Tollen Bridge](<../places/tollen-bridge.md>), Southbridge is also part of the sea-port, though it sees less deepwater traffic and has a less developed waterfront than [Battery](<./battery.md>). 

Southbridge is the center of the Tollen tattoo industry, with many of the most prosperous and skilled tattoo artists, especially those who incorporate magical ink into their work, located along [Inkspell Lane](<../places/inkspell-lane.md>).
