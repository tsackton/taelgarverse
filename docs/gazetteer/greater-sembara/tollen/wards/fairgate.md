# Fairgate
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } A ward in the [Free City of Tollen](<../tollen.md>), the [Western Green Sea](<../../../western-green-sea/western-green-sea.md>)  
</div>


Fairgate is one of Tollen’s western approaches, divided today into [Fairgate Inner](<fairgate-inner.md>) (within the walls) and [Fairgate Outer](<fairgate-outer.md>) (beyond them). 

Fairgate was established as a ward of Tollen when the walls were expanded west to enclose the ancient fort on [Haurhill](<haurhill.md>). At the time, open fields lay to the north, which was kept as a space for markets. Over time, as the [Fairgrounds](<../places/fairgrounds-tollen.md>) were established by halfling caravans, village-like sprawl grew up outside the walls, forming [Fairgate Outer](<fairgate-outer.md>), with the original Fairgate ward renamed [Fairgate Inner](<fairgate-inner.md>).
