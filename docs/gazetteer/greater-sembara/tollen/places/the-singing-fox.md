# The Singing Fox

A cozy halfling tavern in [Fairgate Outer](<../wards/fairgate-outer.md>), run by [Chenna Goodbarrel](<../../../../people/halflings/chenna-goodbarrel.md>) and her wife [Harriet](<../../../../people/halflings/harriet-goodbarrel.md>). Harriet is a good singer and The Singing Fox gets a decent crowd (mostly locals and passing halflings) when she is performing.
