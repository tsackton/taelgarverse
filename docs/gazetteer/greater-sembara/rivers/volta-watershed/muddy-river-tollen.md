# The Muddy River
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } A river in the [Free City of Tollen](<../../tollen/tollen.md>), the [Western Green Sea](<../../../western-green-sea/western-green-sea.md>)  
    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } Located in the [Volta Watershed](<./volta-watershed.md>), [Greater Sembara](<../../greater-sembara.md>)  
</div>


A swampy tributary of the Volta that forms the boundary between [Fenslane](<../../tollen/wards/fenslane.md>) and [Tideswell](<../../tollen/wards/tideswell.md>) in [Tollen](<../../tollen/tollen.md>). Though now channeled, it often floods it banks, especially at high tide.
