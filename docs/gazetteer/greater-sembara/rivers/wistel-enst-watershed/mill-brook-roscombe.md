# The Mill Brook (Roscombe)

The Mill Brook is a rushing brook that flows past [Roscombe](<../../addermarch/roscombe.md>) from the northwest, turning south to pass under the road from [Valcroix](<../../addermarch/valcroix.md>) about a quarter‑mile from the town’s main gates, before eventually joining the [Velan](<./velan.md>).
