# The Wistel–Enst Watershed
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } A watershed in [Greater Sembara](<../../greater-sembara.md>)  
</div>


The Wistel–Enst watershed drains much of southern and central [Sembara](<../../sembara/sembara.md>), carrying upland waters from the [Sentinels](<../../../sentinel-range.md>) and the [Mostreve Hills](<../../mostreve-hills.md>) to the [Western Gulf](<../../western-gulf.md>). It consists of three closely linked systems:

- Wistel system: The [Wistel](<./wistel.md>) rises in the foothills of the Sentinels and flows east to the marshlands of [Ozabal Swamp](<../../ozabal.md>) on the coast. A major tributary is the [Bollin](<./bollin.md>) from the north (joining near [Ainwick](<../../sembara/barony-of-ainwick/ainwick.md>)). The Wistel meets and mingles with the lower [Enst](<./enst.md>) through the Ozabal wetlands before the combined waters reach the sea.

- Enst system: The [Enst](<./enst.md>) is a cold, rocky river forming much of Sembara’s southern frontier. Above [Cleenseau](<../../sembara/barony-of-aveil/cleenseau-region/cleenseau/cleenseau.md>) it splits into the “Three Fingers”: [Enst (North Fork)](<./enst-north-fork.md>), [Enst (Middle Fork)](<./enst-middle-fork.md>), and [Enst (South Fork)](<./enst-south-fork.md>), which rejoin near Lysandale. Key Enst tributaries include the [Auberonne](<./auberonne.md>) (forming [Lake Rin](<../../sembara/barony-of-aveil/lake-rin.md>) at its confluence and fed by the [Leandre](<./leandre.md>) and the [Cranmère](<./cranmere.md>)) and the [Clavert](<./clavert.md>) flowing north off [Westcliff](<../../addermarch/westcliff.md>).

- Aure system: The [Aure](<./aure.md>) rises in the [Darkwood](<../../addermarch/darkwood.md>) and runs north through [Addermarch](<../../addermarch/addermarch.md>) to join the Enst near the Ozabal lowlands. North of [Adderfell](<../../addermarch/adderfell.md>) it gathers the [Velan](<./velan.md>) from the west—which itself receives the [Umber](<./umber.md>) near [Valcroix](<../../addermarch/valcroix.md>)—and the short upland [Branth](<./branth.md>) from the east. [Adderfell](<../../addermarch/adderfell.md>) sits at the height of navigation on the Aure, on a small lake. 

## Character and Use 
The [Wistel](<./wistel.md>) supports heavy barge and river traffic on its lower reaches, primarily along the stretch between [Ainwick](<../../sembara/barony-of-ainwick/ainwick.md>) and [Wisford](<../../sembara/heartlands/wisford.md>).  There are several bridges, including major ones at [Ainwick](<../../sembara/barony-of-ainwick/ainwick.md>), [Gowerbourne](<../../sembara/heartlands/gowerbourne.md>), and [Wisford](<../../sembara/heartlands/wisford.md>) as well as some smaller ones between [Gowerbourne](<../../sembara/heartlands/gowerbourne.md>) and [Wisford](<../../sembara/heartlands/wisford.md>).

The [Enst](<./enst.md>), by contrast, is difficult to cross and forms a major barrier to movement: flooding, cold currents, and limited towpaths restrict navigation. Ferries (most notably at [Rinburg](<../../sembara/barony-of-aveil/rinburg.md>)) and a small number of bridges—most notably at [Cleenseau](<../../sembara/barony-of-aveil/cleenseau-region/cleenseau/cleenseau.md>) and [Eskbridge](<../../sembara/heartlands/eskbridge.md>)—manage crossings where the banks allow.

The Aure supports regular riverboat traffic between [Adderfell](<../../addermarch/adderfell.md>) and the [Enst](<./enst.md>).

Once the Enst and the Wistel join, the river becomes a marshy delta with many meandering paths as it moves through [Ozabal Swamp](<../../ozabal.md>), and it is not generally navigated further east of Wisford (on the Wistel) and Eskbridge (on the Enst).
