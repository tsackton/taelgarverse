# The Branth
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } A river in [Addermarch](<../../addermarch/addermarch.md>), [Greater Sembara](<../../greater-sembara.md>)  
    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } Located in the [Wistel–Enst Watershed](<wistel-enst-watershed.md>), [Greater Sembara](<../../greater-sembara.md>)  
</div>


The Branth is a short upland river flowing west from the ridge country between [Addermarch](<../../addermarch/addermarch.md>) and [Tyrwingha](<../../tyrwingha/tyrwingha.md>) to join the [Aure](<aure.md>) north of [Adderfell](<../../addermarch/adderfell.md>). Its narrow valley is dry in places with thin soils but supports significant pasture; the Branth runs swiftly in spring and dwindles to a manageable flow by late summer.
