# Mournebrook
>[!info]+ Information  
>   
>> A brook in [Addermarch](<../../addermarch/addermarch.md>)
>> Located in the [Wistel–Enst Watershed](<wistel-enst-watershed.md>)



The Mournebrook flows northeast out of the [Shadowmere](<../../addermarch/shadowmere.md>)—a haunted swamp on the western edge of the [Darkwood](<../../addermarch/darkwood.md>)—to join the [Aure](<aure.md>) a little north of where that river exits the forest. In the southern [Addermarian](<../../addermarch/addermarch.md>) frontier it is treated as a boundary as much as a watercourse, and few willingly cross it.
