# The Cambril
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } A river in [Ardlas](<../../ardlas.md>), [Greater Sembara](<../../greater-sembara.md>)  
    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } Located in the [Wistel–Enst Watershed](<wistel-enst-watershed.md>), [Greater Sembara](<../../greater-sembara.md>)  
</div>




The Cambril is a fast flowing tributary of the [Bollin](<bollin.md>) that runs through gorges and rocky cascades before joining the river north of the [Aine Hills](<../../sembara/aine-hills.md>). It forms the southern boundary of [Ardlas](<../../ardlas.md>).
