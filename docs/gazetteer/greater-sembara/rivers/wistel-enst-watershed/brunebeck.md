# The Brunebeck
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } A river in the [Duchy of Wisford](<../../sembara/heartlands/duchy-of-wisford.md>), [Sembara](<../../sembara/sembara.md>)  
    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } Located in the [Wistel–Enst Watershed](<./wistel-enst-watershed.md>)  
</div>


The Brunebeck is a tributary of the [Wistel](<./wistel.md>), joining the river at [Wisford](<../../sembara/heartlands/wisford.md>). It arises near the Enst west and north of Eskbridge, and then follows the line of the [Tyrwinghan Road](<../../roads/tyrwinghan-road.md>) until it joins the Wistel at Wisford.
