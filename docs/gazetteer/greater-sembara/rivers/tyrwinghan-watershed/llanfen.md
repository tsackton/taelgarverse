# The Llanfen
:speaker:{ .middle } *(HLAN-ven)*  
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } A river in [Tyrwingha](<../../tyrwingha/tyrwingha.md>), [Greater Sembara](<../../greater-sembara.md>)  
</div>


The Llanfen is the easternmost of the principal rivers of [Tyrwingha](<../../tyrwingha/tyrwingha.md>), flowing down from the [Mostreve Hills](<../../mostreve-hills.md>) to the [Western Gulf](<../../western-gulf.md>). Its lower reaches lie on the edge of the marshlands of [Latazaro](<../../latazaro.md>).
