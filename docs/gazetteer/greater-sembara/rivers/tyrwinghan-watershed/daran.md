The Daran is a small river of [Tyrwingha](<../../tyrwingha/tyrwingha.md>). Rising in the [Mostreve Hills](<../../mostreve-hills.md>), it flows north to join the [Aben](<./aben.md>) upstream of [Tafolwern](<../../tyrwingha/tafolwern.md>).
