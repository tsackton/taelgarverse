# The Vilna
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } A river in [Zimkova](<../../zimkova/zimkova.md>), [Greater Sembara](<../../greater-sembara.md>)  
    :octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } Located in the [Teft Watershed](<./teft-watershed.md>), [Greater Sembara](<../../greater-sembara.md>)  
</div>


A north-flowing mountain river in the central foothills of the [Sentinels](<../../../sentinel-range.md>). The Vilna joins the [Teft](<./teft.md>) a few miles west of the confluence with the south-flowing [Bērze](<./berze.md>), at the head of the [Great Chasm](<../../zimkova/great-chasm.md>), where the combined waters plunge almost three hundred feet into the giant-carved channel along the chasm's north side.
