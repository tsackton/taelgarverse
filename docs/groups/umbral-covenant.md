# The Umbral Covenant
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-
   :octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } An army  
</div>


The Umbral Covenant is the name used in Chardonian and Apporian records for the shadow host that rose in the interior of [Apporia](<../gazetteer/greater-chardon/chardonian-empire/apporia/apporia.md>) during the [War of the Dark Rift](<../events/1500s/war-of-the-dark-rift.md>). Over thirteen years of war (DR 1584–1597), the Covenant conquered much of northern Apporia, disrupted the [Old Chardon Canal](<../gazetteer/greater-chardon/chardonian-empire/apporia/old-chardon-canal.md>) trade, and brought ruin to cities along the [Gulf of Chardon](<../gazetteer/greater-chardon/gulf-of-chardon.md>).

The war ended in DR 1597 when the Covenant launched a Shadow Armada against [Chardon](<../gazetteer/greater-chardon/chardonian-empire/chardon/chardon.md>) and was destroyed by the Chardonian navy. 

The exact nature of the Umbral Covenant remains unclear.
