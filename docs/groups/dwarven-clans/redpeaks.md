The Redpeaks are a dwarven clan, prominent in [Darba](<../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/realms/dunmar/coastal-dunmar/darba/darba.md>), with ties to [Tharn Todor](<../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/realms/nardith/tharn-todor.md>) and [Nardith](<../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/realms/nardith/nardith.md>). The Redpeaks were among the clans with connections to the [dwarven outpost near Raven's Hold](<../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/hara-basin/dwarven-outpost-raven-s-hold.md>) before the [Great War](<../../events/1500s/great-war.md>); since they fled with many others to [Nardith](<../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/realms/nardith/nardith.md>), they have spread west with trade connections, and now maintain estates in the Darba's dwarven quarter.
