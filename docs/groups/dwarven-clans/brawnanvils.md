# The Brawnanvil Clan

The Brawnanvils are a moderately prominent dwarven clan of [Tharn Todor](<../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/realms/nardith/tharn-todor.md>), with roots in the lost kingdom of [Ardith](<../../gazetteer/central-highlands/dwarven-kingdoms/ardith.md>). Before and during the [Great War](<../../events/1500s/great-war.md>), the clan had a major presence at the [dwarven outpost near Raven's Hold](<../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/hara-basin/dwarven-outpost-raven-s-hold.md>), an iron mine, forge, and trading post linking dwarves from the southern [Sentinels](<../../gazetteer/sentinel-range.md>) with the Dunmari. 

By far the most famous member of the Brawnanvil Clan is [Riswynn](<../../people/pcs/dunmar-fellowship/riswynn.md>), companion of the [Dunmar Fellowship](<../../people/pcs/dunmar-fellowship/dunmar-fellowship.md>), hero of the [War of the Cloak](<../../events/1700s/war-of-the-cloak.md>), champion of the [Bahrazel](<../../gods-and-religions/gods/embodied-gods/bahrazel/bahrazel.md>), and the bearer of the [Crown of Purity](<../../things/artifacts-of-power/crown-of-purity.md>).
