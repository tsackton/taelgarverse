# Concordia Pyrae
>[!info]+ Information  
> An organization  
> 

The Concordia Pyrae was an ancient scholarly society of the [Drankorian Empire](<../../history/historical-realms/drankorian-empire.md>), devoted to the study of fire: pyromancy, planar lore, and the secrets of the [Elemental Plane of Fire](<../../cosmology/energy-realms/elemental-plane-of-fire.md>). Their best‑known work is the *[Compendium of Fire](<../../things/books/compendium-of-fire.md>)*, a fireproof encyclopedia compiled from the society’s research.

Surviving notes attributed to the Concordia Pyrae also include the [Records of the Concordia Pyrae](<../../things/books/records-of-the-concordia-pyrae.md>), which preserve fragments of the society’s history and its connection to the [Circular Island](<../../gazetteer/drankorian-hinterland/circular-island.md>).
