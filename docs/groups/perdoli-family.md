The Perdoli family were a wealthy Cymean merchant family, involved in the winemaking trade, based in [Vistalba](<../gazetteer/western-green-sea/cymea/vistalba.md>), in [Western Cymea](<../gazetteer/western-green-sea/realms/western-cymea.md>). In DR 1479, they built [Perdoli Manor](<../gazetteer/western-green-sea/cymea/perdoli-manor.md>) as a country villa in the hills. After the [Great War](<../events/1500s/great-war.md>), the family declined, lost much of its wealth, and eventually sold the villa.

They are no longer prominent in Cymea, though descendants likely survive, of course.
