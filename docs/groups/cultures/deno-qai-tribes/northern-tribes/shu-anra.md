# The Shu'anra

The Shu'anra are one of the [northern](<northern-tribes.md>) [Deno'qai](<../deno-qai.md>) tribes. Like other northern Deno'qai tribes, the Shu'anra are descended from the survivors of the [Great War](<../../../../events/1500s/great-war.md>). They survived the [Conclave War](<../../../../events/1500s/conclave-war.md>) and the [Blood Years](<../../../../events/1500s/blood-years.md>) in hiding, before emerging from the marginal lands in the low passes of the mountains, and now largely live in the forests east of the [Blackwater Fens](<../../../../gazetteer/northern-sentinels/blackwater-fens.md>) and west of the [Sentinels](<../../../../gazetteer/sentinel-range.md>). 

Less isolated than the [Ko'zula](<ko-zula.md>), a number of Shu'anra familes have drifted south over the years, some settling as far south as the [Elderwood](<../../../../gazetteer/central-highlands/elderwood.md>).
