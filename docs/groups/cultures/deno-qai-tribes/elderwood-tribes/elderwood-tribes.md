# The Elderwood Tribes of the Deno'qai

On the southern edge of [Ainumarya](<../../../../gazetteer/central-highlands/ainumarya.md>) stands the [Elderwood](<../../../../gazetteer/central-highlands/elderwood.md>), an ancient forest that avoided the worst destruction and death during the [Conclave War](<../../../../events/1500s/conclave-war.md>). This forested region, south of the [Chasa River Valley](<../../../../gazetteer/greater-chardon/chardonian-empire/chasa-river-valley/chasa-river-valley.md>), has been settled by [Deno'qai](<../deno-qai.md>) tribes for generations uncounted, many of whom continue to live traditional lives in the forest. 

## Settlement Patterns

Most of the Elderwood tribes settle in small villages nestled within the forest, building in and around natural clearings, rather than cutting extensively. A typical Elderwood village consists of a cluster of buildings in the forest, with a central gathering hall near a clearing, usually with a well. 

Each village will have a tanshi altar, often a coppiced tree or large shaped rock, that serves as a locus for ritual sacrifices. Usually, the local Godcaller will have a home near the altar; the bulk of the rest of the settlement is small cottages, spread out among the trees. 

The Deno'qai clear small plots for agricultural, usually on the outskirts of villages, focusing on shade-adapted crops that can thrive on small plots in the forest, as well as livestock, mostly pigs and chickens. 

## Politics and Culture

Villages are led by a local chief, usually elected by all adults of the village; the local chief is responsible for matters of justice within their village. The chiefs together form a tribal council, choosing one of their own as the over-chief of the tribe, though this is largely a ceremonial role. It is the tribal council as a whole that resolves disputes among villages and, when necessary, decides on matters of war. 
 
Separate from the chiefs, each village has a Godcaller who speaks for and interprets the will of the tanshi, and organizes sacrifices as needed.
