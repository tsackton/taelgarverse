# The De Brunes
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-
   :octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } A family  
</div>


The de Brunes are a wealthy mercantile family with roots in the [Enst](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/rivers/wistel-enst-watershed/enst.md>) river valley, and a primary manor just outside [Eskbridge](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/sembara/heartlands/eskbridge.md>). The family maintains outposts and connections along the Enst, including a longstanding presence in the [Cleenseau Region](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/sembara/barony-of-aveil/cleenseau-region/cleenseau-region.md>). In recent years, de Brune business in the Cleenseau region has been shaped by [Catherine de Brune](<../../people/sembarans/catherine-de-brune.md>)’s investments and trade ties.
