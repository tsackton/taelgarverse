# The de Brunes
>[!info]+ Information  
> A noble house  
> 

The de Brunes are a wealthy mercantile family with long roots in the [Enst](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/rivers/wistel-enst-watershed/enst.md>) river valley. Although [Eskbridge](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/sembara/heartlands/eskbridge.md>) is their primary center of power, the family maintains outposts and connections along the Enst, including a longstanding presence in the [Cleenseau Region](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/sembara/barony-of-aveil/cleenseau-region/cleenseau-region.md>).

In recent years, de Brune business in the Cleenseau region has been shaped by [Catherine de Brune](<../../people/sembarans/catherine-de-brune.md>)’s investments and trade ties.
