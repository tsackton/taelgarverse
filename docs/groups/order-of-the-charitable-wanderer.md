# The Order of the Charitable Wanderer
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-
   :octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } A religious order  
</div>




The Order of the Charitable Wanderer is a [Mos Numena](<../gods-and-religions/religions/mos-numena/mos-numena.md>) religious order dedicated to [The Wanderer](<../gods-and-religions/gods/incorporeal-gods/mos-numena-pantheon/the-wanderer.md>). Its oathsworn are known for walking the roads of Taelgar, practicing the Wanderer’s virtues of charity and hospitality, and offering protection to travelers in dangerous borderlands.
