# Mahaut's Miracle Players
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-
   :octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } An acting troupe  
</div>


Mahaut’s Miracle Players are a traveling troupe of performers, known for staging miracle plays and other road‑show entertainment. [Celyn](<../people/pcs/cleenseau/celyn.md>), later famous as one of the [Heroes of Cleenseau](<../people/pcs/cleenseau/heroes-of-cleenseau.md>), traveled with the troupe for a time in the early DR 1700s.
