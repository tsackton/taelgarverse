# The Radiant Alliance

The Radiant Alliance was the royal field army assembled by [Cece I](<../people/historical-figures/sembaran-royalty/cece-i.md>) in DR 1648 during the [Third Hobgoblin War](<../events/1600s/third-hobgoblin-war-sembara.md>). Formed by combining the Armies of the South and Mostreve and raising a special levy across the realm, the Alliance broke the hobgoblin advance and began the reconquest of southern Sembara.

In the first summer of its campaigning, the Radiant Alliance liberated [Rinburg](<../gazetteer/greater-sembara/sembara/barony-of-aveil/rinburg.md>) and [Cleenseau](<../gazetteer/greater-sembara/sembara/barony-of-aveil/cleenseau-region/cleenseau/cleenseau.md>) and broke the Siege of Dunfry. In the years that followed it continued to push the [Shattered Ice Clan](<hobgoblin-clans/shattered-ice-clan.md>) back toward the foothills of the [Sentinels](<../gazetteer/sentinel-range.md>), culminating in their decisive defeat in DR 1653.
