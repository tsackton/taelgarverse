# Windcallers
>[!info]+ Information  
> An organization  
> 

The Windcallers are chalyte‑empowered navigators in service to the [Chardonian Empire](<../../gazetteer/greater-chardon/chardonian-empire/chardonian-empire.md>). They pilot ships through the narrow, windless reaches of the [Old Chardon Canal](<../../gazetteer/greater-chardon/chardonian-empire/apporia/old-chardon-canal.md>) and are widely credited with making Chardon’s navy the most feared on the Endless Ocean.

The Windcallers are based in [Nevium](<../../gazetteer/greater-chardon/chardonian-empire/apporia/nevium.md>), near the canal mouth, and are treated as a special case within the chartered monopolies that regulate refined [chalyte](<../../things/materials/chalyte.md>): even when other buyers must negotiate through the [Hetaeri Magica](<./hetaeri-magica.md>), the Windcallers are guaranteed access to a fraction of refined production.
