# The Windcallers
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>

The Windcallers are chalyte‑empowered navigators in service to the [Chardonian Empire](<../../gazetteer/greater-chardon/chardonian-empire/chardonian-empire.md>). They pilot ships through the narrow, windless reaches of the [Old Chardon Canal](<../../gazetteer/greater-chardon/chardonian-empire/apporia/old-chardon-canal.md>) and are widely credited with making Chardon’s navy the most feared on the [Endless Ocean](<../../gazetteer/endless-ocean.md>).

The Windcallers are based in [Nevium](<../../gazetteer/greater-chardon/chardonian-empire/apporia/nevium.md>), near the canal mouth, and are treated as a special case within the chartered monopolies that regulate refined [chalyte](<../../things/materials/chalyte.md>), being guaranteed by charter the right to purchase a fraction of refined chalyte production at fixed rates. How, exactly, they use this [chalyte](<../../things/materials/chalyte.md>) is a mystery revealed only to initiates.
