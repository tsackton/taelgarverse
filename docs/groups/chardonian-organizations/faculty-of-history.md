The Faculty of History is one of the five graduate faculties of the [University of Chardon](<../../gazetteer/greater-chardon/chardonian-empire/chardon/university-of-chardon.md>). It is among the principal authorities in Chardon on the chronology and legacy of the [Drankorian Empire](<../../history/historical-realms/drankorian-empire.md>).
