# Hezguli
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-
   :octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } An [orc](<../creatures/species/orcs.md>) communal society  
</div>


The Hezguli are a community of [orcs](<../creatures/species/orcs.md>) who were born under the sway of [Thark](<../gods-and-religions/gods/embodied-gods/thark.md>)’s curse, but freed from it as children or young adults. They have learned—often with difficulty—to live as free orcs. They are most closely associated with General [Murook](<../people/orcs/murook.md>), widely regarded as their unofficial leader.
