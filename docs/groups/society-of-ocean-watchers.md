# The Society of Ocean Watchers
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-
   :octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } A group  
</div>


The Society of Ocean Watchers is a Mawaran organization associated with the old ocean-watching traditions of the [Mawar Confederacy](<../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/mawar-confederacy.md>): the people who keep watch for danger, wrecks, and stranger signs from the western sea. Ocean watchers are known as keepers of stories, warnings, and sea-lore, and some are said to commune with the waters of the [Endless Ocean](<../gazetteer/endless-ocean.md>).
