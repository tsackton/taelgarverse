# Mawaran Religion



The Mawar Confederacy and related cultural areas practice a regional form of [Mos Numena](<mos-numena.md>), oriented around saintly intercessors. While the [Eight Divines](<mos-numena.md>) remain the ultimate source of miracles from the [Divine Presence](<../../gods/high-gods/divine-presence.md>), Mawaran prayer commonly passes through saintly ancestors and heroes who bring the needs of the living before the Divines. This practice reflects a fusion of Mos Numena tradition with [northern intercessory folk religions](<../northern-folk-religions/northern-folk-religions.md>), as well as the influence of salt [lizardfolk](<../../../creatures/species/lizardfolk.md>) ritual practice involving [Gatza](<../../../things/materials/gatza.md>). 

[Guzo the Mariner](<../../gods/incorporeal-gods/mawaran-saints/guzo-the-mariner.md>) is an example of a Mawaran saint.
