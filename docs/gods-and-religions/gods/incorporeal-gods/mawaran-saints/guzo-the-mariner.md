# Guzo the Mariner

Guzo the Mariner, known as "the ancestor of voyages and the western horizon," is a Mawaran hero and saint associated with [Hamri](<../../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/hamri.md>), ocean voyages, and protection for those lost at sea. In Mawaran local religious practice, prayers to the [Eight Divines](<../../../religions/mos-numena/mos-numena.md>) often pass through Guzo and other heroic ancestors as saintly intercessors to the [Eight Divines](<../mos-numena-pantheon/mos-numena-pantheon.md>).
