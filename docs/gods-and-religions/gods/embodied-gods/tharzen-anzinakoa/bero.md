# Bero
:speaker:{ .middle } *(BEH-roh)*  

Bero is one of the four [Tharzen Anzinakoa](<tharzen-anzinakoa.md>) of the [lizardfolk](<../../../../creatures/species/lizardfolk.md>), usually seen as the guardian of parents and associated with abundance, flourishing, and the balance of the land and the people.  

Among many lizardfolk communities, particularly those in [Greater Sembara](<../../../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/greater-sembara.md>), Bero is associated with summer and the wetlands when they are bursting with life.
