# Negu
:speaker:{ .middle } *(EH-goo)*  

Negu is one of the four [Tharzen Anzinakoa](<./tharzen-anzinakoa.md>) of the [lizardfolk](<../../../../creatures/species/lizardfolk.md>), usually seen as the guardian of the ancestral spirits. 

Among many lizardfolk communities, particularly those in [Greater Sembara](<../../../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/greater-sembara.md>), Negu is associated with winter, and the long quiet when the swamps and wetlands lie dormant waiting, poised to erupt with new life.
