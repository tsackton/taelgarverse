# Hazkunde
:speaker:{ .middle } *(has-KOON-dee)*  

Hazkunde is one of the four [Tharzen Anzinakoa](<./tharzen-anzinakoa.md>) of the lizardfolk, usually associated with birth and new growth, and is typically seen as the divinity that guides souls to the [Material Plane](<../../../../cosmology/material-plane.md>) to be reborn. 

Among many lizardfolk communities, particularly those in [Greater Sembara](<../../../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/greater-sembara.md>), Hazkunde is associated with spring, the season of rising waters and the first greening of marsh and river.
