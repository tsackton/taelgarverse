# Ghesyn

Ghesyn, often titled the Goddess of the End and the Hopeless One, is a feared figure associated with the [Three Despairs](<../high-gods/three-despairs.md>) and the curse of undeath, rumored to have been spawned from the union of [G'Zab](<../high-gods/g-zab.md>) and [Jinnik](<../high-gods/jinnik.md>).
