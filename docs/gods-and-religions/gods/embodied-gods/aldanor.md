# Aldanor

Aldanor, the Living Tree, is one of the two deities of the [Elves](<../../../creatures/species/elves.md>), alongside [Elmerca](<elmerca.md>), the Wild Star. In elven lore the pair are called the Firstborn, [Embodied Gods](<embodied-gods.md>) through whom the [Divine Presence](<../high-gods/divine-presence.md>) gave rise to elves. Aldanor embodies life, growth, and endurance, the body and breath of the people, and creates the pull of the *mela* anchors elves to the material world, underlying the elven [cycle of generations](<../../../background/elven-cycle-of-generations.md>).
