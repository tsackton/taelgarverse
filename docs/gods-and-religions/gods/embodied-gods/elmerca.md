# Elmerca

Elmerca, the Wild Star, is one of the two deites of the [Elves](<../../../creatures/species/elves.md>), alongside [Aldanor](<./aldanor.md>), the Living Tree. In elven lore the pair are called the Firstborn, [Embodied Gods](<./embodied-gods.md>) through whom the [Divine Presence](<../high-gods/divine-presence.md>) gave rise to elves. Elmerca is the distant light of insight, passion, and change, the mind and vision of the people, associated with the *leya*, the time of wandering in the [Elven Cycle of Generations](<../../../background/elven-cycle-of-generations.md>). Elmerca gifts the magic of elves, and in elven stories is associated with moments of great foresight.
