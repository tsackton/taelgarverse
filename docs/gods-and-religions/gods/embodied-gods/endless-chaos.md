# The Endless Chaos

The Endless Chaos is a rumored [Embodied God](<./embodied-gods.md>) associated with the [Three Despairs](<../high-gods/three-despairs.md>) and the unmaking of ordered creation, said to spring from the union of [Umidzka](<../high-gods/umidzka.md>) and [Jinnik](<../high-gods/jinnik.md>). Though elven loremasters speak of a  being that delights in unbinding forms, dissolving borders between planes, and returning creation to swirling formlessness, no reliable accounts firmly establish whether such a god truly exists or whether the idea simply personifies the destructive upheavals wrought by the [Downfall Wars](<../../../events/ancient/downfall-wars.md>).
