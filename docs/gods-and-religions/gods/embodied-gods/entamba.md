# Entamba

Entamba, the Elder Mountain, is the [Embodied Divinity](<./embodied-gods.md>) who created the [Stoneborn](<../../../creatures/species/stoneborn.md>), and is venerated by the Stoneborn as the soul of the mountains and the Firstborn of their species.
