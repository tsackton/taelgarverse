# The Chasm Explorers
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-
   :octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } An adventuring party  
</div>


The party consists of:

[Eolo](<eolo.md>), a brash young kenku from the [Far North](<../../../gazetteer/faraway-places/far-north.md>). His village was destroyed by a white dragon when he was young, and he spent much of his childhood raised by Stoneborn in the [Sentinels](<../../../gazetteer/sentinel-range.md>). Recently he has decided to learn (or teach?) the kenku at the [Kemeko Monastery](<../../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/zimkova/kemeko-monastery.md>) how to fight. 

[Ekko](<ekko.md>), a kenku. He grew up in the [Kemeko Monastery](<../../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/zimkova/kemeko-monastery.md>), but as a young adult discovered a mystic connection to the Chasm. He saved one of the Vejo Vaikai from a fall, and came to live with them in their small community around the headwaters of the [Teft](<../../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/rivers/teft-watershed/teft.md>).

[Finnan Oakstride](<finnan-oakstride.md>), a halfling lore-gatherer, interested in ancient tales, wintering in the small village of [Dravnieki](<../../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/zimkova/dravnieki.md>)

[Justas Rhostrin](<justas-rhostrin.md>), an itinerant priest, tender of the [Kestavo](<../../../gods-and-religions/religions/northern-folk-religions/kestavo.md>), also wintering in the small village of [Dravnieki](<../../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/zimkova/dravnieki.md>).

[Jrain Fanlish](<jrain-fanlish.md>), a traveler, messanger, and sometimes member of the Mezhai.
