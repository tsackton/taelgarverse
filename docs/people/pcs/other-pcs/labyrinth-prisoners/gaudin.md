Gadin is an [elf](<../../../../creatures/species/elves.md>) warlock from beyond the [Sentinel Range](<../../../../gazetteer/sentinel-range.md>), born into the first elven generation after the [Great War](<../../../../events/1500s/great-war.md>). The dreams of his childhood were filled with tragedy and confusion, and he eventually left his community to wander and make his own way. He found his way to the [Western Green Sea](<../../../../gazetteer/western-green-sea/western-green-sea.md>), where he turned to smuggling to survive. While leaving [Tyrwingha](<../../../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/tyrwingha/tyrwingha.md>), he was caught and cried out for help; a mysterious fey patron answered, teleporting him to safety and leaving him with new warlock powers.

Gadin was captured after leaving the fishing village of [Redes](<../../../../gazetteer/western-green-sea/cymea/redes.md>) to meet a contact about smuggled wine bound for [Tyrwingha](<../../../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/tyrwingha/tyrwingha.md>). A false cart driver and goblin archers ambushed him on the road, and he was thrown into the same cart as a [halfling woman](<loria-underbough.md>) and a [lizardfolk man](<acescale.md>) before waking in [Perdoli Manor](<../../../../gazetteer/western-green-sea/cymea/perdoli-manor.md>) as one of the [Labyrinth Prisoners](<labyrinth-prisoners.md>).

After escaping the manor, Gadin left with old pirate maps and an eye toward the smuggling routes and hideouts they revealed.
