# The Labyrinth Prisoners

The Labyrinth Prisoners were the four player characters in [Labyrinths of the Lost](<../../../../campaigns/one-shots/labyrinths-of-the-lost/labyrinths-of-the-lost.md>), a short adventure about prisoners escaping an abandoned manor in [Western Cymea](<../../../../gazetteer/western-green-sea/realms/western-cymea.md>).
