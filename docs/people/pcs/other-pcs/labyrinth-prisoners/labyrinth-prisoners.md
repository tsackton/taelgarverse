# The Labyrinth Prisoners
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>
-
   :octicons-info-24:{ .lg .middle } An adventuring party  
</div>


The Labyrinth Prisoners were an ad hoc adventuring party formed by four captives of [Grimstone's](<../../../other-humans/grimstone.md>) prisoner-taking operation at [Perdoli Manor](<../../../../gazetteer/western-green-sea/cymea/perdoli-manor.md>) in [Western Cymea](<../../../../gazetteer/western-green-sea/realms/western-cymea.md>). They came from different roads and backgrounds, but all were taken by goblin raiders and woke in a crude prison cell in the manor's abandoned smithy on October 17th, 1748.

 After they killed [Grimstone](<../../../other-humans/grimstone.md>) and escaped [his lair](<../../../../gazetteer/western-green-sea/cymea/grimstone-s-lair.md>), they went their separate ways.

## Members

- [Acescale](<acescale.md>), a [lizardfolk](<../../../../creatures/species/lizardfolk.md>) alchemist and gambler from [Latazaro](<../../../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/latazaro.md>)
- [Gadin](<gaudin.md>), an [elf](<../../../../creatures/species/elves.md>) warlock and smuggler with fey patron
- [Loria Underbough](<loria-underbough.md>), a [halfling](<../../../../creatures/species/halflings.md>) wanderer and ranger from the [Enst](<../../../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/rivers/wistel-enst-watershed/enst.md>) river valley
- [Dilon](<dilion.md>), a [human](<../../../../creatures/species/humans.md>) artificer and battlesmith who traveled with [Pikeia](<pikeia.md>) and the [Steampup](<steampup.md>)
