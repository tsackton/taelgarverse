Pikeia is [Dilon's](<dilion.md>) dog companion. Pikeia was taken during the events at [Perdoli Manor](<../../../../gazetteer/western-green-sea/cymea/perdoli-manor.md>) and kept alive in [Grimstone's](<../../../other-humans/grimstone.md>) storeroom beneath the manor.

After Grimstone was killed, Dilon found Pikeia tied up in the east storeroom of [Grimstone's Lair](<../../../../gazetteer/western-green-sea/cymea/grimstone-s-lair.md>), and was reunited.
