# The Tollen Misfits

</div>


The Tollen Misfits are the player characters in [Lost in the Feywild](<../../../../campaigns/one-shots/lost-in-the-feywild/lost-in-the-feywild.md>), a found-family band of adventurers from around [Tollen](<../../../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/tollen/tollen.md>).

The group includes:

- [Kaito Min](<kaito-min.md>), an elven performer and opportunist
- [Edric](<edric.md>), a generous wanderer devoted to [The Night Queen](<../../../../gods-and-religions/gods/incorporeal-gods/mos-numena-pantheon/the-night-queen.md>)
- [Tarek](<tarek.md>), a quick-handed traveler and salvager
- [Ayveen](<ayveen.md>), a hopeful Tollish adventurer
- [Txarro](<txarro.md>), a druid whose memories include the wild dangers around Tollen
