Colette Du Bois is a squire of in [Elowen Duval](<elowen-duval.md>)'s household guard, apprenticed to Sir [Gareth Howell](<gareth-howell.md>). She is often restless, and eager to prove herself, which can get her in trouble at times, as when she scouted a goblin lair in the [Highmoor](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/highmoor.md>) ahead of official orders and was captured. 

Though her captivity has slightly tempered her recklessness, she remains impatient and hungry for opportunities for real adventure. She is a reliable scout and source of local knowledge. 

## Events
- Apr 29, 1715 DR: Captured by goblin bandits while scouting in the [Highmoor](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/highmoor.md>)
- May 08, 1715 DR: Rescued from the goblin queen’s cells by the [Addermarch Mercenaries](<../pcs/addermarch/addermarch-mercenaries.md>) during their assault on the [Highmoor](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/highmoor.md>) lair.
