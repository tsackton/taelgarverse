Cadan was a ranger of western Addermarch who died at [Serethwyn Tor](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/serethwyn-tor.md>) during the conflicts that followed the [Dominion of Avatus](<../../history/historical-realms/dominion-of-avatus.md>)’s occupation of the region. His name survives carved into the stone memorial that [Arvanko](<../other-nonhumans/arvanko.md>) is shaping on his hilltop east of [Roscombe](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/roscombe.md>).
