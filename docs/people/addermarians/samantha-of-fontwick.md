Samantha is a village from [Fontwick](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/fontwick.md>), a small village west of [Roscombe](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/roscombe.md>). She operates a small dairy alongside her husband [Godfrey of Fontwick](<godfrey-of-fontwick.md>). She is practical, hospitable, and resilient. 

## Events
- May 11, 1715 DR: Encountered the [Addermarch Mercenaries](<../pcs/addermarch/addermarch-mercenaries.md>) while fleeing an ankheg attack on her cattle, and led them back to the farm.
- May 12, 1715 DR: Stood watch during [the night battle](<../../campaigns/addermarch-campaign/addermarch-session-06.md>) and gratefully received funds from [Yvan Greenrabbit](<../pcs/addermarch/yvan-greenrabbit.md>) to restock her herd.
