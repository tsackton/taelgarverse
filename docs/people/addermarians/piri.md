Piri served as a soldier at [Torvaine Watch](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/torvaine-watch.md>), remembered for his cheerful songs and woodcarving talents that brightened long winter postings. Marshal [Hildo Brask](<./hildo-brask.md>)’s unfinished condolence letter records his accidental death shortly before the watch fell to the curse of the [Serpentine Dagger](<../../things/magic-items/serpentine-dagger.md>).

## Events
