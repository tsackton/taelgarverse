# Gazankoa

The entity called Gazankoa, frequently mentioned in the lore of [Mawar](<../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/mawar-confederacy.md>), [Apporia](<../../gazetteer/greater-chardon/chardonian-empire/apporia/apporia.md>), and the [Chardonian Coastlands](<../../gazetteer/greater-chardon/chardonian-empire/coastlands.md>), is a powerful elemental being, rumored to gift magic to those desperate, foolish, or mad enough to seek it. Though sources differ on Gazankoa's exact identity, all agree on a deep connection to the [Elemental Plane of Water](<../../cosmology/energy-realms/elemental-plane-of-water.md>) and a longstanding hatred of the [Sentient Ocean](<sentient-ocean.md>).
