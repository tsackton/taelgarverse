Harbek Ferrystone is a dwarven mathematical cosmologist, based in Rinburg in the Barony of Aveil. He is most famous for his scholarly work on planar tides, particularly [Planar Tides in Relation to the Dangers of the Plaguelands](<../../things/books/planar-tides-in-relation-to-the-dangers-of-the-plaguelands.md>)(DR 1703), proposing that cyclical “planar tides” modulate [Limbo](<../../cosmology/spiritual-realms/limbo.md>)’s influence upon the world and predict dangerous outbursts. His model was later discredited after the [War of the Ashen Horde](<../../events/1700s/war-of-the-ashen-horde.md>) broke out in DR 1720, contrary to his predictionsthat planar influences should be very weak at that time, but his work remains a notable attempt to quantify Plaguelands phenomena.
