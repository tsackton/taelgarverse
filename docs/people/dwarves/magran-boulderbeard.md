Killed [Inakara](<../other-nonhumans/inakara.md>) and the other denizens of the [dwarven outpost near Raven's Hold](<../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/dunmari-basin/dwarven-outpost-raven-s-hold.md>) in late July, 1748.
