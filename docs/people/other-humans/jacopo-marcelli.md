Jacopo Marcelli was a pirate captain, active in [Western Cymea](<../../gazetteer/western-green-sea/realms/western-cymea.md>) in the early DR 1700s. In life, Jacopo loved [Petrona](<petrona.md>), who urged him to return safely and abandon the pirate life. He died in a naval raid on a pirate hideout under the [Perdoli Manor](<../../gazetteer/western-green-sea/cymea/perdoli-manor.md>), without learning whether she had escaped to safety, and his ghost remained bound to the bedroom. 

When [Grimstone](<grimstone.md>) occupied [Perdoli Manor](<../../gazetteer/western-green-sea/cymea/perdoli-manor.md>), Jacopo's ghost served as a useful guard, kept under Grimstone's control by false promises. On October 18th, 1748, [Gaudin](<../pcs/other-pcs/labyrinth-prisoners/gaudin.md>) and the [Labyrinth Prisoners](<../pcs/other-pcs/labyrinth-prisoners/labyrinth-prisoners.md>) used magic to take Petrona's appearance, convincing Jacopo to that Grimstone had lied to him. The sight of his lost love softened Jacopo: he refused to keep guarding the way below, and faded away.
