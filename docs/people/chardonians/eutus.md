Eutus is a wizard affiliated with the [Great Library](<../../gazetteer/greater-chardon/chardonian-empire/chardon/great-library.md>) in [Chardon](<../../gazetteer/greater-chardon/chardonian-empire/chardon/chardon.md>). While searching for ancient ruins, he encountered and befriended [Aglath](<../pcs/silver-tempests/aglath.md>), eventually bringing him back to Chardon. Years later, Eutus recruited Aglath for the Library's adventuring work and sent him north to [Voltara](<../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/northern-provinces/voltara/voltara.md>).
