Cassia is a paladin and adventurer based in [Chardon](<../../gazetteer/greater-chardon/chardonian-empire/chardon/chardon.md>). In March DR 1748, she explored the lower [Goldpeak Mines](<../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/fiamatara-mountains/goldpeak-mines.md>) with [Alton](<../pcs/silver-tempests/alton.md>) and [Brottor](<../dwarves/brottor.md>), where the beholder Vilaxes had taken control of the depths beneath [Goldpeak Mountain](<../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/fiamatara-mountains/goldpeak-mountain.md>).

After Brottor fell under Vilaxes's influence, Cassia and Alton joined the [Silver Tempests](<../pcs/silver-tempests/silver-tempests.md>) in the fight against the beholder. Cassia survived the expedition after [Brelith](<../pcs/silver-tempests/brelith.md>) raised her from death at the end of the battle.
