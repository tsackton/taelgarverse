![Iveila the Red](/taelgarverse/assets/iveila-the-red.webp){align="right"; width="400"}Iveila the Red, usually just called "Red" by friends, acquaintances, and enemies alike, is a muscular woman with frizzy flame-red hair. She is a crane operator in the [Ragwater Basin](<../../gazetteer/greater-chardon/chardonian-empire/chardon/ragwater-basin.md>), working to unload raw chalyte ore for processing. She is also an informal labor organizer and became prominent as a member of the [Eightfold Flame](<../../groups/chardonian-organizations/eightfold-flame.md>) during the Chardon Chalyte Riots in DR 1749.
