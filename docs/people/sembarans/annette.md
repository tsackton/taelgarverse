One of [Marguerite's](<marguerite-deschamps.md>) senior assistant clerks. Since [Marguerite's](<marguerite-deschamps.md>) death she has been serving as the chief clerk of the [barony](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/sembara/barony-of-aveil/barony-of-aveil.md>) although it is not clear if she will remain in this position. She is new to the post and easily flustered by unusual behavior, although she is also apologetic and conscientious.
