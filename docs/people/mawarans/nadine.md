# Nadine

    A [Mawaran](<../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/mawar-confederacy.md>) [human](<../../creatures/species/humans.md>) (she/her)  
    Died DR 1741  
    { .bio }

    Lived in the [eastern coast of the Mawakel Peninsula](<../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/mawakel-peninsula.md>)

Nadine was the wife of [Azar the Lost](<azar-the-lost.md>). She and their two teenage sons drowned in a summer storm in DR 1741, while traveling to market from a small village on the eastern coast of the [Mawakel Peninsula](<../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/mawakel-peninsula.md>).

Her death drove Azar into grief, [gatza](<../../things/materials/gatza.md>) addiction, and eventually the influence of [Gazankoa](<../extraplanar-powers/gazankoa.md>). Azar believed that the voice he heard through gatza dreams was Nadine, and his [attempts to restore her](<../../campaigns/mawar-adventures/notes/azar-s-tale.md>) led to [murders](<../../campaigns/mawar-adventures/episodes/mawar-adventures-episode-02.md>) in [Hamri](<../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/hamri.md>).
