Nimessa is a marid of the [Elemental Plane of Water](<../../cosmology/energy-realms/elemental-plane-of-water.md>). In DR 1747, she was summoned and controlled by [Azar the Lost](<../mawarans/azar-the-lost.md>) using an old magic ring of [Airion](<../other-humans/airion.md>)'s; she was forced to steal a fragment of living water from the [Sentient Ocean](<../extraplanar-powers/sentient-ocean.md>) for Azar. After fulfilling Azar's command, she vanished, presumably returning to her native plane.
