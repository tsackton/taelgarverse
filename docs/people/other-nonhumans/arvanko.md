:octicons-location-24:{ .lg .middle } Met by the [Addermarch Mercenaries](<../pcs/addermarch/addermarch-mercenaries.md>) on May 23th, 1715 at [Arvanko's Homestead](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/arvanko-s-homestead.md>), in [Carlinshire](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/carlinshire.md>), [Addermarch](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/addermarch.md>)  


Arvanko is a tall gray-black mottled Stoneborn, reclusive, prickly, and slow-to-trust, who lives alone on [a hilltop](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/arvanko-s-homestead.md>) east of [Roscombe](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/roscombe.md>), near the eaves of the [Torvaine Forest](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/torvaine-forest.md>), about a day’s walk from town. Now in his later years, he has turned from hunting threats to carving a monument to the fallen: the unfinished, but massive, sculpture forms a low circular wall nearly 200 feet in diameter, inscribed with the names of Rangers who died protecting Addermarch over the centuries.
