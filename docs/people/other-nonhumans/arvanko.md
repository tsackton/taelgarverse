# Arvanko
<div class="grid cards ext-narrow-margin ext-one-column" markdown>

    Member of [The Rangers](<../../groups/the-rangers.md>)  
    { .bio }

    Based in [Carlinshire](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/carlinshire.md>), [Addermarch](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/addermarch.md>)
</div>



Arvanko is a reclusive, prickly, and slow-to-trust Stoneborn ranger who lives alone on a hilltop east of [Roscombe](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/roscombe.md>), near the eaves of the [Torvaine Forest](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/torvaine-forest.md>), about a day’s walk from town. Now in his later years, he has turned from hunting threats to carving a monument to the fallen: the unfinished, but massive, sculpture forms a a low circular wall nearly 200 feet in diameter, inscribed with the names of Rangers who died protecting Addermarch over the centuries.
