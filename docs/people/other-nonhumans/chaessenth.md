Chaessenth is a young dragon turtle, who was captured very young (probably taken as an egg). He was held in a hidden lair in [Tzingola](<../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/tzingola.md>), where a fire mage controlled him through a burning black spear and used him as the apparent monster behind attacks and [gatza](<../../things/materials/gatza.md>) demands near [Eskorola](<../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/mawar-confederacy/eskorola.md>).

After his release, Chaessenth left for the deep western ocean. He knows songs of the [Wanui Teora](<../extraplanar-powers/sentient-ocean.md>), evoking the stars below and the dazzling lights of the deep sea. Chaessenth formed a particular bond with [Nerissa](<../pcs/mawar/nerissa.md>), who calmed and healed him after the spear was removed and began to pick up the melody of his ocean song.
