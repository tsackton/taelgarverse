# The Blood Plague

The Blood Plague was a deadly outbreak of a magical curse that spread north from the [Plaguelands](<../../gazetteer/upper-istaros/plaguelands.md>), through [Addermarch](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/addermarch/addermarch.md>) and southern [Sembara](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/sembara/sembara.md>). It was part of a series of disasters and evils in the immediate aftermath of the [Great War](<./great-war.md>) that brought hardship and famine during the so-called [Blood Years](<./blood-years.md>).
