# The Second Hobgoblin War (Sembara)



The second major war between [Sembara](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/sembara/sembara.md>) and the [Shattered Ice Clan](<../../groups/hobgoblin-clans/shattered-ice-clan.md>), fought largely in the borderlands south of the [Enst](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/rivers/wistel-enst-watershed/enst.md>), and in the [Duchy of Maseau](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/duchy-of-maseau/duchy-of-maseau.md>). The war ends with Sembara conceding the lands south of the [Enst](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/rivers/wistel-enst-watershed/enst.md>) to the hobgoblins.
