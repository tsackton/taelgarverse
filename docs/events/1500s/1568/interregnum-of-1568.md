# The Interregnum of 1568



The Interregnum of 1568 was a succession crisis in [Sembara](<../../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/sembara/sembara.md>) following the death of [Queen Blanche I](<../../../people/historical-figures/sembaran-royalty/blanche-i.md>). Blanche’s sisters—the twins [Anne](<../../../people/historical-figures/sembaran-royalty/anne.md>) and [Elaine I](<../../../people/historical-figures/sembaran-royalty/elaine-i.md>)—disputed the succession, and their rivalry split the court. In the end, [Wisym I](<../../../people/historical-figures/sembaran-royalty/wisym-i.md>), a cousin, was elevated as a compromise king, in hopes of ending the dispute.
