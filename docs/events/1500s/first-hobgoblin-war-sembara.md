# The First Hobgoblin War (Sembara)



The first of three major wars between [Sembara](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/sembara/sembara.md>) and allies and the [Shattered Ice Clan](<../../groups/hobgoblin-clans/shattered-ice-clan.md>). This war began when the [Shattered Ice Clan](<../../groups/hobgoblin-clans/shattered-ice-clan.md>) crossed the [Sentinels](<../../gazetteer/sentinel-range.md>) in force, attacking across the southwestern frontier of [Sembara](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/sembara/sembara.md>). It ended with in a stalemate: the [Shattered Ice Clan](<../../groups/hobgoblin-clans/shattered-ice-clan.md>) advance was halted, but hobgoblins remained established in parts of the [Western Marches](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/sembara/western-marches/western-marches.md>) and in the western edges of [Maseau](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/duchy-of-maseau/duchy-of-maseau.md>).
