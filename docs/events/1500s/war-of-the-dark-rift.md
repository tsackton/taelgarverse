# The War of the Dark Rift

The War of the Dark Rift was a devastating war fought on [Apporia](<../../gazetteer/greater-chardon/chardonian-empire/apporia/apporia.md>). During a period of 13 years, the [Umbral Covenant](<../../groups/umbral-covenant.md>) largely conquered northern [Apporia](<../../gazetteer/greater-chardon/chardonian-empire/apporia/apporia.md>), save for the coastal plain of [Raziolo](<../../gazetteer/greater-chardon/chardonian-empire/apporia/raziolo.md>) and some fortified parts of [Cedrano](<../../gazetteer/greater-chardon/chardonian-empire/apporia/cedrano.md>). The war ended when the Chardonian navy destroyed the Shadow Armada of the [Umbral Covenant](<../../groups/umbral-covenant.md>) in the [Gulf of Chardon](<../../gazetteer/greater-chardon/gulf-of-chardon.md>). 

Chardonian writers often treat the war as a turning point: the moment [Chardon](<../../gazetteer/greater-chardon/chardonian-empire/chardon/chardon.md>) became an imperial naval power and began to believe it was strong, and the beginnings of Chardonian expansion into [Apporia](<../../gazetteer/greater-chardon/chardonian-empire/apporia/apporia.md>).
