# The Conclave War

The Conclave War was one of the defining conflicts of the [Blood Years](<blood-years.md>) in [Greater Chardon](<../../gazetteer/greater-chardon/greater-chardon.md>). It was fought between the armies of Chardon and allies, including a large host of [Elves](<../../creatures/species/elves.md>) and [Deno'qai](<../../groups/cultures/deno-qai-tribes/deno-qai.md>), and a large part of the remnants of Cha'mutte's northern armies, led by a conclave of dragons. The Conclave war was very destructive, particularly in [Ainumarya](<../../gazetteer/central-highlands/ainumarya.md>), and the fate of the large bulk of the elves who once called [Ainumarya](<../../gazetteer/central-highlands/ainumarya.md>) home is still unknown. 

The war’s key turning point was the [Battle of Metium](<battle-of-metium.md>), fought only a few days’ ride upriver from Chardon, where the dragon host was checked and the conclave destroyed.
