# The Battle of Urlich Pass

The Battle of Urlich Pass was the climactic battle of the [Great War](<../great-war.md>) in DR 1545. It was fought in the central [Sentinel Range](<../../../gazetteer/sentinel-range.md>) at [Urlich Pass](<../../../gazetteer/central-highlands/urlich-pass.md>), where the armies of [Sembara](<../../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/sembara/sembara.md>) and [Ainumarya](<../../../gazetteer/central-highlands/ainumarya.md>), and many allies, met the mostly [hobgoblin](<../../../creatures/species/hobgoblins.md>) armies of [Cha'mutte](<../../../people/extraplanar-powers/cha-mutte.md>).

The smaller combat between [Cha'mutte](<../../../people/extraplanar-powers/cha-mutte.md>) himself and the [Heroes of the Great War](<../../../people/pcs/great-war/heroes-of-the-great-war.md>) occurred nearby, within sight of the massed armies; [Cha'mutte](<../../../people/extraplanar-powers/cha-mutte.md>)'s death marked the true end of the [Great War](<../great-war.md>).
