# The Great War



The Great War is the name given to the war against  [Cha'mutte](<../../people/extraplanar-powers/cha-mutte.md>) that united many human and non-human realms to fight against the vast armies of hobgoblins, monsters, and other allies gathered by [Cha'mutte](<../../people/extraplanar-powers/cha-mutte.md>). It was fought on many fronts, with major battles involving [Dunmar](<../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/realms/dunmar/dunmar.md>), the [Istabor Alliance](<../../history/historical-realms/istabor-alliance.md>), [Sembara](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/sembara/sembara.md>), and [Chardon](<../../gazetteer/greater-chardon/chardonian-empire/chardon/chardon.md>), among others. The war ended in DR 1545 with Cha'mutte’s defeat by the [Heroes of the Great War](<../../people/pcs/great-war/heroes-of-the-great-war.md>) at the [Battle of Urlich Pass](<1545/battle-of-urlich-pass.md>).

The Great War is remembered as an existential war: a struggle not merely against invasion, but against an implacable enemy who sought to destroy the very gods themselves and overthrow the order of the world.

## Legacy

The Great War still leaves a lasting legacy on the world today. The battles of the Great War and its aftermath reshaped the western coast, seeing Chardon turn from a city of mages and merchants to an imperial capital, while many of the other realms in this part of the world, including the great elven realm of [Ainumarya](<../../gazetteer/central-highlands/ainumarya.md>), were destroyed. In Dunmar, the landscape itself was radically altered, changing the climate. In the Green Sea, the Plaguelands grew where [Cha'mutte's Plague](<./cha-mutte-s-plague.md>) had lain heaviest, and the passes through the [Sentinels](<../../gazetteer/sentinel-range.md>) at [Urlich Pass](<../../gazetteer/central-highlands/urlich-pass.md>) were destroyed in a cataclysm upon Cha'mutte's death.
