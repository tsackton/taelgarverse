# The Attempted Geas of Elaine I

The Attempted Geas of Elaine I is the name later chroniclers give to the magical coercion attempted against [Elaine I](<../../../people/historical-figures/sembaran-royalty/elaine-i.md>) during the succession crisis that followed the death of [Wisym I](<../../../people/historical-figures/sembaran-royalty/wisym-i.md>). The attempt failed, and Elaine was ultimately crowned queen; in the aftermath, [Anne](<../../../people/historical-figures/sembaran-royalty/anne.md>) was condemned for treason and executed.
