# The Battle of Voltara

The Battle of Voltara (also called the Battle of Lightning’s Wrath) was the climactic battle of [Grumella's War](<../../grumella-s-war.md>). The defenders of [Voltara](<../../../../gazetteer/northwest-coast/northern-provinces/voltara/voltara.md>)—aided by the adventuring company known as the [Silver Tempests](<../../../../people/pcs/silver-tempests/silver-tempests.md>) and their allies—broke [Grumella](<../../../../people/orcs/grumella.md>)’s siege and [slew the orc warlord](<../../../../campaigns/great-library-campaign/session-notes/great-library-session-notes-arc-2.md>).

The battle is remembered in local chronicles for the storm and lightning that accompanied the fighting, and for the sudden collapse of the horde’s cohesion after Grumella’s death.
