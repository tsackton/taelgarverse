# The Battle of Heartroot Vale

The Battle of Heartroot Vale was a climatic victory by the forces of the [Refounded Alliance of Aurbez](<../../gazetteer/upper-istaros/refounded-alliance-of-aurbez/refounded-alliance-of-aurbez.md>) and the [Dunmar Fellowship](<../../people/pcs/dunmar-fellowship/dunmar-fellowship.md>) against the Iron Fang hobgoblins and the [Empress of Chaos](<../../people/other-nonhumans/empress-of-chaos.md>). 

The [Rangers](<../../groups/rangers.md>) and the [Aurbeze](<../../gazetteer/upper-istaros/refounded-alliance-of-aurbez/refounded-alliance-of-aurbez.md>) and [Lizardfolk](<../../creatures/species/lizardfolk.md>) defenders, aided by a vast magical illusion and shapeshifting magic provided by [Seeker](<../../people/pcs/dunmar-fellowship/seeker.md>) and his simulacrum, Seeker 5, [lured the hobgoblin host into an ambush](<../../campaigns/dunmari-frontier-campaign/session-notes/session-132-dufr.md>). With [Vindristjarna](<../../things/ships/vindristjarna.md>) and an illusory dragon providing air support, the Iron Fang hobgoblins were bogged down in battle. But with the defenders bogged down as well, the [Empress of Chaos](<../../people/other-nonhumans/empress-of-chaos.md>) sped past aiming for the Heartroot itself, using decoy troops on flying chaos-metal hawks to distract [Vindristjarna](<../../things/ships/vindristjarna.md>) and the [Dunmar Fellowship](<../../people/pcs/dunmar-fellowship/dunmar-fellowship.md>). 

Noticing the deception, the [Dunmar Fellowship](<../../people/pcs/dunmar-fellowship/dunmar-fellowship.md>) raced to intercept the [Empress of Chaos](<../../people/other-nonhumans/empress-of-chaos.md>), using _Teleport_ to arrive at the vale in time. With a _Force Cage_ protecting the Heartroot itself, the [Fellowship](<../../people/pcs/dunmar-fellowship/dunmar-fellowship.md>) attacked. The battle seemed to be won when the party defeated the [Empress](<../../people/other-nonhumans/empress-of-chaos.md>), but her Chaos Metal armor erupted into a cataclysm of elemental chaos, unleashing a devastating attack that disrupted the _Force Cage_ and exposed the vulnerable Heartroot. To stop it, [Kenzo](<../../people/pcs/dunmar-fellowship/kenzo.md>) transformed into a silver dragon, destroying the elemental cataclysm, saving the Heartroot, and then turning on the last of the iron Fang survivors, leading a devastating counterattack that shattered the hobgoblin lines.
