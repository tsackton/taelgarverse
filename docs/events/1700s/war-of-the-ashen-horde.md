# The War of the Ashen Horde

In DR 1720, a surge of [Plaguelands](<../../gazetteer/upper-istaros/plaguelands.md>) horrors—ash elementals, ash‑spawn, and corrupted things from the Istaros valley—emerged with the summer ash storms. Striking swiftly across the [Aursen](<../../gazetteer/major-rivers/istaros-watershed/aursen.md>) and towards the [Aurbez Plateau](<../../gazetteer/upper-istaros/aurbez-plateau.md>), they were eventually defeated by a coalition of [Rangers](<../../groups/rangers.md>) and Aurbeze militias. This was a formative event that led to the creation of the [Refounded Alliance of Aurbez](<../../gazetteer/upper-istaros/refounded-alliance-of-aurbez/refounded-alliance-of-aurbez.md>).
