# The Summer Gnoll Raids of 1748



In the spring and summer of DR 1748, gnoll warbands swept across [Eastern Dunmar](<../../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/realms/dunmar/eastern-dunmar/eastern-dunmar.md>), attacking villages and pastoralist camps, and driving waves of refugees westward towards [Tokra](<../../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/realms/dunmar/central-dunmar/tokra/tokra.md>). The raids struck hardest around [Karawa](<../../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/realms/dunmar/eastern-dunmar/karawa.md>) and [Bas Udda](<../../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/realms/dunmar/eastern-dunmar/bas-udda.md>), both of which suffered heavy damage, and took advantage of the disruption in [Shakun's Protection](<../../../gods-and-religions/religions/five-siblings/shakun-s-protection.md>) caused by the meddling of [Agata Dustmother](<../../../people/fey/agata.md>).
