# The Battle of Shrevandurg

A poorly documented battle on the [Shrev](<../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/rivers/shrev.md>) frontier in [Western Dunmar](<../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/realms/dunmar/western-dunmar/western-dunmar.md>), studied by [Govir](<../../people/dunmari/govir.md>) as an example of divine power aiding the [Dunmari](<../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/realms/dunmar/dunmar.md>) in battle.
