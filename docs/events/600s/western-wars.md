# The Western Wars

The Drankorian Western Wars were a series of campaigns by the [Drankorian Empire](<../../history/historical-realms/drankorian-empire.md>) between DR 597 and DR 634, fought to subjugate the peoples of the [Yeraad Watershed](<../../gazetteer/major-rivers/yeraad-watershed/yeraad-watershed.md>), the western [Coastlands](<../../gazetteer/greater-chardon/chardonian-empire/coastlands.md>), and the [Chasa River Valley](<../../gazetteer/greater-chardon/chardonian-empire/chasa-river-valley/chasa-river-valley.md>). The end of these wars established [Lake Valandros](<../../gazetteer/greater-chardon/lake-valandros.md>) as the furthest extent of [Drankorian](<../../history/historical-realms/drankorian-empire.md>) control in the west.
