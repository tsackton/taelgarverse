# The War of the Severed Dreams

A war fought against Yeshara and the [Shemra Azem](<../../groups/shemra-azem.md>), called the [Umbral Covenant](<../../groups/umbral-covenant.md>), who supposedly fought against sleep itself and killed their enemies by severing their ability to dream.
