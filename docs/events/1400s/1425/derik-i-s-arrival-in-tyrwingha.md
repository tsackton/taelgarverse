# Derik I's Arrival in Tyrwingha

In DR 1425, during the wars against the [Dominion of Avatus](<../../../history/historical-realms/dominion-of-avatus.md>), Derik I, then a little-known noble of [Telham](<../../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/sembara/northlands/telham.md>), arrived in [Tyrwingha](<../../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/tyrwingha/tyrwingha.md>). Most tales of the day describe the morning fog parting in golden light at his feet, and [Ethlenn](<../../../people/extraplanar-powers/archfey/archfey-ethlenn.md>) herself appearing to bless him before the people. 

This marked the beginning of Derik I's rise as a royal figure, eventually leading to the foundation of modern Sembara at the [Treaty of Wisford](<../treaty-of-wisford.md>) and the personal union of thrones of [Sembara](<../../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/sembara/sembara.md>) and [Tyrwingha](<../../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/tyrwingha/tyrwingha.md>) under the [House of Sewick](<../../../groups/sembaran-noble-houses/house-of-sewick.md>).
