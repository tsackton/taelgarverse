# The Treaty of Wisford

The Treaty of Wisford is a treaty signed by [Derik I](<../../people/historical-figures/sembaran-royalty/derik-i.md>) that established the [House of Sewick](<../../groups/sembaran-noble-houses/house-of-sewick.md>) and modern [Sembara](<../../gazetteer/greater-sembara/sembara/sembara.md>).
