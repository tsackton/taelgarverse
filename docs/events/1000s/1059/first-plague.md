# The First Plague

The First Plague is the catastrophic wave of magical destruction that spread from [Drankor](<../../../gazetteer/drankorian-hinterland/drankor/drankor.md>), striking the heartland of the [Drankorian Empire](<../../../history/historical-realms/drankorian-empire.md>) in DR 1059. The curse, caused by [Cha'mutte](<../../../people/extraplanar-powers/cha-mutte.md>), killed nearly the entire population of [Drankor](<../../../gazetteer/drankorian-hinterland/drankor/drankor.md>), spread death and famine through the hinterlands of the city, contaminated much of the [Kharja](<../../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/kharja.md>), [Hara](<../../../gazetteer/greater-dunmar/rivers/hara-watershed/hara.md>), and lower [Istaros](<../../../gazetteer/major-rivers/istaros-watershed/istaros.md>) river basins.
