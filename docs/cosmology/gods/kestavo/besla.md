### Besla
*she/her*

A *[kestavo](<../../religions/kestavo.md>)* said to have come to the land in the ancient days from the [Green Sea](<../../../gazetteer/green-sea.md>), and each spring is said to ask [Ako](<../high-gods/ako.md>) to bring the rains.
