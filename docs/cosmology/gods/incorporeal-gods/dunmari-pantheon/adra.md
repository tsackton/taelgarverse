# Adra
:speaker:{ .middle } *(AAD-rah)*  

A demigod associated with the [the Five Siblings](<../../../religions/five-siblings/five-siblings.md>) of the Dunmari religion, and one of the [Incorporeal Gods](<../incorporeal-gods.md>) of humanity. 

Adra is the god of secrets, the quiet night, and darkness, a lovable trickster and patron of outcasts and misfits and lovers. He is the child of [Chidya](<./chidya.md>) and a mortal, although who the mortal is no one can seemingly agree on.Â
